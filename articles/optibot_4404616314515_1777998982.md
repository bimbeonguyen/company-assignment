[L1] # How to Change Account Owners

[L2] 

[L3] Account Owner is a special role. Only Account Owners can request deletion of accounts.  

[L4] There's only 1 Owner per account.

[L5] 

[L6] You can change owner to another user/admin in OptiSigns by:

[L7] 

[L8] * Go to the [Account Member](https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/account-members) page:

[L9] * Click the **Change Role** icon on the user or admin that you want to change to Owner.

[L10] 

[L11] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404609090451)

[L12] 

[L13] * Modify the Change role to the "**Owner**" role, and update it.

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404609100051)

[L16] 

[L17] * It will pop out a warning message. If you confirm it, please type "**CONTINUE**" and click OK to continue.

[L18] 

[L19] ### **mceclip2.png**

[L20] 

[L21] **Note**:

[L22] 

[L23] You can change back by asking the new Owner to make you Owner again.

[L24] 

[L25] ## How to Transfer Account Ownership if Owner Has Left the Organization

[L26] 

[L27] In some cases, the person who owns the account may leave the organization they are managing OptiSigns for, suddenly or otherwise. In this case, it is necessary for someone else to assume account ownership.

[L28] 

[L29] To do this, an authorized representative will need to fill out [this form](https://app-na1.hubspotdocuments.com/documents/20629229/view/1117090375?accessId=0b4807) and submit it to [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns).

[L30] 

[L31] ### **That's all!**

[L32] 

[L33] You have changed the owner to another account successfully.
