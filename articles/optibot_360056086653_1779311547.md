[L1] # How to Change Time Zone on Your Fire Stick device

[L2] 

[L3] Your schedule doesn't start or end at the same time as your schedule. The issue may be your device's time zone setting. You can check your device's setting.

[L4] 

[L5] In this guide, we will walk you through end to end process to change the time zone on your Amazon Fire Stick device.

[L6] 

[L7] Let's begin,

[L8] 

[L9] ### How to Change Time Zone on your Fire Stick device

[L10] 

[L11] To change the time zone on your Fire Stick, go to Settings > Preferences > Time Zone. Then select your time zone location. You are all set.

[L12] 

[L13] 1. Press the **Home button**, and navigate to **Settings** menu

[L14] 

[L15] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114141)

[L16] 

[L17] 2. Select the **Preferences** menu

[L18] 

[L19] ![Fire_Stick-31_Preferences.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114221)

[L20] 

[L21] 3. Click on the arrow down and go to **Time Zone**

[L22] 

[L23] ![Fire_Stick-32_Time_Zone.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114381)

[L24] 

[L25] 4. Then click **Time Zone**

[L26] 

[L27] ![Fire_Stick-33_Time_Zone.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813082)

[L28] 

[L29] 5. Select your region and that will set your time automatically

[L30] 

[L31] ![Fire_Stick-34_Time_Zone.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114401)

[L32] 

[L33] Changing the time zone on your Fire Stick can let your screen start and end with the correct schedule. 

[L34] 

[L35] You're ready to go.
