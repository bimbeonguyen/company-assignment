[L1] # How to use YouTube Playlist with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] Putting YouTube playlist on your digital signs screens is easy with OptiSigns.  

[L4] You just need to copy the YouTube Playlist Link and paste in the YouTube Playlist app in OptiSigns.

[L5] 

[L6] This article will guide you through how to create and use a YouTube Playlist App.

[L7] 

[L8] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L9] 

[L10] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L11] 

[L12] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L13] 

[L14] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L15] 

[L16] ![Youtube_playlist-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001039562)

[L17] 

[L18] Click YouTube Playlist app:

[L19] 

[L20] ![Youtube_playlist-3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001039582)

[L21] 

[L22] Enter your YouTube Playlist information:

[L23] 

[L24] ![Youtube_playlist-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001119001)

[L25] 

[L26] * Name: Give your YouTube Playlist a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only. The app will try to get the video's title and use as a name, but you can change it to something else.

[L27] * URL: Paste the link to your YouTube playlist here.

[L28] * Caption: If the YouTube video has closed caption, the app will show it. Note: closed caption must be created by the YouTube video author, auto generated caption may not work.

[L29] 

[L30] Click Save.

[L31] 

[L32] After Saving, you can Preview videos to verify.

[L33] 

[L34] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L35] 

[L36] You have added a YouTube playlist video.

[L37] 

[L38] You can assign the newly created YouTube playlist asset to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L39] 

[L40] You can put the YouTube playlist to a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L41]
