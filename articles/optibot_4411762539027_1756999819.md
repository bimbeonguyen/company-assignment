[L1] # Multilingual and multi-currency

[L2] 

[L3] #### **Multi-currency:**

[L4] 

[L5] OptiSigns support payment in USD, CAD, GBP, EUR, and AUD  

[L6] Just select your preferred payment currency on checkout.
