[L1] # How to create and use QR Code, Call To Action app

[L2] 

[L3] QR Codes is a great way to connect with your customers. By displaying a Call To Action QR Code as part of your digital signs, customer can scan it and┬Ātake variety number of actions such as open a website, send a SMS Text, follow your Instagram, etc. that you can customize.

[L4] 

[L5] All smartphone (Android and iPhone) have QR Code scanning built-in with their default camera app. Users just need to open the camera app, point toward the QR code and actions will be prompted.

[L6] 

[L7] ![Image result for iphone qr code scanner](https://cdn.macrumors.com/article-new/2017/06/qr-code-in-ios-11-camera.jpg)

[L8] 

[L9] You can create QR code for variety type of actions, here are some good examples to go with Digital Signs:

[L10] 

[L11] * Open your website URL

[L12] * Call a phone number

[L13] * Send an SMS Text message, you can even pre-fill some information

[L14] * Take user to App Store to download an app (it will auto detect Android or iPhone and take user to the appropriate places)

[L15] * Take user to follow your Facebook, Instagram, or other social media pages

[L16] * Write a reviews on Google Reviews

[L17] * and more ...

[L18] 

[L19] ┬Ā

[L20] 

[L21] To create QR Code and put on your signs with OptiSigns.

[L22] 

[L23] ## **To create QR Code and put on your signs with OptiSigns:**

[L24] 

[L25] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click┬Ā[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L26] 

[L27] Then log on to our portal:┬Ā<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L28] 

[L29] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add Instagram on your Account.

[L30] 

[L31] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26499896421395)

[L32] 

[L33] Click QR Code app:

[L34] 

[L35] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360050795914)

[L36] ┬Ā

[L37] Enter information for your QR Code.

[L38] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360050795954)

[L39] ┬Ā

[L40] 

[L41] * Name: Name of your QR Code, this is the name in your asset list. It will┬Ā**not**┬Ābe displayed on your screens.

[L42] * QR Code Type: Select the type of action that you want your QR code to initiate. Depend on the action, there could be additional information. For example, SMS action will ask a phone number and a message. Scanning this QR will open user's phone's message app, pre-fill in the To: phone number and a draft message.![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360051705853)

[L43] 

[L44] Click Save to create the QR Code.

[L45] 

[L46] You can Preview and use your phone to Scan and test to make sure the QR code is doing the actions you want it to do.

[L47] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360050796334)

[L48] ┬Ā

[L49] 

[L50] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L51] 

[L52] Now your QR Code is ready to use.  

[L53] You can use it with a number of apps that integrate QR code such as Google Reviews, Yelp Wall, etc. by edit the wall, in QR Code, select the one you just created.
