[L1] # How to use published Google Slides with OptiSigns (old way)

[L2] 

[L3] This method still works, but we have implemented [new method to connect to your Google Slides](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050642833).

[L4] ┬Ā

[L5] 

[L6] [Google Slides](https://www.google.com/slides/about/) is a very popular ways to create and share your presentation. You can also use it on your Digital Signage screen. For more 5 creatives ideas on how to use Google Slides on Digital Signs, [click here](https://www.optisigns.com/).

[L7] 

[L8] In this article we'll show you how to use Google Slides on OptiSigns. Here are the high level steps:

[L9] 

[L10] 1. Create and prepare your Google slides

[L11] 2. Add Google Slide App on OptiSigns

[L12] 3. Assign it to your screens

[L13] 

[L14] If this is the first time you use OptiSigns, you can [read here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv) to get set up.

[L15] 

[L16] Otherwise, let's dive in.

[L17] 

[L18] ## **1) Create and prepare your Google Slides**

[L19] 

[L20] Open your [Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/?usp=mkt_slides) presentation or create new a new presentation that you like.

[L21] 

[L22] If you need help in using Google Slides, [click here](https://www.wikihow.com/Create-a-Presentation-Using-Google-Slides).

[L23] 

[L24] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657754)

[L25] 

[L26] Once you are done with creating the slides.

[L27] 

[L28] Click File->Publish to web

[L29] 

[L30] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657814)

[L31] ┬Ā

[L32] 

[L33] Pick the duration you want for "Auto-advance slides".

[L34] 

[L35] Check "Start slideshow as soon as player loads.

[L36] 

[L37] Check "Restart the slideshow after the last slide".

[L38] 

[L39] Then Click "Publish"

[L40] 

[L41] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657854)

[L42] ┬Ā

[L43] 

[L44] Click OK to confirm that you want to publish

[L45] 

[L46] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657874)

[L47] 

[L48] You will get a link like below. Copy that link to clipboard. We will use it in OptiSigns Digital Signage.

[L49] 

[L50] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657974)

[L51] 

[L52] If you make changes to the slides, you don't have to regenerate the link again.

[L53] 

[L54] ## **2) Add Google Slide App on OptiSigns**

[L55] 

[L56] Go to our portal: https://app.optisigns.com

[L57] 

[L58] Click File/Asset

[L59] 

[L60] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055531173)

[L61] ┬Ā

[L62] 

[L63] Click App:

[L64] 

[L65] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658054)

[L66] ┬Ā

[L67] 

[L68] Click Google Slides:

[L69] 

[L70] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658094)

[L71] ┬Ā

[L72] 

[L73] Give it a name for you to remember, you can use the same Google's slide's name. In this case we put "Google Slide Presentation for Lobby TV"

[L74] 

[L75] Paste the URL you got from previous Step here.

[L76] 

[L77] ![mceclip8.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658254)

[L78] 

[L79] The Update Interval default is 10 minutes.

[L80] 

[L81] This mean the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes in your presentation.

[L82] 

[L83] If you have a lot of slides, you may want to change this interval to something longer.

[L84] 

[L85] Click Save

[L86] 

[L87] You will see your Google Slides Presentation is added.

[L88] 

[L89] ![mceclip9.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055531293)

[L90] ┬Ā

[L91] ┬Ā

[L92] 

[L93] ## **3) Assign it to your screens**

[L94] 

[L95] Now go back to Screen tab.

[L96] 

[L97] ![mceclip10.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658294)

[L98] 

[L99] Click Edit on the screen you want to display the slides on.

[L100] 

[L101] ![mceclip11.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658314)

[L102] 

[L103] In the Type drop-down click "Assets":

[L104] 

[L105] ![mceclip12.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055531333)

[L106] ┬Ā

[L107] 

[L108] Select the Google Slides App asset you just created.

[L109] 

[L110] ![mceclip13.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055531373)

[L111] 

[L112] Click Save

[L113] 

[L114] ![mceclip14.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054658394)

[L115] ┬Ā

[L116] ┬Ā

[L117] 

[L118] **That's it!** Your Google Slides is now playing on your screen.

[L119] 

[L120] If you make changes to the slides, and save them, the screen will updated every "Update Interval".

[L121]
