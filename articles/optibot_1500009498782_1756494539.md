[L1] # How to replace/update an existing asset (image, video, documents)

[L2] 

[L3] You can replace/change an existing asset (image, video or document) by hovering your mouse over the asset in your library, and click ... then click "Replace asset".

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500019968561)

[L8] 

[L9]  

[L10] 

[L11] A window will pop up.  You can drag in the new file that you want to use.

[L12] 

[L13] Click Upload

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500014519522)

[L16] 

[L17] The new file will be uploaded and take place of the old one.

[L18] 

[L19] Every where this image is used (playlist, schedule, ScreenZones, etc.) it will be replaced with the new file.

[L20] 

[L21] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500014828281)
