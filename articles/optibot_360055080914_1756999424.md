[L1] # How to Use USB HUB Adaptor Connect to Your Fire Stick

[L2] 

[L3] Sometime you have content behind a secured website that requires login, such as your project schedule in Atlassian's JIRA, Trello, dashboard behind PowerBI, Tableau, etc.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] To type in username, password, you just need to connect a [Bluetooth keyboard or mouse](https://www.amazon.com/Logitech-Illuminated-Living-Room-Keyboard-Touchpad/dp/B00ZOPVSKW/) with your Fire Stick.

[L8] 

[L9] There is more cost effective way to is to use [USB HUB Adaptor](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00LTHBCNM/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o08_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1) to connect USB keyboard, mouse with your Fire Stick.

[L10] 

[L11] * You can use this to connect your USB wireless keyboard or mouse with your Fire Stick.

[L12] * You can use this to connect your USB wired keyboard or mouse with your Fire Stick.

[L13] 

[L14] ![USB_HUB_Adaptor.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360093195114)

[L15] 

[L16] Note: You need to switch to OTM on USB HUB Adaptor

[L17] 

[L18] You're ready to go.

[L19] 

[L20] You can use a keyboard or mouse to enter login information.
