[L1] # Multi-users: Invite your team members to your account

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns, you can invite your team members to help with managing and updating screens.  

[L4] For more advanced multi teams and different levels of security setup, [please read this article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360034883113)

[L5] 

[L6] To invite your team members, go to Account Members in your Preference Setting.

[L7] 

[L8] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360102976193)

[L9] 

[L10] There are two options that you can invite your teammates.

[L11] 

[L12] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001917702)

[L13] 

[L14] **1. Add** (Add New User):

[L15] 

[L16] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001967801)Enter the New Team Member's Name, Email Address/ Username and select a Role of either User or Admin.

[L17] 

[L18] * Automatically generate a password: You can set the password for the user.

[L19] * Ask for a password change at the next sign-in: When the user login, the user will need to change the password or not.

[L20] 

[L21] Confirm this is a genuine request via reCAPTCHA

[L22] 

[L23] Click **Add New User**.

[L24] 

[L25] When you add the user successfully, you can send the email notification for the user.

[L26] 

[L27]  

[L28] 

[L29] **2. Invite** (Invite User)

[L30] 

[L31] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360100812154)

[L32] 

[L33] Enter the New Team Member's Name, and Email Address and select a Role of either User or Admin.

[L34] 

[L35] Confirm this is a genuine request via reCAPTCHA

[L36] 

[L37] Click **Invite**.

[L38] 

[L39] Your teammate will receive an invitation email looking like below:

[L40] 

[L41] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360025442233)

[L42] 

[L43] They can click on the link to finish the registration process and then can help you with managing your account.
