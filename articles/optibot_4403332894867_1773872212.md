[L1] # How to set up Audit Log for your account

[L2] 

[L3] With Pro Plus or Enterprise Plan, you can enable Audit Log feature, which will log who has made what changes to your account.

[L4] 

[L5] Here's the feature highlights:

[L6] 

[L7] * Audit Log data is kept for **2 years or 200K records which ever reached first**┬Ā(if you need longer data retention, please contact [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com), this will incur additional cost)

[L8] * User login (how user logged in via Google SSO, MS SSO or email, password)

[L9] * New, Update, Delete Screens

[L10] * New, Update. Delete Assets/Files

[L11] * New, Update. Delete Playlist

[L12] * New, Update. Delete Schedule

[L13] * New, Update, Delete, Folders, change folder security settings

[L14] * Update Personal Profile, change password

[L15] * New, Update, Invite, Delete Users, Team (who invites who, who updates who)

[L16] * Change to Account Preference

[L17] * Submit, approve, reject of approval requests

[L18] * You can filter, search, export data

[L19] 

[L20] **To enable Audit Log**

[L21] 

[L22] Go to your [account settings page](https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/preference-settings):

[L23] 

[L24] Click Enable Audit Log.

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50055310528787)After that audit events will automatically be logged.

[L27] 

[L28] **To view, search, export Audit Log data:**
