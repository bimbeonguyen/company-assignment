[L4] | --- |

[L8] ### With Instagram Business/Creator account you can:

[L9] 

[L10] * Display post from your profiles

[L11] * You can display posts from any public profiles (i.e.В <https://www.instagram.com/arsenal>)

[L12] * You can also follow, monitor up to 30 hashtags

[L13] * Get follower counts (for [Instagram Counter App](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360047501233))

[L14] 

[L15] ### **To set up an Instagram Business/Creator account:**

[L16] 

[L17] Open Instagram app on you phone, you can convert your personal profile to a business account to access features that can help you grow your business.

[L18] 

[L19] To switch your profile to a business account:

[L20] 

[L21] 1. Go to your profile and tapВ ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?revision=641770783093270&name=instagram-sidetrayhamburger-ios&density=1)В in the upper right corner.

[L22] 2. TapВ ![](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/assets/?revision=641770783093270&name=instagram-newsettings-ios&density=1)**Settings.**

[L23] 3. TapВ **Account**.

[L24] 4. TapВ **Switch to Professional Account**.

[L25] 5. TapВ **Business**. or **Creator**

[L26] 6. Follow the steps to connect your business account to a Facebook Page associated with your business. **This step is required** for your Instagram Business/Account to work with 3rd party app like OptiSigns. At this time, only one Facebook Page can be connected to your business account.

[L27] 7. Add details, like your business category and contact information.

[L28] 8. TapВ **Done**.

[L29] 

[L30] You can now use your Instagram with OptiSigns, when log in, authenticate, please use the Facebook account that you linked in Step 6 above.
