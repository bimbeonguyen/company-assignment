[L1] # How to use YouTube with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] Putting YouTube video on your digital signs screens is easy with OptiSigns.  

[L4] You just need to copy the YouTube Link and paste in the YouTube app in OptiSigns.

[L5] 

[L6] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L7] 

[L8] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click┬Ā[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L9] 

[L10] Then log on to our portal:┬Ā<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L11] 

[L12] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L13] 

[L14] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26482420798611)

[L15] ┬Ā

[L16] 

[L17] Click YouTube (or YouTube Live):

[L18] 

[L19] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360077769013)

[L20] 

[L21] Enter your YouTube video information:

[L22] 

[L23] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076614294)

[L24] 

[L25] * **Name**: Give your YouTube video a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only. The app will try to get the video's title and use as name, but you can change it to something else.

[L26] * **URL**: Paste the link to your YouTube video here. This should be the actual URL, not the Share link:

[L27] 

[L28] ![firefox_0SsesYq2vw(1).png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/39392515861395)

[L29] 

[L30] |  |

[L31] | --- |

[L32] | **NOTE** |

[L33] | For Youtube Shorts, change the part of the URL that reads┬Ā**/shorts****/**to instead read┬Ā**/embed/**.Example:

[L34] 

[L35] <https://youtube.com/shorts/BUXczOFByAU?si=TO8RGfmokQMdsI6R> 

[L36] becomes

[L37] [https://youtube.com/embed/BUXczOFByAU?si=TO8RGfmokQMdsI6R](https://youtube.com/embed/BUXczOFByAU?si=TO8RGfmokQMdsI6R "https://youtube.com/embed/BUXczOFByAU?si=TO8RGfmokQMdsI6R")This will allow Youtube Shorts to display in OptiSigns.

[L38]  |

[L39] 

[L40] * **Caption**:┬ĀIf the YouTube video has closed caption, the app will show it. Note: closed caption must be created by the YouTube video author, auto generated caption may not work.

[L41] 

[L42] Click Save.  

[L43] After Saving, you can Preview the video to verify.  

[L44]   

[L45] 

[L46] ## **That's all! Congratulations!**

[L47] 

[L48] You have added a YouTube video.

[L49] 

[L50] You can assign the newly created YouTube asset to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L51] 

[L52] You can put the YouTube Video to a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L53]
