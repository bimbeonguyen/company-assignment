[L1] # Present/ share iOS/ Android phone/ tablet to your screen with OptiSigns & AeriCast Add-on

[L2] 

[L3] In this article, we will guide you on how to present/ share from iOS/ Android phone/ tablet to your screen.

[L4] 

[L5] You can download the AeriCast Presenter app from the app store.¬†

[L6] 

[L7] * iOS App Store: <https://apps.apple.com/us/app/aericast-presenter/id1629924738>

[L8] * Android App Store: <https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aericast.presenter>

[L9] 

[L10] ![AeriCast_Presenter_QR.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558247414163)

[L11] 

[L12] On your TVs running OptiSigns, you should see 4-digit presenter codes like below:

[L13] 

[L14] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14557985533075)

[L15] 

[L16] Or at the bottom strip:

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558020377363)

[L19] 

[L20] You can click [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/14502723487379) to know how to create the AeriCast Wireless Presentation app¬†

[L21] 

[L22] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L23] 

[L24] Launch the **AeriCast Present** app. Enter the present code.

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14557991027603)

[L27] 

[L28] Click "**Tap to present**"

[L29] 

[L30] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558021130003)

[L31] 

[L32] Click "**Start Broadcast**"

[L33] 

[L34] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558027185811)

[L35] 

[L36] You will preset your phone/ tablet on your screen.

[L37] 

[L38] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558077259795)

[L39] 

[L40] When you are done presenting, you can click the red button on the top right of the screen. Click "**Stop**"

[L41] 

[L42] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14558079816083)

[L43] 

[L44] ### That's all!

[L45] 

[L46] You can also present your laptop on your screen. Click [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/14502378549907) for more info.
