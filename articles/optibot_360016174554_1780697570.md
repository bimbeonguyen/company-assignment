[L1] # Amazon Firestick

[L2] 

[L3] |  |

[L4] | --- |

[L5] | **PLEASE READ** |

[L6] | 

[L7] With the launch of the Amazon Signage Stick, Amazon has been removing support for digital signage on FireSticks. As such, OptiSigns will be ending its support for Amazon Firesticks in the near future**.** We recommend our OptiStick device, or the Amazon Signage Stick.

[L8] In addition, in October 2024, [**Amazon eliminated the ability to auto start the app**](https://www.aftvnews.com/amazon-blocks-long-running-fire-tv-capability-breaking-popular-apps-with-no-warning-and-giving-developers-the-runaround/) with FireStick & Fire TVs starting with FireOS 8. Amazon have released their own [**digital signage stick**](https://signage.amazon.com/), which supports OptiSigns, and we also have our own [**OptiSigns Android Player**](https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/order-device). |

[L9] 

[L10] ┬Ā

[L11] There are workarounds with [ADB commands](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/23274673797139) or manually to start the app.

[L12] However, those workarounds are very inconvenient. We believe that, without an easy way to AutoStart apps, the Fire Stick is not a good solution for a Digital Signage player.

[L13] ┬Ā

[L14] In addition, newer Firesticks, including the Firestick Select series, use a new OS called Vega OS. In addition to the auto-start problems, with Vega OS the app will fall asleep frequently unless videos are played every several minutes. This means it cannot be used to display static images by themselves.

[L15] ┬Ā

[L16] Consider a dedicated player, like our [Android Stick Player](https://links.optisigns.com/szzk), Amazon's [digital signage stick](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/34087061669523-Amazon-Digital-Signage-Stick-Setup), Google ChromeCast, or any [Windows, Mac, or Linux](https://www.optisigns.com/download) device.

[L17] * [FireStick Lite](https://www.amazon.com/fire-tv-stick-lite-latest-alexa-voice-remote-lite/dp/B091G4YP57/) - $29.99 - this one is still running FireOS 7 and still can AutoStart OptiSigns. However, Amazon may roll out OS updates in the future.

[L18] 	+ FireStick Lite has severe issues due to performance, so we do not recommend these even though they technically work.

[L34] ## **Here are the high-level steps:**

[L35] 

[L36] 1) Set up Amazon Fire TV Stick

[L37] 

[L38] 2) Download OptiSigns Digital Signage from Amazon App Store

[L39] 

[L40] 3) Using OptiSigns' web portal to assign content and remotely manage your screens

[L41] 

[L42] So let's dive in!

[L43] 

[L44] ┬Ā

[L45] ### **1) Set up Amazon Fire TV Stick**

[L46] 

[L47] Plug in FireStick and connect it to power.

[L48] 

[L49] When connecting to power, it's temping to use the USB of the TV to power the Fire TV for convenience, but Fire TV consume a lot more power than a TV's USB can supply. So, it is always best to use the power adapter and cable that come with the Fire TV.

[L50] 

[L51] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055529993)

[L52] 

[L53] Power on the Fire TV and follow the on-screen instruction to connect to Wi-Fi, and register the device with your Amazon account.

[L54] 

[L55] |  |

[L56] | --- |

[L57] | Note: Amazon Fire TV requires internet to work. |

[L58] 

[L59] ### **2) Download & Set up OptiSigns Digital Signage from Amazon App Store**

[L60] 

[L61] Press the **Search** icon on the **Find** section of the Fire TV,

[L62] 

[L63] ![Fire_Stick-12.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012109741)

[L64] 

[L65] Type in "**OptiSigns**".  

[L66] Fire TV Search can be tricky to use, and sometimes, as you type, it does not work properly.  

[L67] After you have typed "optisigns", be sure to press the menu down button until the "Optisigns" text is highlighted and then click on it.

[L68] 

[L69] ![Fire_Stick-13.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011810362)

[L70] 

[L71] Click **Download** on the OptiSigns app icon

[L72] 

[L73] ![Fire_Stick-14.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012113201)

[L74] 

[L75] Once the app is installed, **launch** it.

[L76] 

[L77] The app will ask if you want to automatically start when device start up. It is recommended to select **yes**, unless you want to manually launch the app yourself every time.

[L78] 

[L79] Next, you will see this pairing screen:

[L80] 

[L81] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054657494)

[L82] 

[L83] Go to our website: [https://app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/app/screenManagement)

[L84] 

[L85] If you don't have an account already, you can create one, or you can also log in with Google or Facebook account.

[L86] 

[L87] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055530533)Once you logged in Click "**Add screen**" button

[L88] ┬Ā

[L89] 

[L90] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054655934)

[L91] In this pop up, type in the Pair Code showing up on your Fire TV screen. Then, click Pair:

[L92] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054655994)

[L93] 

[L94] The Fire TV screen will change to:

[L95] 

[L96] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055530573)

[L97] 

[L98] Now you are ready to upload and assign content.
