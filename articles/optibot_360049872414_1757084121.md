[L1] # How to use Facebook Video App

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you through how to create and use Facebook Video App.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360075549614) 

[L8] 

[L9] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L10] 

[L11] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L12] 

[L13] Open the Facebook video that you would like to use and copy the URL:

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360075549634)

[L16] 

[L17] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L18] 

[L19] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L20] 

[L21] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26482753727507)

[L22]  

[L23] 

[L24] Click Facebook Video:

[L25] 

[L26] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076684933)

[L27] 

[L28] Enter your information:

[L29] 

[L30] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076684973)

[L31] 

[L32] * Name: Name of your Facebook Video asset, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L33] * Facebook Video URL: Paste the video URL link that you copied from Facebook here.

[L34] * Mute: Select if you want to mute the video during playback.

[L35] 

[L36] Click Save.  

[L37] After Saving, you can Preview the asset.  

[L38]   

[L39] 

[L40] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L41] 

[L42] You have created your Facebook Video asset.  

[L43] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab. 

[L44] 

[L45] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L46] 

[L47] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L48] 

[L49]
