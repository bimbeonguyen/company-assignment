[L1] # How to display Google Slide in Portrait Mode

[L2] 

[L3] You can set up Google Slides to present properly in Portrait mode by setting the right page dimensions.  

[L4] [Here's an example](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1k2Er26_MJhwrYEMcwiq766OCTMAFj4V2fqb9kP3tdn8/edit?usp=sharing).

[L5] 

[L6] Go go your Google Slides.  

[L7] Click File -> Page setup...

[L8] 

[L9] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360028446254)

[L10] 

[L11] Select Custom, and enter an aspect ratio of 9:16 (Normal TV's aspect ratio)  

[L12] 7.5 and 13.33 inches are a good reference.  

[L13] That's it! you can start editing your sides now and assign it to your screens with OptiSigns.  

[L14] For more information on how to use Google Slides with OptiSigns in general, please [click here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/use-google-slides-with-optisigns).
