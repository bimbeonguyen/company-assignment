[L31] The OptiSigns Designer app is our most feature-rich application, allowing complete creation of content for display on digital signs. This guide will get you started on creating content within the OptiSigns platform.

[L32] 

[L33] ---

[L34] 

[L35] ## Opening Designer

[L36] 

[L37] To open designer, click **Files/Assets**, then **App**.

[L38] 

[L39] ![opening designer app](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941829011)

[L40] 

[L41] Next, hit **Designer**. This will open the Designer app.

[L42] 

[L43] ![designer app](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941835667)

[L44]

[L62] ## Canvas

[L63] 

[L64] The Designer viewer is where your design lives. Each element is visible and selectable within the window.

[L65] 

[L66] ![designer viewer](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011998739)

[L67] 

[L68] Elements can be moved, resized, deleted, and more from here.

[L71] 

[L72] ## Top Menu - Basic Functions

[L73] 

[L74] The Top Menu of the Design window is where basic functions can be found. These include adding images, text, shapes, backgrounds, filters, animation, and frames, naming your design, drawing, and resizing your design.

[L75] 

[L76] ### Naming Your Design

[L77] 

[L78] By default, each new design will be titled “Your new untitled design,” as shown below.

[L79] 

[L80] ![designer name entry](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941844243)

[L81] 

[L82] We recommend changing this name as soon as possible, as, while it is possible to save a design with the default name, it will be hard to identify in your Assets folder.

[L83] 

[L84] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42282228321299)Once the design has been given a name, it will appear there throughout the design process. 

[L85] 

[L86] ### Move, Save, New Design, and Download Functions

[L87] 

[L88] The **Move**, **Save**, **New Design**, and **Download** functions are to the right of the name.

[L89] 

[L90] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42282228324115)These work the same way as in most other applications:

[L91] 

[L92] **Move -** Lets you move the Design asset to different folders. When hitting this button, a popup will appear allowing you to **Select Folder**.

[L93] 

[L94] ![select folder tab](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941861011)

[L95] 

[L96] You can also create a New Folder here if needed.

[L97] 

[L98] **Save -** Saves your Design asset. This will overwrite any earlier versions of the asset, unless you give it another name.

[L99] 

[L100] |  |

[L101] | --- |

[L102] | **NOTE** |

[L103] | Hitting **Save** with a blank design triggers a warning letting you know you are saving a blank design. The design will not save. |

[L104] 

[L105] ![empty design saved notification](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941863315)

[L106] 

[L107] Any time a change is made to your design without saving, a notification will appear in the Top Menu alerting you that you have unsaved changes:

[L108] 

[L109] ![unsaved changes notification](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941868819)

[L110] 

[L111] **New Design -** Exits the current design and creates a new one. The **New Design** panel will appear on the left sidebar:

[L112] 

[L113] ![new design left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941873043)

[L114] 

[L115] Here, you can determine the New Design’s orientation and size.

[L116] 

[L117] **Download -** Downloads the current design to your local device. You’ll be able to rename the file, and choose between **PNG** and **JPG** filetypes.

[L118] 

[L119] ![download options](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941875987)

[L121] ### Image

[L122] 

[L123] The **Image** option allows you to upload images into your design.

[L124] 

[L125] ![image button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941879187)

[L126] 

[L127] When clicked, it will give you two options: **My Device**, and **Image Assets**.

[L128] 

[L129] ![image location options](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941883155)

[L130] 

[L131] Clicking My Device will let you upload an image from the current device you’re using, while Image Assets lets you upload an image directly from your Files/Assets library.

[L132] 

[L133] For more, see our [**Images / Photos in Designer**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/42526907045651-Photos-GIFs-and-QR-Codes-in-Designer) article.

[L135] ### Text

[L136] 

[L137] The **Text** option allows you to insert text into the design.

[L138] 

[L139] ![text button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941884563)

[L140] 

[L141] Text can be chosen from either the Top Menu or the Side Menu. Either will open the Side Menu for more options:

[L142] 

[L143] ![text left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011924371)

[L144] 

[L145] Clicking **Add a text box** or one of the Predefined text options will add a text box to the Designer View:

[L146] 

[L147] ![text added to design](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011925523)

[L148] 

[L149] Do as the prompt says, and numerous options open up to you. First, the Text itself has a full set of options:

[L150] 

[L151] ![text options](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941889939)Clicking the text also alters the Top Menu:

[L152] 

[L153] ![text change top menu](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941891347)Double-clicking or selecting the text allows you to enter text directly.

[L154] 

[L155] ![text entering demonstration](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011930259)Text can be resized at will.

[L156] 

[L157] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45411285130515)

[L158] 

[L159] |  |

[L160] | --- |

[L161] | **IMPORTANT NOTE** |

[L162] | How your text is resized is determined by which part of the text you drag from. If you drag **from the sides**, this will **Crop** your text and force it to fit into a smaller range. Dragging **from the corners** will maintain the aspect ratio of the selection and change the size of the font automatically. |

[L163] 

[L164] Lastly, single clicking the text, then holding down the mouse button and dragging allows you to reposition the text.

[L165] 

[L166] For a complete breakdown of the Text function, see our [**Text in Designer article**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/42157810188179-Customize-Text).

[L237] ### Draw

[L238] 

[L239] Selecting the **Draw** option will allow freeform drawing on your design.

[L240] 

[L241] ![draw button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941931411)

[L242] 

[L243] Clicking it opens up the **Draw** panel on the Side Menu:

[L244] 

[L245] ![draw option left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011974803)

[L246] 

[L247] Here, you’ll have a few options:

[L248] 

[L249] * **Stop Drawing** - Keeps the Draw panel open, but allows adjustments to be made to the image. When clicked, this will change to **Start Drawing**.

[L250] * **Brush Type** - Selects which type of brush to use when drawing.

[L251] * **Brush Size** - Lets you customize the size of your brush.

[L252] * **Brush Color** - Select brush color from a set of default colors or through the color picker.

[L253] 

[L254] Once the options you want have been selected, you can draw whatever you like on the design:

[L255] 

[L256] ![designer draw demonstration](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941936403)These drawn elements can be selected and edited just like any other element of the design.

[L257] 

[L258] ![drawn elements move demonstation](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941938067)### Resize

[L259] 

[L260] Selecting the **Resize** option allows you to change the size of the design.

[L261] 

[L262] ![resize button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088011986195)

[L263] 

[L264] Clicking it will open the **Resize** area on the Side Menu:

[L265] 

[L266] ![resize left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941942675)

[L267] 

[L268] Here, you’ll have a few options:

[L269] 

[L270] * **Orientation -** Switch between Landscape and Portrait orientation.

[L271] * **New Size -** Let’s you change the width and height of the design. The default or custom numbers will swap to adjust to selected orientation.

[L272] * **Maintain Aspect Ratio** - When checked, any change to width or height will be mirrored by the other value.

[L273] * **Use Percentages -** Changes the width and height values from pixels to percentages. Will grey out the orientation selections.

[L274] * **Resize Canvas Size Only -** When checked, will only alter the size of the canvas, not any of the elements present in the design. If not checked, all elements of the design will be resized to match the canvas.

[L275] 

[L276] Once you’ve made the adjustments you’d like, hit the **Resize** button and your design will be resized.

[L319] ### Undo/Redo

[L320] 

[L321] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42282228332691)The **Undo** option does what it says on the tin - it undoes the last action. If this button is pushed in error, the **Redo** button will fix it.

[L323] |  |

[L330] ## Toolbar - Positioning

[L331] 

[L332] The Toolbar on the right hand is primarily used for positioning elements of your design via **Zoom, Layer, History,** and the **Hand Tool** functions.

[L333] 

[L334] ### Zoom

[L335] 

[L336] Shows the level of **Zoom** on the design. Can be adjusted with the ‘+’ or ‘-’ buttons.

[L337] 

[L338] ![zoom button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941957523)

[L339] 

[L340] Can be adjusted with the ‘+’ or ‘-’ buttons.

[L341] 

[L342] ![zoom demonstration](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941959059)### Layers

[L343] 

[L344] The **Layers** button allows you to change the layering of the elements on your design.

[L345] 

[L346] ![layer button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088012017939)

[L347] 

[L348] When clicked, it will open the **Position** window on the Side Menu, under the **Layers** tab.

[L349] 

[L350] ![layer tab left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941967123)

[L351] 

[L352] Here, the different elements of your design will be arranged in from the top layer to the bottom layer. They can be dragged and dropped to rearrange the layers, like so:

[L353] 

[L354] ![layer demonstration](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088107390611)Note that widgets cannot be rearranged and will always be overlaid on top of the design.

[L355] 

[L356] ### History

[L357] 

[L358] Selecting the **History** button will allow you to view the history of all changes made to the design.

[L359] 

[L360] ![history button location](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941970195)

[L361] 

[L362] Clicking it will open the **Position** window on the Side Menu under the **History** tab:

[L363] 

[L364] ![history tab left navbar](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42087941973267)

[L365] 

[L366] This will display the actions taken in descending order, from how the design was when it first opened through each individual change. Clicking each will bring the design back to that point:

[L367] 

[L368] ![history tab demonstration](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42088012037523)Please note that the History tab will clear each time the design is closed and reopened.
