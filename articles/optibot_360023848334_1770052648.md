[L1] # How to Use the Facebook Page App

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain how to set up a Facebook app to display information off Facebook on your digital signs.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Setting Up a Facebook App](#SettingUp)

[L7] 	+ [Advanced](#Advanced)

[L8] * [Deploying a Facebook App](#Deploying)

[L9] 

[L10] With the Facebook app, you're able to display posts and such from public-facing Facebook pages you have ownership access over.

[L11] 

[L12] Let's get started.

[L13] 

[L14] ---

[L15] 

[L16] ## What You'll Need

[L17] 

[L18] * An OptiSigns account - [**Free Plan or higher**](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)

[L19] * A Facebook account

[L20] * An [OptiSigns-enabled device](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021855653-What-hardware-and-devices-are-supported) - if on Free plan, one of [these devices](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/33940834613139-What-Do-I-Get-With-an-OptiSigns-Free-Plan#Devices)

[L21] * A screen, [set up and paired with OptiSigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/18823504383891-OptiSigns-Getting-Started-Guide)

[L25] ## Setting Up a Facebook App

[L26] 

[L27] On the OptiSigns portal, go to **Assets ‚Üí Add Asset ‚Üí Apps.**

[L28] 

[L29] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277912467)

[L30] 

[L31] Select **Facebook**:

[L32] 

[L33] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749293297299)

[L34] 

[L35] Login to Facebook. If this is the first time registering it with OptiSigns, you will need to hit **Connect Facebook Pages** then log in.¬†![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277917715)

[L36] 

[L37] ¬†

[L38] The Facebook App allows you to register numerous Facebook accounts. If you have already registered your account, you can choose it with the **Select** option.

[L39] ¬†

[L40] After logging on with Facebook in the Facebook popup window, select the Pages you want OptiSigns to access data to create your Wall.

[L41] ¬†![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277921427)

[L42] 

[L43] Click **Next**, then Click **Done**.

[L44] ¬†

[L45] Whether you connect a new Facebook page or choose it from an existing on, you'll see the below screen:

[L46] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277928595)

[L47] 

[L48] After that, you can enter information for your wall.* **Name**: Name of your Facebook Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L49] * **Page**: Page you want to use (if you have more than 1).¬†

[L50] 

[L51] |  |

[L52] | --- |

[L53] | **IMPORTANT** |

[L54] | A Facebook asset can only access a single page, and this can only be changed during the creation of the asset. In order to change this, you will need to delete this asset and create a new one.If you wish to display multiple Facebook pages, you will need to create individual assets for each one, then use them in a [Playlist](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/28295104605843-How-to-Create-Use-Playlists). |

[L55] * **Display Mode**: Choose how you want your Facebook page to display. There are numerous options here - play around with them, and they should automatically populate the Preview area on the right. Choose the one which suits you best!

[L56] * **Theme**: Change between Light and Dark themes.

[L57] * **Speed**: This determines how quickly your Facebook posts cycle. Choose between **Slow**, **Medium**, **Fast**, and **Custom**. When Custom is chosen, you will see the following slider:

[L58] 

[L59] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277931283)

[L60] * **Font Size**:¬†Set the font size. Default is 20, but this can be set to **Custom** and changed to whatever you wish.

[L61] * **Landscape / Portrait:** Choose the orientation for the Preview.

[L62] 

[L63] ### Advanced

[L64] 

[L65] Click the **Advanced** option to¬†

[L66] 

[L67] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277935763)

[L68] 

[L69] * **Moderation:** Clicking Moderation opens up a popup:

[L70] 

[L71] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749293318419)

[L72] 

[L73] This screen allows you to determine which posts will appear on your app - you can change the settings to disallow certain posts.

[L74] 

[L75] * **Always show new posts:** When checked, this will ensure you are only showing new posts

[L76] * **Filter Type:** Choose to Filter by Quantity or by Time. If one of these is selected, you can choose how many posts to show, or across what timeframe to show posts.

[L77] * **Only show posts with image:** When selected, will only show posts that have images. If you want to display text-only Facebook posts, leave this unchecked.

[L78] * **Scrolling Strip Font Size:** Set the font size of the scrolling strip. Default is 20, but this can be set to **Custom** and changed to whatever you wish.

[L79] * **Continue Playing Content in Playlist:** When checked, if the Facebook wall is placed in a Playlist, it will continue displaying posts from where it left off. Otherwise, it will always start with the first post in your sequence.

[L80] * **Show QR Code**: When checked, allows you to add a QR code. You can choose for the QR code to go to the Facebook page, or to any other page by choosing **Custom**.

[L81] 

[L82] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749293325203)

[L83] 

[L84] Click Save to the QR code and the Facebook app page.¬†Finally, hit **Save**. You've created a Facebook asset.

[L88] ## Deploying a Facebook App

[L89] 

[L90] You can deploy your new Facebook app as an individual asset, or as part of a [Split Screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

[L91] 

[L92] To get your new Facebook asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

[L93] 

[L94] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277954707)

[L95] 

[L96] This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

[L97] 

[L98] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749277959699)

[L99] 

[L100] Here, select **Asset** under Content type, then hit **Select Asset**.

[L101] 

[L102] Then, select your created Facebook Asset:

[L103] 

[L104] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48749293336467)

[L105] 

[L106] Now hit **Save**. Your Facebook asset will now display on screen.

[L107] 

[L108] You can also deploy it as part of a split screen, allowing you to show other assets at the same time. See how in our [Split Screen app article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App)¬†

[L109] 

[L110] In order to display multiple Facebook pages, you can create individual page assets, then place them in a [Playlist](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/28295104605843-How-to-Create-Use-Playlists). The asset or playlist can also be placed in a [Schedule](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016981853-Creating-and-Using-Schedules-with-OptiSigns).
