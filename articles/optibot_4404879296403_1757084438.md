[L1] # How to Clean Data on the OptiSigns Android Stick

[L2] 

[L3] This article, it guides you on how to clear data on the Android Stick.  

[L4]   

[L5] 1. Go to the Setting 

[L6] 

[L7] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780903482131)

[L8] 

[L9] 2. Click **Apps**  

[L10] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780946862355)

[L11] 

[L12] 3. Click **OptiSigns**

[L13] 

[L14] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780946867347)

[L15] 

[L16] 4. Select Clear data

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780946872723)

[L19] 

[L20] 5. Select **OK**

[L21] 

[L22] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780946875155)

[L23] 

[L24] 6. Then you can click **Open** to relaunch the OptiSigns app

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/37780903501843)
