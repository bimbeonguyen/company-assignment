[L1] # Android TV / ChromeCast

[L2] 

[L3] In this guide, we will walk you through end to end process to install OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your Android TV and assign a playlist to it.

[L4] 

[L5] ---

[L6] 

[L7] ## **Here are the high-level steps:**

[L8] 

[L9] 1) **Download** & **Set** **up** OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your Android TV.  

[L10]   

[L11] 2) Using OptiSigns's web portal to assign content and manage your screens  

[L12]   

[L13] So let's dive in!

[L14] 

[L15] ## Download & Set up OptiSigns Digital Signage from Google App Store

[L16] 

[L17] Press the "**Get more app"** button on the Android TV.

[L18] 

[L19] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500006116882)

[L20] 

[L21] Type in "**O****ptiSigns**" > Be sure to press the menu down button until the "**OptiSigns Digital Signage**" text is highlighted > **Click** on it.

[L22] 

[L23] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500006232641)

[L24] 

[L25] Click **Download** on the OptiSigns app icon.

[L26] 

[L27] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500006116982)

[L28] 

[L29] Once the app is installed, **launch** it.

[L30] 

[L31] |  |

[L32] | --- |

[L33] | The app will ask if you want to automatically start when device start up. It recommended to select yes, unless you want to launch the app yourself every time manually. |

[L34] 

[L35] You then will see this pairing screen:

[L36] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399132494227)

[L37] 

[L38] Go to our website: [https://app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/app/screenManagement)

[L39] 

[L40] If you don't have an account already, create one, or you can also log in with Google or Facebook account.

[L41] 

[L42] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399147694355)

[L43] 

[L44] Once you logged in click "**Add screen**" button.

[L45] 

[L46] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399147696147)

[L47] In this pop up window, type in the Pair Code showing up on your Android TV screen > Click **Pair**

[L48] 

[L49] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399147697427)

[L50] 

[L51] The Android TV screen will change to:

[L52] 

[L53] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399147699603)

[L54] 

[L55] Now you are ready to upload and assign content!

[L56]
