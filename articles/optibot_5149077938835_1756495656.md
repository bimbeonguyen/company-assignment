[L1] # How to use Advanced Website App

[L2] 

[L3] Sometimes you have requirement to show a certain website and have it scroll or zoom in a certain way.  

[L4] Advanced Website App is the solution for that, it will allow you to:

[L5] 

[L6] * Set the app to scroll down & up the web page, or just scroll to a certain location

[L7] * Set scrolling speed

[L8] * Set the app to zoom in/out

[L9] 

[L10] ## Let's jump in and get started:

[L11] 

[L12] ┬ĀGo to File/Assets -> App, search and select Advanced Website App:

[L13] 

[L14] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/5174945453843)

[L15] 

[L16] ┬Ā

[L17] 

[L18] Set up your Advanced Website App:

[L19] 

[L20] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/5175002170899)

[L21] 

[L22] * Name: name of your Advanced Website App, this is only for use in File/Asset list, it will not be displayed on your screen.

[L23] * URL: the URL link to the page you want to display.

[L24] * Scroll Type: You can select

[L25] 	+ - * Down & Up: the app will scroll the web page down, and then scroll up.

[L26] 			* Scroll to: the app will jump to a certain location on the web page

[L27] * Speed: how fast you want to scroll, you can also customize the speed.

[L28] * Page Height: enter the number in Pixel. This is needed because the app does not know how long a web page is, also some web page like social media has infinite scrolling.

[L29] * Zoom: depend on what kind of your website's content, zoom may help the content to be displayed better on your screens.

[L30] * Update interval: default is 10 minutes. This means the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes of your website.

[L31] 

[L32] You can click Advanced and set:

[L33] 

[L34] * Preload: if you want to show your Advanced Website App to user instantly (when you put it in a playlist), you can set preload value (by seconds), the app will preload the URL in the background.

[L35] * Render Engine: if your website is not displayed as expected, you can change between three different webview: Normal, CrossWalk or Advanced Webview on FireStick Normal, FireStick 4K or Other devices.

[L36] 

[L37] Click Save to save your asset.

[L38] 

[L39] You can preview how it look by clicking the "Preview button".

[L40] 

[L41] ┬Ā

[L42] 

[L43] ## That's all!

[L44] 

[L45] Congratulation! You have created your Advanced Website App.

[L46] 

[L47] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L48] 

[L49] ┬Ā

[L50] You can assign the newly created app to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L51] 

[L52] You can put the walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
