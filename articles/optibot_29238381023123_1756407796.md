[L1] # How to Clear Cache on a BrightSign Player

[L2] 

[L3] ### If you're experiencing issues with OptiSigns' applications not performing correctly on your BrightSign player, one of the most common reason is localStorage folder on your SD Card is full.

[L4] 

[L5] * BrightSigns does not provide API for apps like OptiSigns to clear or delete the local cache folder.

[L6] * So after sometime, the folder can be so big that it consume all storage on the SD card and cause issues.

[L7] 

[L8] |  |

[L9] | --- |

[L10] | **This can help resolve problems such as apps not updating properly, displaying outdated content, or other performance issues.** |

[L13] ## Follow these steps to clear the cache on your BrightSign player:

[L14] 

[L15] ### **Step 1: Remove the SD Card**

[L16] 

[L17] **1. Locate the SD Card Slot:** Find the SD card slot on your BrightSign player.

[L18] 

[L19] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/29258078833811)

[L20] 

[L21] **2. Eject the SD Card:** Carefully remove the SD card from the slot.

[L22] 

[L23] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/29258078842387)

[L24] 

[L25] ### **Step 2: Clear the Cache from the SD Card**

[L26] 

[L27] **1. Insert the SD Card into a Computer:** Use an SD card reader to connect the SD card to your computer.

[L28] 

[L29] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/29258094750611)

[L30] 

[L31] **2. Access the SD Card's Contents:** Once the SD card is recognized by your computer, open it in File Explorer (Windows) or Finder (Mac).

[L32] 

[L33] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/29258094752531)**3. Locate the Local Storage Folder:** Search for a folder typically named ŌĆś**localStorage**ŌĆÖ.

[L34] 

[L35] * The exact name can vary based on how the app developer set up the directory structure.

[L36] 

[L37] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/29258094758035)

[L38] 

[L39] **4. Check the size of the localStorage Folder**

[L40] 

[L41] * See if the folder is taking up many GBs.

[L42] 

[L43] **5. Delete the localStorage Folder:** Right-click on the folder and select "**Delete**" to clear all cached data.

[L44] 

[L45] * If youŌĆÖre unsure about deleting, you can alternatively move the folder to your computer as a backup before deleting it from the SD card. This may take several minutes depending on how many items are in the folder.

[L63] **1. Reset/Reboot the Player:** Reset or reboot your BrightSign player. It will now reload its contents from the SD card, which no longer includes the previously stored cache.
