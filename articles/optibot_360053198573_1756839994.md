[L1] # How to uninstall/reinstall OptiSigns app from your Fire Stick device

[L2] 

[L3] Sometimes, if you experiencing issues with OptiSigns app, a common way to fix is to reinstall the app.  

[L4] In this guide, we will walk you through end to end process to uninstall OptiSigns app from your Amazon Fire Stick device.  

[L5] Here is a quick video showing you how to uninstall the reinstall the OptiSigns app on your Fire Stick:

[L6] 

[L7] ### How to Uninstall OptiSigns app on Fire Stick

[L8] 

[L9] To uninstall OptiSigns app on your Fire Stick, go to Settings > Applications > Manage Installed Applications. Then select OptiSignsВ app, and select Uninstall. Finally, click Uninstall again when you see the pop-up warning screen.

[L10] 

[L11] 1. From your Fire Stick homepage go to **"Settings".**

[L12] 

[L13] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813162)

[L14] 

[L15] 2. Scroll through and click **"Applications".**

[L16] 

[L17] ![Fire_Stick-17_Application.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813182)

[L18] 

[L19] 3. Then click **"Manage Installed Applications".**

[L20] 

[L21] ![Fire_Stick-18_Management.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813202)

[L22] 

[L23] 4. Select OptiSignsВ app that you want to uninstall.

[L24] 

[L25] ![Fire_Stick-19_Management_OptiSigns.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114501)

[L26] 

[L27] 5. Then scroll down and click **"Uninstall".**

[L28] 

[L29] ![Fire_Stick-22_uninstall.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813702)

[L30] 

[L31] 6. Click **"Uninstall".**

[L32] 

[L33] ![Fire_Stick-23_uninstall.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011813722)

[L34] 

[L35] Now you uninstall Optisigns app successfully. You can reinstall the app. You can go [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016174554) to learn more about how to install Optisigns app.

[L36]
