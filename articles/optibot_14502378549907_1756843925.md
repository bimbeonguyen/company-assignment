[L1] # Present / share your screen with OptiSigns & AeriCast Add-on

[L2] 

[L3] In this article, we will guide you on how to present/ share from your laptop to your screen.

[L4] 

[L5] On your TVs running OptiSigns, you should see 4 digit presenter codes like below:  

[L6] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14557704816659)  

[L7] Or at the bottom strip:  

[L8] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/article_attachments/14558304494483/57587dcb74a671880199ecea65c2e566f11ddffdd56714dde673d8b07a3d973e.png)

[L9] 

[L10] You can click [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/14502723487379) to know how to create the AeriCast Wireless Presentation app┬Ā

[L11] 

[L12] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L13] 

[L14] Go to┬Ā[present.aericast.com](https://present.aericast.com/)(we recommend Chrome or FireFox for best experience).

[L15] Enter the 4 digit codes

[L16] Click Continue

[L17] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502304608531)

[L18] Now presenter is connected to the room, just click Present to start sharing screen.

[L19] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502347997843)

[L20] 

[L21] You can select sharing:

[L22] * Your entire screen (you will need to select the screen then click share, see 1, 2 in below screenshot)

[L23] * A certain Application

[L24] * Or a certain Tab on your Browser

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502351030035)

[L27] ┬Ā

[L28] You can stop presenting by click either the "Stop" button or "Stop Sharing" button.┬Ā

[L29] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502387545875)

[L30] If there are more than 1 presenters, you can share the Presenter Code with your colleague so they can join the room as presenters.

[L31] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502381725075)

[L32] When there are more than 1 presenters, the app will show the list of presenters, and when it's your turn to present, you can stop the current presenters and then share your screen.

[L33] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/14502391260435)

[L34] 

[L35] ### That's all!

[L36] 

[L37] You can also present from iOS, Android phone, or tablet to your screen. Click [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/14557706459283) for more info. You can download the **AeriCast Presenter** app from the app store.
