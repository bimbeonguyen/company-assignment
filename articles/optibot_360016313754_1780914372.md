[L1] # How to Install an APK file on your Fire TV

[L2] 

[L3] We strongly recommend to install the Fire TV app via Amazon App store, that way Amazon will automatically keep your app up to date with new releases.

[L4] 

[L5] **Steps to install APK on your Fire TV:**

[L6] 

[L7] 1) Go to your **Fire TV Stick’s settings page** located at the top of the home menu.

[L8] 

[L9] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012107941)

[L10] 

[L11] 2) Scroll right and choose **My Fire TV**

[L12] 

[L13] ![Fire_Stick-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012107921)

[L14] 

[L15] 3) Move down to **Developer Options**

[L16] 

[L17] **![Fire_Stick-3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011806502)**

[L18] 

[L19] 4) Set **Apps from Unknown Sources** to ON.

[L20] 

[L21] ![Fire_Stick-4_ADB.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015575461)

[L22] 

[L23] 5) **Accept the warning message** about installing external apps

[L24] 

[L25] ![Fire_Stick-5_ADB.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015577081)

[L26] 

[L27] 6) Next is to download and install OptiSigns' APK app.

[L28] 

[L29] * Open the **Amazon appstore** on your Fire TV.

[L30] * Search for [Downloader](https://www.amazon.com/AFTVnews-com-Downloader/dp/B01N0BP507) and install the program.

[L31] 

[L32] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015576341)

[L33] 

[L34] * **Open Downloader and type in the URL: <https://links.optisigns.com/fireos>**

[L35] * **Download the file through Downloader**. Once complete, you can install it by selecting “Files” within the interface.

[L36] * Installed apps will appear on your Fire Stick’s main page along with the rest of your software.

[L37]
