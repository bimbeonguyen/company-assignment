[L1] # How to use RTSP Streams on OptiSigns Devices

[L2] 

[L3] #### Nowadays, RTSP Camera Streaming is popular. You can display your RTSP Stream on your Digital Screen via OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] This article will guide you through how to create and use the RTSP Stream app on the OptiSigns devices. 

[L6] 

[L7] For displaying RTSP streams via Windows or Raspberry Pi, see our article [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/8066591235603).

[L8] 

[L9] ### **How to Use the RTSP App on an OptiSigns Device**

[L10] 

[L11] Go to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L12] 

[L13] Then go to **Files/Assets**, click on **App**, then search for and add the **RTSP Stream** app to your Account.

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26500916181267)

[L16] 

[L17] Enter your RTSP Stream app information:

[L18] 

[L19] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099265055251)

[L20] 

[L21] * Name: Name of your RTSP Stream app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L22] * URL: the URL link to the RTSP Stream link you want to display.

[L23] 

[L24] Here are some free public RTSP URLs:

[L25] 

[L26] ```

[L27] rtsp://wowzaec2demo.streamlock.net/vod/mp4:BigBuckBunny_115k.mp4

[L28] 

[L29] rtsp://demo:demo@ipvmdemo.dyndns.org:5541/onvif-media/media.amp?profile=profile_1_h264&sessiontimeout=60&streamtype=unicast

[L30] ```

[L31] Click Save to save your asset.

[L32] 

[L33] |  |

[L34] | --- |

[L35] | **NOTE** |

[L36] | Ensure your cameras are on the same network as your digital signage device. |

[L37] 

[L38] ## That's all!

[L39] 

[L40] Congratulations! You have created your RTSP Stream App. This can be assigned to your OptiSigns devices - as many as you like.

[L41] 

[L42] You can change the wall at any time by clicking on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L43] 

[L44] You can put the walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L45]
