[L1] # How To Connect To WiFi For Raspberry Pi

[L2] 

[L3] In this guide, we will walk you through end to end process to connect to WiFi on your Raspberry Pi.

[L4] 

[L5] If you haven't set up a Raspberry Pi OS on your device, you can check [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360034379693-How-to-install-OptiSigns-Player-on-Raspberry-Pi).

[L6] 

[L7] After you set up a Raspberry Pi device, it's common requirement to set up a WiFi connection.

[L8] 

[L9] Let's dive in.

[L10] 

[L11] **1. Optisign app shows "Internet Connection is required for first time"**

[L12] 

[L13] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092630194)

[L14] 

[L15] **2. Click the WiFi connection button.**

[L16] 

[L17] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092721134)

[L18] 

[L19] **3. Select your WiFi's name**

[L20] 

[L21] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092630794)

[L22] 

[L23] **4. Enter your Wifi's password**

[L24] 

[L25] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092720674)

[L26] 

[L27] **5.┬Ā Click OK**

[L28] 

[L29] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094946253)

[L30] 

[L31] **6. Take a few seconds to connect your WiFi**

[L32] 

[L33] You may need to restart the device for WiFi config to set in.

[L34] 

[L35] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094945513)

[L36] 

[L37] **7. OptiSigns app shows pair code on your screen**

[L38] 

[L39] ![RPi_Wifi_connection-7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092721474)

[L40] 

[L41] When your screen shows a paring code, your Raspberry Pi has an internet connection.┬Ā

[L42] 

[L43] You're ready to go.

[L44] 

[L45] Then you can enter this pairing code on your web portal. If you want to learn more, you can click [here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016374813-Set-up-add-a-screen).

[L46]
