[L1] # How to use Facebook Live Stream App

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you through how to create and use Facebook Live Stream app.

[L4] 

[L5] You can push a Facebook Live streams to all of your digital signs.

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360071919973)

[L10] 

[L11] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L12] 

[L13] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L14] 

[L15] First open the Facebook live stream you want to use and copy the URL link:

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360071920473)

[L18] 

[L19] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L20] 

[L21] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add Instagram on your Account.

[L22] 

[L23] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360070784894)

[L24] ¬†

[L25] 

[L26] Click Facebook Live:

[L27] 

[L28] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360070785074)

[L29] 

[L30] Enter your Facebook like URL:

[L31] 

[L32] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360070785814)

[L33] 

[L34] * Name: Name of your Facebook Live Stream, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L35] * Facebook Live Stream URL: Paste the URL of the Facebook live stream you want to use here.

[L36] * Mute:¬†If you want to play the video muted.

[L37] 

[L38] Click Save.  

[L39] After Saving, you can Preview the wall.  

[L40]   

[L41] 

[L42] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L43] 

[L44] You have created your Facebook Live Stream app.

[L45] 

[L46] You can assign it to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L47] 

[L48] ## **Note:**

[L49] 

[L50] Facebook Live Stream URL will become a video on demand once the stream is finished.  

[L51] So if the stream is already finished, the OptiSigns still get the video and plays it.  

[L52] If you are following a Facebook Live Stream channel, each new stream session is a new link, so you will have to copy, update the link every time.  

[L53] If you are producing live streams on Facebook, you can create, schedule the link ahead of time.  

[L54] More information on how to schedule live stream here:¬†<https://www.facebook.com/business/help/2087325741287572?id=1123223941353904>
