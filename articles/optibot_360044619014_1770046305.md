[L20] ## Setting Up a Facebook Counter App

[L21] 

[L22] On the OptiSigns portal, go to **Assets ‚Üí Add Asset ‚Üí Apps.**

[L23] 

[L24] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745309088915)

[L25] 

[L26] Select **Facebook Counter**:

[L27] 

[L28] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745324663315)

[L29] 

[L30] Login to Facebook. If this is the first time registering it with OptiSigns, you will need to hit **Connect Facebook Pages** then log in.¬†![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745324667155)

[L31] 

[L32] ¬†

[L33] The Facebook Counter App allows you to register numerous Facebook accounts. If you have already registered your account, you can choose it with the **Select** option.

[L34] ¬†

[L35] After logging on with Facebook in the Facebook popup window, select the Pages you want OptiSigns to access data to create the Counter.

[L36] ¬†![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360061959213)

[L37] 

[L38] Click **Next**, then Click **Done**.

[L39] ¬†

[L40] Whether you connect a new Facebook page or choose it from an existing on, you'll see the below screen:

[L41] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745309102483)

[L42] 

[L43] After that, you can enter information for your wall.* **Name**: Name of your Facebook Counter Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L44] * **Page**: Page you want to use (if you have more than 1).¬†**Important**: you can only set this one time during the creation of the asset, if you want to change this later, you should delete the old asset and create a new one.

[L45] * **Type**: There are two types: Like or Follower. You can select what do you want to show on your Facebook page.

[L46] * **Title**: This is the Title you want to give your Counter. It will be displayed above the Counter.  

[L47] This is an optional field.

[L48] * **Text**: This is a description text you want to provide the viewer. i.e. Use case may be to instruct viewer to 'Like' your page by scanning QR.

[L49] * **Theme**:¬†Select Light (Facebook blue background) or Dark Theme (black background).

[L50] * **Show QR Code**: When checked, allows you to add a QR code. You can choose for the QR code to go to the Facebook page, or to any other page by choosing **Custom**.

[L51] 

[L52] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745324675091)

[L53] 

[L54] Click Save to the QR code and the Facebook Counter app page.¬†Finally, hit **Save**. You've created a Facebook Counter asset.

[L55]

[L58] ## Deploying a Facebook Counter App

[L59] 

[L60] You can deploy your new Facebook Counter app as an individual asset, or as part of a [Split Screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

[L61] 

[L62] To get your new Facebook Counter asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

[L63] 

[L64] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745309108371)

[L65] 

[L66] This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

[L67] 

[L68] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745309110419)

[L69] 

[L70] Here, select **Asset** under Content type, then hit **Select Asset**.

[L71] 

[L72] Then, select your created Facebook Counter Asset:

[L73] 

[L74] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48745324685331)

[L75] 

[L76] Now hit **Save**. Your Facebook Counter asset will now display on screen.

[L77] 

[L78] You can also deploy it as part of a split screen, allowing you to show other assets at the same time. See how in our [Split Screen app article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App) It can also be displayed in a Playlist or Schedule.
