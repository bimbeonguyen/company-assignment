[L1] # How to use RSS feeds with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] RSS feeds is a popular way for organization to push out information. Whether you have a well-know RSS feeds like from the [New York Times](https://archive.nytimes.com/www.nytimes.com/services/xml/rss/index.html) or one from your local community or company, you can put the feed on big screens TV with OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] Viewer can scan the QR code to be taken to the news article.

[L6] 

[L7] ¬†

[L8] 

[L9] This article will guide you through how to create and use RSS app with OptiSigns.

[L10] 

[L11] Below are example screenshots of how it look on your screens, you can view a live example [here](https://social-player.optisigns.com/rss/?asset_id=5ZAhQubRWfCiNWSAZ):

[L18] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L19] 

[L20] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L21] 

[L22] Then log on to our portal:¬†[https://app.optisigns.com/](http://app.optisigns.com/)

[L23] 

[L24] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add RSS feeds on your Account.

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483190036115)

[L27] ¬†

[L28] 

[L29] Click RSS app:

[L30] 

[L31] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360049128674)

[L32] ¬†

[L33] 

[L34] Enter your RSS feed information:

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46077478790547)

[L37] 

[L38] * Name: Name of your RSS Feed app, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L39] * RSS Feed URL: an RSS feed format URL such as: <https://rss.nytimes.com/services/xml/rss/nyt/World.xml>   

[L40] More RSS feeds from the [New York Times here](https://archive.nytimes.com/www.nytimes.com/services/xml/rss/index.html).  

[L41] Or:¬†<http://rss.cnn.com/rss/cnn_topstories.rss>  

[L42] More RSS feeds from [CNN here](http://www.cnn.com/services/rss/).

[L43] * Speed: Select how fast you want the wall to scroll or switch between RSS feed items.

[L44] * Show QR code: Display the QR code for the RSS feed.

[L45] * Font Size: Set the font size for the RSS feed.

[L46] * Theme:¬†Select Light or Dark Theme. You can also customize your background color if you select Custom.

[L47] 

[L48] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483190054419)

[L49] 

[L50] You can click Advanced and set:

[L51] 

[L52] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46185093417619)

[L53] 

[L54] * Filter Posts:¬†By Time (i.e last 30 days) or by last XX number of posts.

[L55] * Date & Time: Select the format for your date.

[L56] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the RSS feed app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be. This can be Customized:

[L57] 

[L58] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/46077525864211)

[L59] 

[L60] * Publish Order: Choose which order you'd like to RSS feed to publish in, between Latest First and Oldest First.

[L61] * Continue Playing Content in Playlist: In the playlist, the RSS feed app plays 10 items in the 1st round, and it will start with #11 item in the 2nd round.

[L62] 

[L63] Click **Save**.  

[L64] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app to fetch the RSS items and prepare your feed, so please be patient.  

[L65]   

[L66] 

[L67] ## **That's all! Congratulations!**

[L68] 

[L69] You have created your RSS feed wall.  

[L70] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L71] 

[L72] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L73] 

[L74] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
