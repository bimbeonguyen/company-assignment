[L1] # Engage Plan - FAQs

[L2] 

[L3] **What is OptiSigns Engage Plan?**

[L4] 

[L5] Engage is a new suite of apps and features designed to help you turn screens into two-way communications. It enables the creation of engaging, impressive, and interactive connections with your customers.

[L6] 

[L7] Here are a few highlighted features in Engage:

[L8] 

[L9] * [Kiosk Designer Pro](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/15313086319763)

[L10] * [Content Library Kiosk](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/17604055961747)

[L11] * [Lift & Learn](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/13097501958291) (and other IoT Sensor experience)

[L12] * [QR Scan-to-Interact:](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/8899727608083) Turn static screens into an interactive experience, no additional sensor, HW needed

[L13] * [AI Camera:](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360058259834) Customize experience based on your viewer, collect data to understand your audience

[L14] * [Enterprise App](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/13320135306515)

[L15] * [Check-in & SMS follow up](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/23566927217939)

[L20]

[L29] **How much is it?**

[L30] 

[L31] Engage plan is $30/mo/screen. With an annual plan, that cost is reduced to $27/mo/screen.

[L32] 

[L33] ┬Ā

[L34] 

[L35] **Only some of my Screens need Engage features. Can I only buy Engage Subscriptions for them?**

[L36] 

[L37] Since Engage includes platform-wide features (QR Scan to Interact, Check-in & SMS, analytics data, etc.), it cannot be separately billed by screens. We suggest:

[L38] 

[L39] * If you have fewer than 25 screens that need to be on Engage, you can create a new account and manage only your Engage screens there.

[L40] * If you have more than 25 screens to be on Engage and need to mix-use them with your regular signage-only screens, you can [talk to our Sales team.](https://share.hsforms.com/1r9KTTxPFSYijx-DVR6I7nwca5m5)

[L41] 

[L42] **Does SMS messaging cost extra?**

[L43] 

[L44] 500 SMS per Engage screen subscription is included. That is enough for ~300 check-ins, depending on how your users respond.

[L45] 

[L46] Additional SMS are billed at $0.025 per message
