[L1] # External Storage on OptiSigns Devices

[L2] 

[L3] **NOTE:** In order to use External Storage, you must be a [Pro Plus subscriber or above](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing). This also only applies to ***OptiSigns Devices***, and will not appear on any devices not manufactured by OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] If you feel there is not enough storage on your device, you can use the USB drive or micro SD card as extra storage to add additional storage capacity to it.

[L6] 

[L7] After you plug in the USB drive or micro SD card on your device, go to the **Edit Screen** tab.

[L8] 

[L9] Click **Advanced**, then click **More:**  

[L10] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/20153104606995)

[L11] 

[L12] Check "**Use external Storage**" and select Auto Detect in the "**External Storage Path**".

[L13] 

[L14] After that, the OptiSigns app will use the external storage on this device.

[L15]
