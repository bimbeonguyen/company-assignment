[L1] # Resolve Autostart issues on your Android TV devices

[L2] 

[L3] Auto Start feature is not working on your devices. The devices give example: Chromecast with Android TV, Mi Stick, HiSense, Sony, and other TV with Android TV

[L4] 

[L5] This article guides you on how to resolve the Auto Start feature on your Android device.

[L6] 

[L7] ### **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L8] 

[L9] Go to Settings > Apps > Special app access > Display over other apps, make sure the OptiSigns app is allowed.

[L10] 

[L11] 1. ) Go to **Setting**

[L12] 

[L13] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015378922)

[L14] 

[L15] 2. Select **Apps**

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015697201)

[L18] 

[L19] 3. Go to **Special app access**

[L20] 

[L21] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015697221)

[L22] 

[L23] 4) Select **Display over other apps**

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015697241)

[L26] 

[L27] 5. Make sure the OptiSigns app is **ON**

[L28] 

[L29] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500015697261)

[L30] 

[L31] ### **That's it.**

[L32] 

[L33] Your Auto Start feature will be worked.
