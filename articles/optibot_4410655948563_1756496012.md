[L1] # How to use livestream.com app

[L2] 

[L3] You can broadcast your live events with livestream.com to your OptiSigns digital signs screens.

[L4] 

[L5] With livestream.com you can even create your event ahead of time, then schedule it to play with OptiSigns.

[L6] 

[L7] ¬†

[L8] 

[L9] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L10] 

[L11] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L12] 

[L13] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L14] 

[L15] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add Instagram on your Account.

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410640446867)

[L18] ¬†

[L19] 

[L20] Click search for and click Livestream.com

[L21] 

[L22] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410649648403)

[L23] 

[L24] Give your app a name, this is just for organizing your assets, it will not show up on your screens.

[L25] 

[L26] And the URL of your livestream.com event.

[L27] 

[L28] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410640465171)

[L29] 

[L30] To get URL of your livestream.com event go to your event.

[L31] 

[L32] Click the Share button

[L33] 

[L34] If you need some live streams to test, you can pick a public one here: <https://livestream.com/watch>

[L35] 

[L36] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410649703059)

[L37] 

[L38] Click Copy to copy the link

[L39] 

[L40] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410649710355)

[L41] 

[L42] Then paste in the URL field of the Livestream.com app in OptiSigns.

[L43] 

[L44] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4410649717523)

[L45] 

[L46] Click Save.

[L47] 

[L48] ¬†

[L49] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L50] 

[L51] You have created your Livestream.com app.

[L52] 

[L53] After Saving, if the event is already live, you can just assign it to your screens to start playing.

[L54] If your event is scheduled, you can use our schedule feature to schedule the event to go live on your screens.

[L55] If you have any additional questions, concerns or any feedback about OptiSigns, feel free to reach out to our support team at¬†[support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com)
