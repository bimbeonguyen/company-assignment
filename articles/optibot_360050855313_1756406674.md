[L1] # How to link Instagram Business or Creator Account to Facebook Account

[L2] 

[L3] |  |

[L4] | --- |

[L5] | **NOTE** |

[L6] | In order to use Instagram on OptiSigns, you cannot use any Personal Accounts. They must be Business Accounts. This is a policy of Meta. |

[L7] 

[L8] Having an Instagram Business or Creator come with many advantages including ability to use hashtag or view other business profile.

[L9] 

[L10] To use Instagram Business or Creator account with apps like OptiSigns, you will need link your Instagram account to a [Facebook Business Page](https://www.facebook.com/business/pages/set-up).

[L11] 

[L12] When you [created your Instagram Business/Creator account](https://help.instagram.com/502981923235522?helpref=hc_fnav). It should have asked you to connect to existing Facebook Business Page or create one. You probably reading this guide because you have skipped that step.

[L13] 

[L14] Here's how to link your Instagram account to Facebook Business Page:

[L15] 

[L16] Use your phone, make sure you have both Instagram and Facebook apps installed, and log in to the correct accounts.

[L17] 

[L18] Go to your Instagram app on your phone. Open Setting

[L19] 

[L20] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076156914)

[L21] 

[L22] Then tap "Business":

[L23] 

[L24] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360077304173)

[L25] 

[L26] Then tap "Connect to a Facebook Page":

[L27] 

[L28] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360077304273)

[L29] 

[L30] Then select Creating a new one or connecting to your existing one.

[L31] 

[L32] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076162134)

[L33] 

[L34] After this, it will open Facebook app asking for your permission to link the 2 apps and will take care of the rest for you.

[L35] 

[L36] Still need help? You can refer to official Instagram & Facebook guide here.  

[L37] <https://help.instagram.com/570895513091465>
