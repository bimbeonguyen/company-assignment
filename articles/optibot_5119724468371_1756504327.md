[L1] # How to use Notion App

[L2] 

[L3] To put Notion Note on your Digital Signs.  

[L4] Go to Notion.com on your browser, open the note.  

[L5] Click Share and Copy Share to web URL.

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/5174799770003)

[L8] 

[L9] Go to [app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/).

[L10] 

[L11] Click Files/Assets, then click Apps

[L12] 

[L13] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483573742739)

[L14] 

[L15] Search and click the Notion app

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/5174763924115)

[L18] 

[L19] Fill out:

[L20] 

[L21] * Name: name of your Notion App, this is only for use in File/Asset list, it will not be displayed on your screen.

[L22] * URL: Share to web URL of your Notion Note that you have copied.

[L23] * Update interval: default is 10 minutes. This means the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes in your note.

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/5174823736851)

[L26] 

[L27] Click Save to save your asset.

[L28] 

[L29] ## That's all!

[L30] 

[L31] Congratulation! You have created your Notion App.

[L32] 

[L33] You can change the app any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L34] 

[L35] You can assign the newly created app to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L36] 

[L37] You can put the apps in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L38]
