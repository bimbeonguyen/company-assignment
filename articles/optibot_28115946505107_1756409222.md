[L1] # How to Change Close & Nav Bar Styler Preferences

[L2] 

[L3] When you change your Close & Nav Bar Styler Preference settings, it will adjust the default options set for your Kiosk Lite and Kiosk Designer Pro.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] While you can modify the settings individually for specific apps, setting a default will help maintain consistency, ensuring your preferences remain unchanged unless manually adjusted.

[L8] 

[L9]  

[L10] 

[L11] You can adjust these settings by:

[L12] 

[L13] * First, click on your profile name in the top right corner of your OptiSigns webpage.

[L14] * Then, click on the "Preferences" tab in the drop-down menu.

[L15] 

[L16] ![chrome_KqTF9UeA8h.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28115918025875)

[L17] 

[L18]  

[L19] 

[L20] * You will then be directed to the Preferences page. To access the Close & Nav Bar settings, scroll down to the bottom of the page.

[L21] 

[L22]  

[L23] 

[L24] ![chrome_6gpZoPUEQG.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28115918027155)

[L25] 

[L26] **Close & Nav Bar Styler:**

[L27] 

[L28] * + **Default Icon Position**: Adjust the placement of icons within the bar to optimize visibility and access: none, top left, top center, top right, bottom left, bottom center, bottom right

[L29] 	+ **Default Icon Style:** Select from a variety of icon designs to match your kiosk's aesthetic: Default, Outline, Solid, or Thin

[L30] 	+ **Default Icon Color**: Customize the color of your icons to complement your brand or theme.

[L31] 	+ **Default Icon Size**: Scale the icons to the ideal size for easy recognition and usability.

[L32] 	+ **Default Background Color:** You can customize the color for the navigation bar background to align with your design scheme.

[L33] 	+ **Default Opacity:** If Show Back/Forward is turned on, you can adjust the transparency of the navigation bar for subtle or bold visibility.

[L34] 

[L35] Click Save.

[L36] 

[L37]  

[L38] 

[L39] ## **That's all!**

[L40] 

[L41]  

[L42] 

[L43] You can change these default settings at any time or adjust the settings manually within the kiosk apps.

[L44]
