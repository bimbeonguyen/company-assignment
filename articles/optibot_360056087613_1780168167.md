[L1] # How to Clear Cache on Your Fire Stick device

[L2] 

[L3] When the OptiSigns stops working correctly, one of the most common causes is corrupted data. In some cases, OptiSigns may be slow down and doesn't perform well due to a cache that has become too large. In these cases, the solution is to clear the cache.

[L4] 

[L5] Let's begin,

[L6] 

[L7] ## **How to clear the cache on your Fire Stick device**

[L8] 

[L9] To clean the cache on your Fire Stick, go to Settings > Applications > Manage Installed Applications. Then select OptiSigns app, and select Clear cache. 

[L10] 

[L11] 1. From your Fire Stick homepage go to**"Settings"**

[L12] 

[L13] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012113701)

[L14] 

[L15] 2. Scroll through and click **"Applications"**

[L16] 

[L17] ![Fire_Stick-17_Application.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012113941)

[L18] 

[L19] 3. Then click **"Manage Installed Applications"**

[L20] 

[L21] ![Fire_Stick-18_Management.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012113981)

[L22] 

[L23] 4. Select **OptiSigns** app that you want to clear cache

[L24] 

[L25] ![Fire_Stick-19_Management_OptiSigns.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011812742)

[L26] 

[L27] 5. Then scroll down and click **"Clear cache"**

[L28] 

[L29] ![Fire_Stick-21_clear_cache.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011812702)

[L30] 

[L31] Note: If OptiSigns app still doesn't work correctly, repeat this process, and select **Clear data**.

[L32] 

[L33] ![Fire_Stick-20_clean_data.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012114021)

[L34] 

[L35] After that, OptiSigns app should work correctly on your Fire Stick. If it still doesn't work correctly after clearing the cache in the OptiSigns app, you can perform a **"**[Factory Reset](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360054298754-How-to-Factory-Reset-Your-Fire-Stick-device-)**"** to clear all of the data that's stored on your device.

[L36]
