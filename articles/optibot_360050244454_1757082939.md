[L1] # How to use Trello App

[L2] 

[L3] To put Trello Board on your Digital Signs.  

[L4] Got to Trello.com on your browser, open the board,  

[L5] Copy the board's URL Link from the address bar.

[L6] 

[L7] Go to [app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com).

[L8] 

[L9] Click Files/Assets, then click Apps

[L10] 

[L11] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360077797693)

[L12] 

[L13] Then click Trello app

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076630234)

[L16] 

[L17] Paste the Trello Board URL in.

[L18] 

[L19] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076630254)

[L20] 

[L21] On your device, if your Trello Board is private, you will need to enter in username and password to log in.  

[L22] You only need to do it once and set remember credential.  

[L23] If you are using FireStick, you can connect up a Bluetooth keyboard, mouse to enter the User name and password. Here is a related [article](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360037391854-How-to-display-content-inside-websites-that-require-login-).
