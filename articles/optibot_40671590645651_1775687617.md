[L68] ## Setting Up an OptiSound Add-On

[L69] 

[L70] To subscribe to OptiSound, go to the **Subscription** **Plan** option in the main menu. Scroll down until you see **OptiSound**.

[L71] 

[L72] You'll have the Option to begin a 14-Day Free Trial. This will default to the Standard plan. To update to the **Pro Plan**, click **Update Licenses**.

[L73] 

[L74] ![optisound add on update license](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50666262099475)

[L75] 

[L76] You'll have the option to choose your plan. For Premium music, choose the **Pro** plan, then hit **Update**.

[L77] 

[L78] ![optisound add-on plan options](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50666262101523)

[L79] 

[L80] When you've chosen the Pro Plan, it will be visible next to the Add-On menu.

[L81] 

[L82] ![optisound add-on pro plan icon](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50666262102035)

[L83] 

[L84] In order to assign it to a screen, you can assign the add-on by hitting the **Add Screen** button here:

[L85] 

[L86] ![optisound add-on add screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50666271206675)

[L87] 

[L88] You can also assign OptiSound to a screen directly - see below.

[L89] 

[L90] ### Browse and Assign Music Stations

[L91] 

[L92] To browse and assign stations, go to the **Screens** tab.  

[L93] ![firefox_I20eGnlmsp.jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/40674055498259)

[L94] 

[L95] Select the screen where you would like to assign music. This could be a screen you've already activated, or not. If not, click **Activate Background Music**.

[L96] 

[L97] ![optisound background music activation](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/40674236429203)  

[L98] This will use up an Add-On license.

[L99] 

[L100] Next, click **Change** to browse through the list of available stations.

[L101] 

[L102] ![optisound background music change station](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/40674236430739)

[L103] 

[L104] Select the station that best fits your business environment. Premium music stations are only available on a Pro plan.

[L105] 

[L106] ![optisound premium stations](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/50666271194643)  

[L107] Ensure the **Audio Source Priority** is set to **Background Music** in the screen settings. Setting the audio priority ensures your background music keeps playing, even when showing muted videos or other content.

[L108] 

[L109] ![optisound priority audio](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/40674172244371)  

[L110] Finish assigning any additional content to the screen as needed. Then click **Save** ŌĆö the selected music station will begin playing immediately.

[L112] ### Connect Your Device to Your Audio System

[L113] 

[L114] To play the music through your businessŌĆÖs sound system you can either:

[L115]

[L117] * Pair the device via **Bluetooth** to your speaker setup.

[L118] 

[L119] Double-check your deviceŌĆÖs audio output settings to make sure it matches your connection type.
