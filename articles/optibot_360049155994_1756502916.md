[L1] # How to use Market Index App

[L2] 

[L3] Beside the [Stock Apps](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360044115513), where you can select a list of your own Stock Tickers, you can use Marked Index app for quicker, more general market prices update. You can add one of these app:

[L4] 

[L5] * World Market Index: Display Index for US, UK, Japan, China, Hong Hong indices

[L6] * US Market Index: S&P 500, Dow Jones industrial Average and NASDAQ indices

[L7] * Market Index: select from list of other indices around the world

[L8] 

[L9] Data is updated every 15 min.

[L10]
