[L1] # How to use Microsoft Teams Meeting app with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] You can broadcast a Microsoft Teams meeting on your digital signs with OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] It's simple, create (or schedule a MS Teams meeting).

[L6] 

[L7] In the Meeting Options:

[L8] 

[L9] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404053182483)

[L10] 

[L11] Make sure you set "Who can bypass the lobby" to "Everyone".

[L12] 

[L13] Otherwise OptiSigns app will stuck at the lobby.

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404053190163)

[L16] 

[L17] After that, copy your MS Team's URL.

[L18] 

[L19] There are many ways you can copy the MS Teams Meeting URL. Here' one:  

[L20] Click Participants, then click Share Invite. Then click Copy Link

[L21] 

[L22] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404053323411)

[L23] 

[L24] ┬Ā

[L25] 

[L26] Log in to OptiSigns:  

[L27] <https://app.optisigns.com/app/assetManagement>

[L28] 

[L29] Click Files/Assets -> Apps -> and select Microsoft Teams Meetings

[L30] 

[L31] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404053219347)

[L32] 

[L33] Give your MS Teams Meeting app a name and paste the URL in there:

[L34] 

[L35] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404053339411)

[L36] 

[L37] That's it!

[L38] 

[L39] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404045864339)

[L40] 

[L41] Now you can assign this MS Teams Meeting app to your screens, or put it on a schedule so the screens will join automatically when the meeting start.

[L42] 

[L43] The screens will join the meeting as Guests with names are the name of the screens.

[L44] 

[L45] This can also be done with Teams Town hall. To enable Town hall, simply hit **Share Event**, then copy the **Join** **link** URL and paste this into OptiSigns.

[L46] 

[L47] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38297330621459)

[L48] 

[L49] **Tips:**

[L50] 

[L51] Have the host start the meeting a few mins before screens are joining automatically.  

[L52] It will take a device 10-15s to join the meeting, so it's not a good idea to put the Teams Meeting in a playlist. It's better to use it as single asset to take up full screen or be part of a [SplitScreen zone](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573).
