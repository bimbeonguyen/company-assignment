[L1] # How to use Matterport App

[L2] 

[L3] Matterport is a very cool way to visualize, show case your facilities.

[L4] 

[L5] You can also share it on Digital Signage screens with OptiSigns.

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6175547898131)

[L8] 

[L9] To put Matterport on your Digital Signs:

[L10] 

[L11] Go to [app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/).

[L12] 

[L13] Click Files/Assets, then click Apps

[L14] 

[L15] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483527349267)

[L16] 

[L17] Search and click the Matterport app

[L18] 

[L19] ![blobid0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6091365149971)

[L20] 

[L21] Fill out:

[L22] 

[L23] * Name: name of your Matterport App, this is only for use in File/Asset list, it will not be displayed on your screen.

[L24] * URL:Your Matterport URL (e.x: <https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=tyEb2t1RWsc>)

[L25] 

[L26] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6175585009811)

[L27] 

[L28] Note that Matterport is very heavy app, it may overload older FireStick devices. We recommend FireStick 4K max, our Android Stick device, or Windows player for best performance.

[L29] 

[L30] Click Save to save your asset.

[L31] 

[L32] ## That's all!

[L33] 

[L34] Congratulation! You have created your Matterport App.

[L35] 

[L36] You can change the app any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L37] 

[L38] You can assign the newly created app to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L39] 

[L40] You can put the apps in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L41]
