[L1] # Synchronized playback (Sync Play) feature

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns you can enable screens to play in-sync. This means:

[L4] 

[L5] * If content published screens are **the same**, all screens will play the content at the same time.

[L6] * If content published screens are **different**, it will start playing them from the beginning

[L7] 

[L8] Supported on: Android, Fire TV, Windows, Linux, MacOS, ChromeOS, Webplayer

[L9] 

[L10] Note:

[L11] 

[L12] * When Sync Play enabled, playlists may not start from the beginning (because they are synced).

[L13] * When Sync Play is enabled, subplaylist control features will no longer apply.

[L14] * Devices do not need to be on the same network, nor there's any special configuration needed on devices.

[L15] * Devices time need to be updated, and devices needs to be in the same time zone.

[L16] * Android, Fire TV requires updates to 5.13.7 or newer.

[L17] * Windows, Linux, MacOS, ChromeOS, and Webplayer don't have the timer server setting yet.

[L18] 

[L19] To use the Sync Play feature, just select the screens you want to enable this feature for.

[L20] 

[L21] ### 1. Click **Edit** -> **Advanced** -> check **Sync Play**

[L22]

[L25] ### 2. Open the side menu of the OptiSigns app on your screen. Go to the Sync Play Timeserver.

[L26] 

[L27] #### 

[L28]
