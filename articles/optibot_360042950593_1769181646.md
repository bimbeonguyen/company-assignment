[L32] ## Setting Up a Simple Clock App

[L33] 

[L34] On the OptiSigns portal, go to **Assets ŌåÆ Add Asset ŌåÆ Apps**.

[L35] 

[L36] ![open apps tab optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414193939)

[L37] 

[L38] Select **Simple Clock:**

[L39] 

[L40] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410410834707)

[L41] 

[L42] Set up your Simple Clock app:

[L43] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414212499)

[L44] 

[L45] * **Name -** Name of your assets. This is for organizational purposes and will not be displayed on your screens.

[L46] * **Clock Type -** Choose your type of clock. Pick the one that you like best! There are six options:

[L47] 	+ **Classic Digital**

[L48] 	+ **Modern Digital**

[L49] 	+ **Classic Analog**

[L50] 	+ **Modern Analog**

[L51] 	+ **Minimalist**

[L52] 	+ **Kinetic**

[L53] * **Theme -** Choose between **Light** and **Dark** theme, or set it to Automatically shift between them depending on the time displayed.

[L54] * **Landscape / Portrait -** Switch the Preview between Landscape and Portrait.

[L55] 

[L56] ### Theme and Clock Settings

[L57] 

[L58] The actual Theme and Clock Settings will vary depending on whether your clock display is digital or analog.

[L59] 

[L60] #### Digital Clocks

[L61] 

[L62] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414214803)

[L63] 

[L64] * **Time Format -** How you want the time to be formatted on your digital clock.

[L65] 	+ **Show Seconds -**Choose whether or not to show seconds.

[L66] 	+ **Show Date -**Choose whether or not to display the date.

[L67] * **Date Format -**If "Show Date" is checked, you can customize the way the date is formatted here.

[L68] 

[L69] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410410853651)

[L70] * **Time Font Size -** Choose the font size for the time display.

[L71] * **Date Font Size -** Choose the font size for the date. This option only appears if "Show Date" is checked above.

[L72] * **Text Color -** Choose the Text color for the digital clock.

[L73] * **Background Color -** Choose the Background color for the display.

[L74] 

[L75] |  |

[L76] | --- |

[L77] | **NOTE** |

[L78] | Using Modern Digital, the Presets are Neon Cyberpunk and Gaming RGB. The Background colors of these Presets cannot have their gradi. |

[L79] 

[L80] #### Analog Clocks

[L81] 

[L82] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414219795)

[L83] 

[L84] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410410862739)* **Show Numbers -** Choose whether or not to show numbers on the analog clock display.

[L85] * **Show Tick Marks -** Choose whether or not to show tick marks on the analog clock display.

[L86] * **Dial Color** - Change the dial color on the analog clock display.

[L87] * **Hour Hand Color -** Change the color of the hour hand on the analog clock display.

[L88] * **Minute Hand Color -** Change the color of the minute hand on the analog clock display.

[L89] * **Second Hand Color -** Change the color of the second hand on the analog clock display.

[L90] * **Clock Face Background -** Change the background color of the clock face.

[L91] * **Background Color -** Change the background color of the entire display outside the clock face.

[L92] 

[L93] ![color picker optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414226067)

[L94] 

[L95] Once you've configured the app the way you want, hit **Save**.

[L99] ## Deploying a Simple Clock App

[L100] 

[L101] You can deploy your new Simple Clock app as an individual asset, or as part of a [Split Screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

[L102] 

[L103] To get your new Simple Clock asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

[L104] 

[L105] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410410870547)

[L106] 

[L107] This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

[L108] 

[L109] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414232467)

[L110] 

[L111] Here, select **Asset** under Content type, then hit **Change** next to Selected Asset.

[L112] 

[L113] Then, select your created Simple Clock Asset:

[L114] 

[L115] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48410414234003)

[L116] 

[L117] Now hit **Save**. Your Simple Clock asset will now display on screen.

[L118] 

[L119] You can also deploy it as part of a split screen, allowing you to show other assets at the same time. See how in our [Split Screen app article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App) It can also be displayed in a Playlist or Schedule.
