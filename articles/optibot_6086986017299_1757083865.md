[L1] # How to use OptiSigns Screen Capture app

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns Screen Capture App, you can capture a screenshot on your Windows PC or Mac and send it to the Digital Signs screen.

[L4] 

[L5] This is particularly useful if you have secured Dashboards, Enterprise applications running on Windows, Java, etc. that you want to share on your Digital Signage screens.

[L6] 

[L7] Note: Pro Plus or Enterprise Plan is required.

[L8] 

[L9] ### Here's high-level steps:

[L10] 

[L11] 1. Create a Screen Capture asset in your account

[L12] 2. Download the OptiSigns Screen Capture app on your PC & set it up ([Windows](https://links.optisigns.com/screen-capture-win) or [MacOS](https://links.optisigns.com/screen-capture-mac))

[L13] 3. Assign it to your screen

[L14] 

[L15] ### **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L16] 

[L17] ### 1. Create a Screen Capture asset

[L18] 

[L19] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L20] 

[L21] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L22] 

[L23] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App", search for, and add Screen Capture to your Account.

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6086697409683)

[L26] 

[L27] Enter your Screen Capture app information:

[L28] 

[L29] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6086733185811)

[L30] 

[L31] * Name: Name of your Screen Capture app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L32] * Snapshot Type: Image (Screenshot).

[L33] * Snapshot Interval: in minutes, how often the Screen Capture app should take screenshots of your desktop. The default is 60min.

[L34] * Snapshot resolution: Select the resolution for your snapshot.

[L35] 

[L36] ### 2. Download the OptiSigns Screen Capture app on your PC

[L37] 

[L38] Download & install the OptiSigns Screen Capture app.

[L39] 

[L40] * [Windows version](https://links.optisigns.com/screen-capture-win)

[L41] * [Mac OS version](https://links.optisigns.com/screen-capture-mac)

[L42] 

[L43] Then install & open it, then log in with your OptiSigns account.

[L44] 

[L45] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6086795591699)

[L46] 

[L47] Then you can select your target Screen Capture asset that you created on the portal above.  

[L48] This way the Screen Capture app knows where to upload the screenshots too.

[L49] 

[L50] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6086892380819)

[L51] 

[L52] Then it will take a screenshot right away on your PC.

[L53] 

[L54] You can click the Take Screenshot Now button anytime to immediately take & upload a screenshot.

[L55] 

[L56] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/6086913245459)

[L57] 

[L58] ### 3. Assign it to your screen

[L59] 

[L60] You can assign the Screen Capture app anywhere like any other asset, image, or video:

[L61] 

[L62] * Directly to a screen as an asset

[L63] * In your playlist

[L64] * In your schedule

[L65] * In a SplitScreen ScreenZone

[L66] 

[L67] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L68] 

[L69] You have created your Screen Capture app.  

[L70] You can change the wall at any time by clicking on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†
