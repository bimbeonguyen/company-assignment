[L1] # Cannot find the OptiSigns app on Your Samsung Smart TV

[L2] 

[L3] If you've encountered an issue with the OptiSigns application not being available in your Samsung Smart TV's default region, you can easily change the region to access the application.

[L4] 

[L5] Here's how to do it:

[L6] 

[L7] **Step 1:** Power on your Samsung Smart TV and press the Menu button on your remote control.

[L8] 

[L9] **Step 2:** Using the arrow buttons on your remote, navigate to the Settings option and press Enter.

[L10] 

[L11] **Step 3:** Scroll down to the System option and select it.

[L12] 

[L13] **Step 4:** Scroll down to the Language & Time option and select it.

[L14] 

[L15] **Step 5:** Select the Time Zone option and choose the region you want to change to.

[L16] 

[L17] **Step 6:** If the desired region is not listed, you may need to change the country in the TV's settings. Navigate back to the Language & Time menu and select the Country option.

[L18] 

[L19] **Step 7:** Select the country you want to change to and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the change.

[L20] 

[L21] Note: Altering the region or country on your Samsung Smart TV may affect certain features or apps that are available on the device.

[L22] 

[L23] After changing the region or country, you may need to restart your Samsung Smart TV for the modifications to take effect.

[L24] 

[L25] We hope this guide assists you in changing the region on your Samsung Smart TV. If you require any further assistance or have additional questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team for help.
