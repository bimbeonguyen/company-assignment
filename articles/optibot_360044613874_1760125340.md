[L1] # How to use the Guardian App

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain how to use the Guardian News app on OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040991534#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Creating a The Guardian News Asset](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040991534#Creating)

[L7] 

[L8] The OptiSigns The Guardian app allows you to display news from The Guardian on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Top Stories, World news, Football, Sport, Culture, Lifestyle news and more.

[L9] 

[L10] QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. The Guardian News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)

[L18] ## What You'll Need

[L19] 

[L20] * An [**OptiSigns-capable device**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021855653-What-hardware-and-devices-are-supported)

[L21] * An OptiSigns [**Free plan or higher**](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)**\***

[L22] * A screen, [**set up and paired**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/18823504383891-OptiSigns-Getting-Started-Guide)

[L23] 

[L24] **\*** If using a Free plan, make sure you're using a [Free-plan capable device](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/33940834613139-What-Do-I-Get-With-an-OptiSigns-Free-Plan)

[L28] ## Creating a The Guardian News Asset

[L29] 

[L30] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L31] 

[L32] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L33] 

[L34] Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add┬Ā**The Guardian**┬Āon your Account.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516845734035)

[L37] 

[L38] ┬Ā

[L39] 

[L40] Click **The Guardian**:

[L41] 

[L42] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516868514707)

[L43] 

[L44] Enter your The Guardian Wall App information:

[L45] 

[L46] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516845740947)

[L47] 

[L48] * Name: Name of your The Guardian Wall app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L49] * Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.

[L50] * Theme: Select Light or Dark Theme.

[L51] * Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[L52] 

[L53] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516868519059)

[L54] 

[L55] * Font Size: Set the font size for the The Guardian Wall app.

[L56] 

[L57] Clicking┬Ā**Advanced**opens up additional options:

[L58] 

[L59] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516845747475)

[L60] 

[L61] * Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:  

[L62] ┬Ā

[L63] 

[L64] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516845748243)

[L65] 

[L66] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the The Guardian News Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

[L67] 

[L68]   

[L69] After you've configured the Guardian News app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your Guardian app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.
