[L1] # The OptiSigns app keeps turning on the TV screen

[L2] 

[L3] If the OptiSigns app keeps turning on your TV screen, you can follow this article.

[L4] 

[L5]   

[L6] 1. Open the side menu of the OptiSigns app.

[L7] 

[L8] * If using a FireStick Remote, press the three-bar button on your remote to open the side menu.

[L9] * If using an Android TV remote, press the left key on your D-Pad to open the side menu.

[L10] 

[L11] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/15808346193939)

[L12] 

[L13] 2. Navigate to "**Keep Awake**" in the side menu of the OptiSigns app.

[L14] 

[L15] 3. Switch it off.  

[L16]   

[L17] Then when you power off your TV screen, the OptiSigns app will not turn on your TV screen.
