[L1] # How to Lockdown iPad/Single App with Guided Accessibility

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you on how to activate the kiosk mode on your iPad for a specific application.

[L4] 

[L5] This can be particularly beneficial if you intend to use the OptiSigns software as a kiosk device, ensuring that the OptiSigns application remains open and cannot be exited without authorization.

[L6] 

[L7] Here is a general video on limiting an iPhone or iPad to one app with Guided Access:

[L8] 

[L9] ## Lockdown app on iPad Settings

[L10] 

[L11] Go to **Settings**, select **Accessibility**, then **Guided Access**

[L12] 

[L13] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273043474451)

[L14] 

[L15] Tap **Enable Guided Access**, then tap **Passcode Settings**

[L16] 

[L17] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273043477523)

[L18] 

[L19] Tap **Set Guided Access Passcode**. Enter a passcode, then re-enter it

[L20] 

[L21] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273090484499)

[L22] 

[L23] Open the app you want to lockdown: **OptiSigns App**

[L24] 

[L25] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273043479827)

[L26] 

[L27] Triple-click the **Power** (If there is no home button) or **Home** button to start lockdown  

[L28] note: Please make sure the orientation is in portrait mode if no home button exists

[L29] 

[L30] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273090486675)

[L31] 

[L32] To end **Guided Access**, triple-click the **Power** button or **Home** button enter your passcode, and tap **End:**

[L33] 

[L34] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/25273090489747)

[L35] 

[L36] That's it! Now, the OptiSigns app will be locked on your screen.

[L37]
