[L1] # How to get the MAC Address on your media player

[L2] 

[L3] If your IT team is looking for the MAC address on your media player, you can follow this article to get the MAC Address on your devices.

[L4] 

[L5] In this article, we will guide you on how to get the MAC Address on these devices.

[L6] 

[L7] * OptiSigns Android Stick

[L8] * Fire Stick

[L9] * Raspberry Pi

[L10] * Windows

[L11] 

[L12] ### 1) How to get the MAC Address on the OptiSigns Android Stick

[L13] 

[L14] 1. Go to the Android Home Page > Settings

[L15] 

[L16] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099392313107)

[L17] 

[L18] 2. Click "Network & Internet"

[L19] 

[L20] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099440965779)

[L21] 

[L22] 3. Select "your wifi SSID name"

[L23] 

[L24] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099446318099)

[L25] 

[L26] 4. You will see your device's MAC Address.

[L27] 

[L28] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099481961235)

[L29] 

[L30] ### 2) How to get the MAC Address on the Fire Stick

[L31] 

[L32] 1. Go to the Fire Stick Home Page > Settings > My Fire TV

[L33] 

[L34] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099583974803)

[L35] 

[L36] 2. Click "About"

[L37] 

[L38] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099585346451)

[L39] 

[L40] 3. Then go to "Network", you will see the MAC Address

[L41] 

[L42] ![mceclip8.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099602869139)

[L43] 

[L44] ### 3) How to get the MAC Address on the Raspberry Pi

[L45] 

[L46] 1. Open the "**terminal**"

[L47] 

[L48] ![mceclip12.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099797282067)

[L49] 

[L50] 2. Type the

[L51] 

[L52] ```

[L53] *ifconfig*

[L54] ```

[L55] 

[L56] 3. The result will be like this, check section eth0 if you are using wired connection. Check section wlan0 if you are using WiFi connection.

[L57] 

[L58] ![mceclip11.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/8099782797587)

[L59] 

[L60] The MAC address is visible after the **ÔÇťetherÔÇŁ** keyword.
