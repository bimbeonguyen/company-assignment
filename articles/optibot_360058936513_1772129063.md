[L1] # Advanced: Proof of Play (or Playback report)

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns you can enable tracking of playback of your contents.

[L4] 

[L5] When Proof-of-play Report is enabled, the screen will report the starting and ending timestamp of each asset it played.

[L6] 

[L7] You can also include Assets Tags and Screens Tags that will allow you to create complex aggregated reports.

[L8] 

[L9] |  |

[L10] | --- |

[L11] | This feature is available for **Pro-Plus** or above subscription plans.  |

[L12] 

[L13] **Feature summary:**

[L14] 

[L15] * Track starting, ending timestamp of each assets played

[L16] * Data can be collected real-time or by upload interval (useful for cases where you have devices with limited internet, and only do a "drive-by" once a week/month to collect data)

[L17] * Slide & dice report in your admin portal: <https://app.optisigns.com/app/playbackReport>

[L18] * Reports can be filtered, saved and scheduled to email to any email addresses

[L19] * Raw data can be exported for your custom reporting

[L35] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L36] 

[L37] **1. Setup: Enable Proof of Play feature on your account:**

[L38] 

[L39] You need to be on our Pro Plus or Enterprise subscription plans to have any of these options.

[L40] 

[L41] Once you are on Pro Plus or Enterprise plan:

[L42] 

[L43] Go **Account Settings** -> **Proof of Play**

[L44] 

[L45] And check "Enable Playback Reports"

[L46] 

[L47] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360099191493)

[L48] 

[L49] By default, screens will update data in real-time as assets are played.  

[L50] If internet is disconnected, data will be saved locally and will be synced to server automatically when the device come back online.

[L51] 

[L52] If for some reason, you don't want real-time data (i.e. to reduce network usage during day time, or if you do "drive-by" data collection once a while). You can unchecked Real-time option and select an upload interval of your choice.

[L53] 

[L54] Once enabled, within a few minutes, all of your screens will start sending back proof-of-play data.

[L55] 

[L56] You can now go to Analytics tab to view the report.

[L57] 

[L58]  

[L59] 

[L60] **2. Setup: (Optional) Setting Tags to your Screens, Assets**

[L61] 

[L62] This step is optional, but it's usually useful for you to set tags for your screens and assets it will make reporting, grouping data later on easier.

[L63] 

[L64] * To set Tags for Screens, just go to Screens tab, Edit your screens and type in Tags. [Read this article for more details.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051746974)

[L65] * To set Tags for Assets, just go to Files/Assets tab, select the Assets and click the tags button, then type in your tags. [Read this article for more details.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360056397634)

[L66] 

[L67] You can changes, update your tags any time and use them right away in report, data export.  

[L68] If you change, update tags; data, report that already exported will not have the changes, but you can re-run the report, data export to include the new tagging changes.

[L128] ## **Summary:**

[L129] 

[L130] OptiSigns' Proof of play or Playback Report give you real-time insight into playback of your assets on screens. You have the flexibility of slice & dice the data in your the Portal or schedule raw data export for your own analysis.

[L131] 

[L132] You can also schedule report to be automatically sent to your emails.
