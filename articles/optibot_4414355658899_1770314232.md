[L20] ## Option 1: Upload a PowerPoint File

[L21] 

[L22] ***We highly recommend this method!***

[L23] 

[L24] Depending on how you download your PowerPoint, this option can be useful if you want your videos, animations or transitions to work. You can either download your PowerPoint as:

[L25] 

[L26] * **A Video/MP4:** This **will play** any videos, transitions, or animations your PowerPoint may have.

[L27] * **A PPT File:** Videos, transitions, and animations will not play, but you can adjust the duration of slides.

[L28] 

[L29] **That said, let's get started!**

[L30] 

[L31] 1. Download your PowerPoint to your device.

[L32] 2. Log in to the **OptiSigns** portal

[L33] 3. Navigate to the **Files/Assets** page

[L34] 4. Click [**Upload Files**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016247974)

[L35] 5. **Drag** in or **select** your PowerPoint file from your device

[L36] 6. Click **Upload**  

[L37] **![chrome_g1LHNs2Iy9.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/32753273873683)**

[L38] 7. Either: **Assign** to a screen on the Screens Management page or **push** the asset to your screen.

[L39] 	* If downloaded as a **PDF** or **PPT,** select the **Duration (seconds)**of the entire document. *(Min. 4/page)*  

[L40] 	![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/32753266813587)

[L41] 

[L42] ### Limitations

[L43] 

[L44] * Videos, transitions, and animations will not play if downloaded as a PDF or PPT file. It will just be a slideshow.

[L81] ## Option 3: Embed Your PowerPoint

[L82] 

[L83] *We do not currently recommend this option unless you are displaying one page.*

[L84] 

[L85] 1. **Upload your PowerPoint file** to Microsoft OneDrive and **open it**.

[L86] 2. Navigate to the **File** tab, select **Share**, and then click **Embed**.  

[L87] ![chrome_9JwlCn8RzV.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/32753260331411)

[L115] ## Best Practices for Using PowerPoint with OptiSigns

[L116] 

[L117] * **File Size:** Keep your PowerPoint file under **25MB** for optimal performance. Complete app size **cannot exceed 105MB**.

[L118] * **Avoid Embedded Videos:** Videos can increase file size and cause performance issues. Instead, consider exporting your presentation as a video if animations or video playback is essential.

[L119] * **Use Simple Transitions:** Complex animations and transitions may not render properly. Stick to basic transitions or upload a video version of your presentation if needed.

[L123] ## FAQs

[L124] 

[L125] ### Why does using PowerPoint require giving OptiSigns Admin permission to use?

[L126] 

[L127] OptiSigns uses Microsoft APIs for integration. In order for our integrations to work, the integration has to be approved by an administrator. This is the same across all integrations using Microsoft APIs.

[L128] 

[L129] This administrator access is only needed for first time access. Once the OptiSigns app is approved for use, other users can use OptiSigns directly.

[L130]
