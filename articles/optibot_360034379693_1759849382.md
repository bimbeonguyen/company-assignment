[L152] Now your Raspberry Pi image already has Digital Signage player features configured:

[L153] 

[L154] * Autostart up and launch OptiSigns.

[L161] ## Limitations of OptiSigns on Raspberry Pi

[L162] 

[L163] While Raspberry Pi do run OptiSigns well under most circumstances, there are a few limitations to be aware of.

[L164] 

[L165] * **Video Playback and Encoding** - Raspberry Pi 5's only support hardware decoding for HEVC. Certain apps like Youtube, Vimeo, etc. will display in low-quality due to using software decoding.

[L166] * **Video Quality Limitations** - Raspberry Pi is not designed for video playback, and we don't recommend videos in quality over 1080p. This is due to chipset performance limitations.
