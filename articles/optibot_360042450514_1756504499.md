[L1] # How to use World Clock App

[L2] 

[L3] World Clock App allow you to show up to 6 clocks of different cities, locations on your screen.  

[L4] Below is an example:

[L5] 

[L6] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360057588553)

[L7] 

[L8] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360057588593)

[L9] 

[L10] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L11] 

[L12] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L13] 

[L14] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L15] 

[L16] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L17] 

[L18] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360056709434)

[L19] 

[L20] Click World Clock app:

[L21] 

[L22] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360056709454)

[L23]  

[L24] Enter your World Clock wall information:

[L25]  

[L26]  ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360056709474)

[L27] 

[L28] * Name: Name of your World Clock wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L29] * Clock Type: select Analog or Digital clock type.

[L30] * 24 Hours Format: If Clock Type is Digital, you can select the 24h format.

[L31] * Location: Type in the location of the clock, you can add up to 6 locations.

[L32] 

[L33] Click Save.  

[L34] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app prepare your wall.  

[L35]   

[L36] 

[L37] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L38] 

[L39] You have created your World Clock Wall.  

[L40] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab. 

[L41] 

[L42] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L43] 

[L44] You can put the created walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L45]
