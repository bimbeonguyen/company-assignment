[L1] # Does OptiSigns offer Non-profit/Education discount?

[L2] 

[L3] Yes, we are supportive of those in non-profit organizations and the education field.

[L4] 

[L5] For non-profit or education organizations, we offer a flat 10% discount on subscriptions. This discount does not apply to hardware.

[L6] 

[L7] To obtain this discount, send us an email request to [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com) with your organization's website and documentation for us to verify. This documentation must prove your organization's tax-exempt status. Typically, this is a 501c3 document, but it can be anything that indicates your organization is tax-exempt.

[L8] 

[L9] Once verified, we will apply the discount to your account going forward.

[L10] 

[L11] If you have any additional questions, concerns, or any feedback about OptiSigns, feel free to reach out to our support team at [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com)
