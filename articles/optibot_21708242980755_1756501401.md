[L1] # How to Share Playlist and Folder with External Users

[L2] 

[L3] Making it effortless to share playlists and folders with external users has become a must. Exclusive to our Pro Plus and higher plans, this feature┬Āopens the door to a wide array of content contributions, encompassing images, videos, and documentsŌĆöessential for enhancing collaboration.

[L4] 

[L5] Furthermore, there are several compelling use cases for this feature:

[L6] 

[L7] * Media advertising companies can collaborate with partner locations such as doctor's offices, streamlining content creation efforts without the need for additional sign-ups or logins.

[L8] * Major retail brands aspire to enable retail locations to enrich shared playlists with localized content.

[L9] * Educational institutions and offices seek a streamlined solution that empowers local administrators to efficiently contribute their own content.

[L10] 

[L11] In this article, we'll guide you on how to seamlessly share your playlist and/or folder with external users, making collaboration a breeze.

[L12] 

[L13] ┬Ā

[L14] 

[L15] # **Ready to get started? Then, let's jump in:**

[L16] 

[L17] ## **How to Share Your Playlist**

[L18] 

[L19] Go to Playlists

[L20] 

[L21] You can create a new playlist to share or share an existing playlist

[L22] 

[L23] Check the box to the right of the selected playlist

[L24] 

[L25] Then click 'Share Link'![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746509029395)

[L26] 

[L27] In the pop-up, click on 'Create Share Link'![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746524473363)

[L28] 

[L29] Enter the email addresses of the external users you'd like to share it with and hit 'Enter'.![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746541383699)

[L30] 

[L31] Share Playlist Options![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746560932499)

[L32] 

[L33] * **Access** - Decide how you would like your external user to collaborate with your playlist

[L34] 	+ **Editor** - Allows users to add, delete, and modify content within the shared playlist

[L35] 	+ **Viewer** - Allows users to view the contents within the shared playlist

[L36] * **Notify Email** - Allows you to send email notifications to recipients, informing them of your intention to share a playlist and providing convenient access links directly within their email.

[L37] * **BCC Myself** - You can BCC yourself if would like to receive a copy of the email in your own inbox without the recipient knowing.

[L38] * **Message** - You can include a personalized message to accompany the shared content, providing additional context, instructions, or any relevant information for the recipients.

[L39] 

[L40] General Access Options

[L41] 

[L42] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21745885762195)

[L43] 

[L44] * **Restricted Access**: Only specific individuals or a defined group of people are granted access to the file or folder.

[L45] * **Your Account Members Access**: Limited to members of your OptiSigns account.

[L46] * **Anyone with the Link Access:** Anyone who possesses the unique URL/link can access the content without requiring specific account authentication.

[L47] 

[L48] Advanced Options

[L49] 

[L50] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746532979987)

[L51] 

[L52] * **Enable Access Password** - Users must enter the password to open and view the content; Enhances security by adding an additional layer of authentication.

[L53] 	+ When enabled, you can either type in your custom password or click on 'Create Password' for the system to generate a password.

[L54] 	+ Click on 'Update'

[L55] 		- Password is automatically hidden when the pop-up is closed. To reveal the password, click on 'Reveal Password'

[L56] 

[L57] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746107146003)

[L58] 

[L59] * **Enable Expiration** - Access to the content automatically expires after the specified date and time; Controls access duration, useful for time-sensitive or temporary sharing.

[L60] 	+ When enabled, you can choose the date and time you would like the link to expire. Click 'Apply'

[L61] 

[L62] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746198931347)

[L63] 

[L64] Click on 'Share' when ready

[L65] 

[L66] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746564730899)

[L67] 

[L68] You can also share the link manually without sending email notifications by clicking on 'Copy Link'. This will automatically copy the link to clipboard.

[L69] 

[L70] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746311068435)

[L71] 

[L72] If you no longer wish to share the playlist, you can click on 'Unshare Link' to retract or revoke access to the playlist and its content.

[L73] 

[L74] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746341100435)

[L75] 

[L76] You have the flexibility to manage the shared playlist effortlessly, allowing you to seamlessly add new collaborators, adjust their permissions, or remove access whenever needed.

[L77]

[L80] ## **How to Share Your Folder**

[L81] 

[L82] Sharing your folder is a very similar process to sharing your playlist, making it an intuitive and straightforward experience.

[L83] 

[L84] ┬Ā

[L85] 

[L86] Go to Files/Assets

[L87] 

[L88] Select the folder you wish to share

[L89] 

[L90] On the top right hand corner, click on the 3 dots

[L91] 

[L92] Then click 'Share Link'

[L93] 

[L94] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746590244627)

[L95] 

[L96] In the pop-up, click on 'Create Share Link'

[L97] 

[L98] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746610159635)

[L99] 

[L100] Enter the email addresses of the external users you'd like to share it with and hit 'Enter'.

[L101] 

[L102] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/21746690978963)

[L103] 

[L104] **NOTE:** The features, functionality, and settings for sharing, general access, and advanced options remain consistent with those of sharing a playlist.
