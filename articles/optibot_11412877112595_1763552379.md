[L1] # What if the OptiSigns app does not auto-start on an Android TV?

[L2] 

[L3] Android TV has different versions of firmware on the Android system. Sometimes, OptiSigns will not auto-start when the Android TV reboots. There is a default app that will block any app from auto-starting.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] **TCL Android Smart TV:**

[L8] 

[L9] Safety Guard app will disable the OptiSigns app auto starts. Here is a step-by-step on how to enable it. 

[L10] 

[L11] To provide permission on "Auto Launch Safety Guard":  

[L12] Safety Guard -> Permission Shield -> Auto Launch -> Auto Manager: Closed; And also provide permission to OptiSigns player by changing it to Open.

[L13] 

[L14] **Google Chromecast with Android TV**:

[L15] 

[L16] To enable OptiSigns auto start:  

[L17] Settings -> Apps -> Special app access -> Energy optimization -> OptiSigns app -> Disable it  

[L18] Then go to "Turn screen on" in the Special app access -> OptiSigns app -> Enable it.   

[L19]
