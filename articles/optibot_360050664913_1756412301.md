[L2] 

[L3] [Google Trends](https://trends.google.com/trends/)¬†show search, interest trend for certain keywords, topic, it is a good way to create interesting, engaging content for your viewers.

[L10] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L11] 

[L12] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L13] 

[L14] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L15] 

[L16] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26482298674323)

[L19] ¬†

[L20] 

[L21] Click Google Trends:

[L22] 

[L23] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360075536814)

[L24] Then go to Google Trends:¬†<https://trends.google.com/>

[L25] 

[L26] Find the trends that you want to display on your screen. Then click the Share Embed button:

[L27] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076676713)

[L28] ¬†

[L29] Click on the embed code and copy it.

[L30] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360075536854)

[L31] ¬†

[L32] 

[L33] Go back to OptiSigns and paste it in the Google Trends app:

[L34] 

[L35] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360077231294)

[L36] 

[L37] * Name: Name of your Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L38] * Embed Code: The code you copied from Google Trends

[L39] * Update Interval:¬†Google Trends is auto update by default. You can also input an interval here on how often you want to refresh the content.

[L40] * Zoom: Depend on what kind of chart you picked, zoom may help the content to be displayed better on your screens.

[L41] 

[L42] Click Save.  

[L43] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app to fetch posts and prepare your wall, so please be patient.

[L44] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L45] 

[L46] You have created your Google Trends Wall.  

[L47] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L48] 

[L49] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L50] 

[L51] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
