[L1] # Windows

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this guide, we will walk you through the end-to-end process to install OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your Windows. You can easily use your Windows laptop or PC as a test screen. Follow the steps below:

[L4] 

[L5] ## **Here are the high-level steps:**

[L6] 

[L7] 1) **Download** & **set up** OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your PC.  

[L8]   

[L9] 2) Use OptiSigns' web portal to **assign content** and **manage your screens**.

[L10] 

[L11] So, let's dive in!

[L12] 

[L13] ## **1) Download & Set up OptiSigns Digital Signage App on Your PC.**

[L14] 

[L15] OptiSigns supports Windows 7, 8, 10. 32bit and 64bit.

[L16] 

[L17] To install our Windows-compatible app, click **[here](https://links.optisigns.com/win).**  

[L18] Once downloaded. **Right-click** and select "**Run as Administrator**"

[L19] 

[L20] ![1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360084993434)

[L21] 

[L22] The app will run in full-screen mode, and generate a pairing code for you to pair with the [app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/)portal. You can also move the mouse around and click the top 3 buttons to resize, open side bar menu, or close the app.

[L23] 

[L24] ![2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360084993634)

[L25] 

[L26] On the side menu, you can set Orientation, etc. The app has AutoStart and Fullscreen on Startup checked as default. So, next time your PC starts up, it will run the OptiSigns app automatically.

[L27] 

[L28] ![3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086179473)

[L29] 

[L30] Now, you can go to our portal at: <https://app.optisigns.com/>to pair the screen.

[L31] 

[L32] ![4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086179513)

[L33] 

[L34] Once logged in. click the "**Add screen**" button.

[L35] 

[L36] ┬Ā![5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086179653)

[L37] 

[L38] In the pop-up window, type in the **Pair Code** showing up on the OptiSigns App. Then, click **Pair**.

[L39] 

[L40] ![6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086179793)

[L41] 

[L42] The Optisigns App will change to:

[L43] 

[L44] ![7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086179813)

[L45] 

[L46] Now, you are ready to upload and assign content.
