[L1] # How to use Follow Us App

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you through how to create and use Follow Us Wall app.

[L4] 

[L5] This app is useful to encourage viewers to follow you on social media platform, and increase your follower base.

[L17] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L18] 

[L19] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L20] 

[L21] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L22] 

[L23] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App", and Click Follow Us app:

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500019182342)

[L26] 

[L27] Enter your Follow Us Wall information:

[L28] 

[L29] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076704093)

[L30] 

[L31] * Name: Name of your Follow Us Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L32] * Title: Type in a Title you want to display on the screen.

[L33] * Social Profile:¬†You can select Facebook, Twitter or Instagram, you can just have 1 or add all 3. (for example¬†<https://www.instagram.com/nasa/>¬†put¬†**nasa**). Please check to ensure there's no typo.

[L34] * Theme:¬†Select Light or Dark Theme.

[L35] * Speed: If there are more than 1 Social Profile added, this determine how long each profile will show.

[L36] * Website (Optional): if you want to put your website URL on the screen.

[L37] * Email (Optional): if you want to put your email address on the screen.

[L38] * Telephone (Optional): if you want to put phone number of the screen.

[L39] 

[L40] Click Save.  

[L41] After Saving, you can Preview the wall.  

[L42]   

[L43] 

[L44] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L45] 

[L46] You have created your Follow Us Wall.  

[L47] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L48] 

[L49] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L50] 

[L51] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
