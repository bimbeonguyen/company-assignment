[L1] # Custom Branding Settings

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain everything you need to know about Custom Branding in OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] * [Branding Section](#Branding)

[L6] * [Login Page Section](#Login)

[L7] * [Account Management Page Section](#Account)

[L8] 

[L9] It's possible to customize the OptiSigns Portal experience to match your brand. It's also possible to hide OptiSigns branding apps and features.

[L10] 

[L11] The Branding feature is available to OptiSigns **Pro Plus**plan subscribers or higher.

[L12] 

[L13] To use it, navigate to the [**Branding Page**](https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/branding-settings) in your Account Settings:

[L14] 

[L15] ![firefox_d9M2jq9spn(1).jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38892952107923)

[L16] 

[L17] Inside, you'll find three sections: Branding, Login Page, and Account Management. Below, we'll go over each setting within the three sections and explain what they do.

[L18] 

[L19] ---

[L20]

[L23] The Branding section primarily deals with how your custom portal will appear and be shared with others.

[L33] **Sub-domain:**

[L34] 

[L35] This is for a customized login page, you can make your sub-domain login (i.e. abcmedia.optisigns.net)

[L61] ## Login Page Section

[L62] 

[L63] The Login Page section deals with settings for your login page - where your users will initially engage with the platform.

[L64] 

[L65] ![firefox_WkUJ7s8Stl.jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38892952112787)

[L66] 

[L67] **Upload new Logo**

[L68] 

[L69] This is the logo on your login page.

[L70] 

[L71] You can see an example here: <https://abcmedia.optisigns.net/signIn>

[L72] 

[L73] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500000523622)

[L74] 

[L75] **Upload New Background**

[L76] 

[L77] This is the background image for your login page

[L78] 

[L79] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500000524022)

[L80] 

[L81] **Remove OptiSigns Branding**

[L82] 

[L83] Check this option to remove all OptiSigns branding from the Login Page and all other parts of the portal.

[L84] 

[L85] **Hide Account Button**

[L86] 

[L87] Check this option to remove the "Account" button from the Login Page.

[L88] 

[L89] **Hide Google Login**

[L90] 

[L91] Check this option to remove the option to login through Google from the Login Page.

[L92] 

[L93] **Hide FB Login**

[L94] 

[L95] Check this option to remove the option to login through Facebook from the Login Page.

[L96] 

[L97] **Hide MS Login**

[L98] 

[L99] Check this option to remove the option to login through Microsoft from the Login Page.

[L100] 

[L101] **Login Box**

[L102] 

[L103] You can move the login box location and customize it further.

[L111] ## Account Management Page

[L112] 

[L113] ![firefox_Lkvy7zVYLt.jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38892952113683)

[L114] 

[L115] **Hide Payment History Page**

[L116] 

[L117] Check this to hide the Payment History page from your Branded Portal.

[L118] 

[L119] **Hide Subscription Page**

[L120] 

[L121] Check this to hide the Subscription page from your Branded Portal.

[L122] 

[L123] **Hide Account Members Page**

[L124] 

[L125] Check this to hide the Account Members page from your Branded Portal.

[L126] 

[L127] **Hide Proof of Play Page**

[L128] 

[L129] Check this to hide the Proof of Play page from your Branded Portal.

[L130] 

[L131] **Hide Audit Logging Page**

[L132] 

[L133] Check this to hide the Audit Logging page from your Branded Portal.

[L134] 

[L135] **Hide Monitor & Alert Page**

[L136] 

[L137] Check this to hide the Monitor & Alert page from your Branded Portal.

[L138] 

[L139] **Enable Templates & Designer**

[L140] 

[L141] Check to allow use of OptiSigns Template and OptiSigns Designer in your Branded Portal.

[L142] 

[L143] **Hide External Communications (RS232) Page**

[L144] 

[L145] Check this to hide the External Communications (RS232) page from your Branded Portal.

[L155] **Show/Hide Apps**

[L156] 

[L157] Customize the Apps that can appear in your branded portal. Selected apps will appear - to hide apps, make sure they are unselected.

[L159] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/42392086965267)

[L161] **Show/Hide Apps For Share Links**

[L162] 

[L163] Customize the Apps that appear when Share Links are sent. Works the same as normal Show/Hide apps, but allows further restriction if desired.

[L171] **Send emails from:**

[L172] 

[L173] For Proof of play report, you can schedule report emails to be sent directly to your users.

[L174] 

[L175] With this setting you can set the reports to be sent from your email address (not OptiSigns email address).

[L177] You can set a Custom email address for users to receive these emails.
