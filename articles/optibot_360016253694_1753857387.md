[L1] # Is there storage limit for my account?

[L2] 

[L3] Currently there's limit of 10GB of cloud storage per paid screen you have.  

[L4] I.e. if you have 5 paid screens subscription, you will have 50GB.  

[L5] However, if you need more, please just send us an email at [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com)
