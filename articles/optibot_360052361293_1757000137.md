[L1] # How to Update Fire TV OS to latest version

[L2] 

[L3] Some times your Fire Stick could encounter issues with some apps like weather, social not displaying correctly.

[L4] 

[L5] This could caused because Fire TV OS is not up to date.

[L6] 

[L7] To update your Fire TV OS, Go to Fire TV's setting: **Settings -> My Fire TV -> About -> Check for Updates**

[L8] 

[L9] 1. Go to Home Page and scroll right to "**Settings**"

[L10] 

[L11] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012115221)

[L12] 

[L13] 2. Click "**My Fire TV**" in the Settings

[L14] 

[L15] ![Fire_Stick-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011814102)

[L16] 

[L17] 3. Then Select "**About**"

[L18] 

[L19] ![Fire_Stick-41_About.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012115441)

[L20] 

[L21] 4. Click "**Install Update**"

[L22] 

[L23] ![Fire_Stick-41_install_update.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012115481)

[L24] 

[L25] The FireStick update will now start. It may take some times to download and update your Fire Stick

[L26] Once it is updated, your Fire Stick will automatically restart. When you check for its Fire OS, you will find that a new version is installed.

[L27] 

[L28] If your device hasn’t restarted, you can do it manually by pressing on the **Middle**button and **Play/Pause** button at the same time and hold it for 5 seconds.

[L29]
