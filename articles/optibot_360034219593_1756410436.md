[L1] # PowerPoint Transition and Animation effect

[L2] 

[L3] By default, uploaded PowerPoint presentation will be played without transition or animation effect.

[L4] 

[L5] To use the transition, animation effects you can export your PowerPoint to MP4 video.

[L6] 

[L7] You can do so by Click **File** > **Export** > **Create a Video**. 

[L8] 

[L9] Select the video quality you want, which pertains to the resolution of the finished video.

[L10] 

[L11] Then Upload to OptiSigns.
