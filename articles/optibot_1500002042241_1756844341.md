[L1] # How to use HDMI & other video input sources in with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] It can be useful to split up your screens with part of Live TV, HDMI in, and part display of other information.

[L4] 

[L5] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360100856774)

[L6] 

[L7] ## What You'll Need

[L8] 

[L9] * An OptiSigns **[Standard plan or higher](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing).**

[L10] 	+ **NOTE:** Running the Live TV app only requires a Standard plan, but running the Live TV with Ads app requires an┬Ā**Engage**┬Ā**plan or higher**.

[L11] * **Video source**. This is most commonly a TV set-top box, but could be anything from a video game console to a streaming device.

[L12] * **USB video capture card**. This transforms the video into a usable format for the OptiSigns Live TV app.

[L13] * Device running OptiSigns. Currently, we only support┬Ā**Windows PCs** (with an Intel i5 CPU at minimum) and **[OptiSigns Pro](https://shop.optisigns.com/products/optisigns-digital-signage-player) or [ProMax players](https://shop.optisigns.com/products/optisigns-promax-signage-player)**. Ensure you are running the latest version of the OptiSigns app.

[L14] * A **screen** to display it on.

[L15] 

[L16] You'll connect it up in this fashion:┬Ā**Video Source ŌåÆ USB Capture Card ŌåÆ Player / PC ŌåÆ Screen**.

[L17] 

[L18] ### **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L19] 

[L20] Then go to <https://app.optisigns.com/>

[L21] 

[L22] Click Files/Assets -> Apps

[L23] 

[L24] Then find the Live TV app:  

[L25] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001961182)

[L26] 

[L27] Give it a name & click Save

[L28] 

[L29] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001961202)

[L30] 

[L31] Then you can put this TV app in a Playlist or a Zone of your [SplitScreen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573).

[L32] 

[L33] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500001962422)

[L34] 

[L35] ### **That's all! Congratulations!**

[L36] 

[L37] When you assign the SplitScreen to your WIndows Mini PC it will play the video input and other splitscreen content.

[L38] 

[L39] Troubleshooting common problems:

[L40] 

[L41] 1) Make sure your USB device is working and showing content in other apps like [OBS Streaming](https://obsproject.com).

[L42] 

[L43] 2) Troubleshoot, change video, audio sources by go to OptiSigns Player's side menu select Live TV input sources.

[L44] 

[L45] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360103020713)

[L46] 

[L47] And select the correct input source.
