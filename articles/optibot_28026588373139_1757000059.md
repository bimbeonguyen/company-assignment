[L1] # How to Fix The Scaling Issue on OptiSigns Android Device Gen 3

[L2] 

[L3] If you encounter any issues with displaying the correct text size or graph size using the OptiSigns Android Stick Gen 3, following these steps should help resolve the problem.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] **Here is an example of Different Scaling Views:**

[L8] 

[L9] * **Smallest Display View:**

[L10] 

[L11] **![Screenshot 2024-04-01 095007.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026605056659)**

[L12] 

[L13] * **Largest Display View:**

[L14] 

[L15] **![Screenshot 2024-04-01 094928.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026605062035)**

[L16] 

[L17]  

[L18] 

[L19] This article provides instructions on adjusting the scaling settings for the OptiSigns Android Device Gen 3.

[L20] 

[L21]  

[L22] 

[L23] 1. Go to the **Settings**

[L24] 

[L25] **![annotely_image (31).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026605064851)**

[L26] 

[L27] 2. Click **Device Preferences**

[L28] 

[L29] ![annotely_image (32).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026588345107)

[L30] 

[L31] 3. Click **Display & Sound**

[L32] 

[L33] ![annotely_image (33).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026605069459)

[L34] 

[L35] 4. Click **Display Size**

[L36] 

[L37] ![annotely_image (34).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026588354579)

[L38] 

[L39] 5. You will then arrive at the Display Size page. Here, you can simply use the left or right arrow on your remote to adjust the display size to your preferred setting.

[L40] 

[L41] ![annotely_image (35).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/28026588356499)

[L42] 

[L43] After completing these steps, you can relaunch the OptiSigns app. Adjusting the settings should allow you to achieve the desired scaling on your screen.

[L44]
