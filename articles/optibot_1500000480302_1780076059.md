[L1] # Advanced: Custom Domain mapping

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns Pro Plus and Enterprise plan, you can enhance your branding by mapping your custom domain for OptiSigns Management Portal.

[L4] 

[L5] ¬Ý

[L6] 

[L7] For example: you can map your sub-domain: **login.abcmedia.com** so that your users can log in and use the portal from **login.abcmedia.com** and use the app like the screenshot below.

[L8] 

[L9] ¬Ý

[L10] 

[L11] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500000517302)

[L15] #### **Let's jump in and get started!**

[L16] 

[L17] **1) Activate your OptiSigns sub-domain (in this example: abcmedia.optisigns.net):**

[L18] 

[L19] Go to the Branding page of your Account Management Settings:

[L20] 

[L21] <https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/branding-settings>

[L22] 

[L23] Type in your desired sub-domain for optisigns.net. In this case, we type in "abcmedia".  

[L24] Don't worry about optisigns.net, you will map your domain in the next step.

[L25] 

[L26] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360099399934)

[L27] 

[L28] **2) Map CNAME alias for your domain/sub-domain:**

[L29] 

[L30] In your Domain DNS management, map your desired domain/sub-domain to your OptiSigns sub-domain using CNAME Alias.  

[L31] In this example, we map: login.abcmedia.com -> abcmedia.optisigns.net

[L32] 

[L33] Refer to your domain host documentation for more specific details.

[L34] 

[L35] Here are the generic steps:

[L36] 

[L37] 1. Go to your domain‚Äôs DNS records.

[L38] 2. Add a record to your DNS settings, selecting¬Ý**CNAME**¬Ýas the record type.

[L39] 3. Return to the first window or tab and copy the contents of the¬Ý**Label/Host**¬Ýfield.

[L40] 4. Paste the copied contents into the¬Ý**Label**¬Ýor¬Ý**Host**¬Ýfield with your DNS records.

[L43] 

[L44] Your record should look similar to one of the tables below:

[L45] 

[L46] **CNAME Record**

[L47] 

[L48] | Record type | Label/Host field | Time To Live (TTL) | Destination/Target field |

[L49] | --- | --- | --- | --- |

[L50] | CNAME | login | 3600 or leave the default | abcmedia.optisigns.net |

[L51] 7. Save your record.  

[L52] CNAME record changes can take up to 72 hours to go into effect, but typically they happen much sooner.

[L64] **3) Activate SSL for your sub-domain**

[L65] 

[L66] Once you have done step 2, return to the Branding page:

[L67] 

[L68] <https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/branding-settings>

[L69] 

[L70] Enter the domain/sub-domain you have configured in Step 2 in Your domain section.

[L71] 

[L72] In this example, we use: login.abcmedia.com

[L73] 

[L74] Then click Activate.

[L75] 

[L76] This will trigger the process on OptiSigns side to activate SSL for your sub-domain. This will ensure that your user can use HTTPS: i.e. <https://login.abcmedia.com> to use the app.

[L77] 

[L78] This process can take up to 24-48 hours to complete. You will be notified via email once it's done.

[L79] 

[L80] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360101613533)

[L82] ¬Ý

[L83] 

[L84] #### **That's all!**

[L85] 

[L86] Once you get the notification that your SSL is activated, you can start using your own domain/sub-domain (i.e. <https://login.abcmedia.com>).
