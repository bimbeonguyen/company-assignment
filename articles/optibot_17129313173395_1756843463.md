[L1] # How to manage Sub-Account billing with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] OptiSigns supports managing billing for sub-accounts. With this feature, headquarters/corporate can manage the billing & payment on behalf of the subsidiaries, like franchisees or different departments. For example, a branch manages the account and screens on its own, but corporates can set up the subaccount and add payment methods for local branches.  

[L4]   

[L5] **Important Note**: Sub-account is a separate account, so **screens**, **contents**, **playlists,** and **schedules** are segregated in the subaccount. The sub-account billing feature is only to support the main account to manage the billing and payment for the sub-account.

[L6] 

[L7] If you would like to manage the recourses in the "sub-account" instead, we suggest that you use the "[**Teams**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360034883113)" feature so that you can reserve the screens per team, and then allocate the cost by the number of screens.

[L15] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L16] 

[L17] Log on to our portal: <https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/sub-account-billing>

[L18] 

[L19] Go to "**Sub-Account Billing**" and Click "**Add Sub-account**"

[L20] 

[L21] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/17128740809491)

[L22] 

[L23] You will have two options.

[L24] 

[L25] * Link an existing OptiSigns account | Click Link Account

[L26] * Create a new sub-account for your new department | Create New Account

[L27] 

[L28] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/17128808299667)

[L29] 

[L30] Once you link to the account, it will send the email notification to that email account.

[L31] 

[L32] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/17128964490771)

[L33] 

[L34] After the sub-account approve the request. It will show the subscription detail.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/17129028739731)

[L37] 

[L38] You can update the payment method for the sub-account.

[L39] 

[L40] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/17129119195795)

[L41] 

[L42] You can only do these for your sub-account

[L43] 

[L44] * Update the subscription plan

[L45] * See the payment history

[L46] * Update the billing email

[L47] * Update the billing information

[L48] * Update the billing address

[L49] 

[L50] **Important Note**: Sub-account is a separate account. The main account can update the billing info for the sub-account. All screens, contents, playlists, and schedules will not be able to be shared between the main account and the sub-account.
