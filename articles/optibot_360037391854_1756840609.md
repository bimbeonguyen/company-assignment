[L1] # How to display content inside websites that require login?

[L2] 

[L3] Sometimes you have content behind a website that requires log-in, such as your project schedule in Atlassian's JIRA, Trello, dashboard behind Tableau, Salesforces, etc.

[L4] 

[L5] It can either be a Browser Popup or a Gated Website (like below)

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360103797493)

[L8] 

[L9] The solution is simple, you just need to:

[L10] 

[L11] * Copy the link to the page, and dashboard you need to display.

[L12] * Put it in the Website app in OptiSigns

[L13] * Then put the asset on your screen or in the playlist as an asset

[L14] * When it shows up on your device, just use your mouse & keyboard to enter in username and password (remember to save credentials, for Fire TV, you may need a [Bluetooth mouse & keyboard](https://www.amazon.com/Logitech-Illuminated-Living-Room-Keyboard-Touchpad/dp/B00ZOPVSKW/) to pair and type this information in).

[L15] * If you don't have a Bluetooth mouse & keyboard, you can use a [USB HUB adaptor](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360055080914) to connect with your mouse & keyboard.

[L16] 

[L17] That's it, the credential will be saved, and every time OptiSigns plays the link, it will show the secured information inside the website.
