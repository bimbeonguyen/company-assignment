[L1] # How to use the CNN News App

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain how to use the CNN News app on OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Creating a CNN News Asset](#Creating)

[L7] 

[L8] The OptiSigns CNN app allows you to display CNN News on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Top Stories, Breaking News, World News, US News, EU News, Business, Technology, and more.

[L9] 

[L10] QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. CNN News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)

[L28] ## Creating a CNN News Asset

[L29] 

[L30] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L31] 

[L32] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L33] 

[L34] Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add┬Ā**CNN**┬Āon your Account.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189027306771)┬Ā

[L37] Click **CNN**:

[L38] 

[L39] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189041510419)Enter your CNN Wall App information:

[L40] 

[L41] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189041515923)* Name: Name of your CNN Wall app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L42] * Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.

[L43] * Theme: Select Light (default CNN red) or Dark Theme.

[L44] * Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[L45] 

[L46] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189041520659)

[L47] 

[L48] * Font Size: Set the font size for the CNN Wall app.

[L49] 

[L50] Clicking┬Ā**Advanced**opens up additional options:

[L51] 

[L52] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189041521811)* Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:  

[L53] ┬Ā

[L54] 

[L55] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45189041526547)

[L56] 

[L57] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the CNN News Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

[L58] 

[L59]   

[L60] After you've configured the CNN News app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your CNN app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.
