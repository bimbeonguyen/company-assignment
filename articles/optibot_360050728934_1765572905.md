[L1] # Difference between Instagram & Instagram Business App

[L2] 

[L3] |  |

[L4] | --- |

[L5] | **NOTE** |

[L6] | To set up an Instagram Business account, we recommend following [this article on Instagram's website](https://help.instagram.com/502981923235522). |

[L7] 

[L8] As result of Facebook's change in policies, Instagram Consumer and Instagram Business/Creator have different level of access.

[L9] 

[L10] **Instagram (or Instagram Consumer):**

[L11] 

[L12] * Display posts in your profiles only.

[L13] 

[L14] **Instagram Business/Creator:**

[L15] 

[L16] * Display post from your profiles

[L17] * You can display posts from any public profiles (i.e. <https://www.instagram.com/arsenal>)

[L18] * You can also follow and monitor a hashtag

[L19] * Get follower counts (for [Instagram Counter App](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360047501233))

[L20] 

[L21] To learn how to use our Instagram [click here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016388733); or how to use our Instagram Business app [click here.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051495193)
