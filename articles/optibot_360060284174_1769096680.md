[L1] # How to use YouTube Live Schedule with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] A real world scenario would be:

[L4] 

[L5] A church has scheduled a video to go live on Sunday morning at 9 o'clock through YouTube Live. If the main hall is filled, the church has overflow rooms with televisions, screens for signage, and presentations. You can use the OptiSigns YouTube Live schedule feature to schedule a live session to broadcast in the overflow rooms every Sunday 9AM automatically.┬Ā ┬Ā

[L6] 

[L7] Putting YouTube Live Schedule on your digital signs screens is easy with OptiSigns.  

[L8] You just need to copy the YouTube channel link and paste in the YouTube Live Schedule app and select the schedule.  

[L9] **Note: To use the app, Youtube Channel URL is required. The format is like below**

[L10] 

[L11] **https://www.youtube.com/channel/Your-Channel-Id**  

[L12] **To get your channel Id, go to the about page of your channel, click on share channel and copy channel Id.**

[L23] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L24] 

[L25] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click┬Ā[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L27] Then log on to our portal:┬Ā<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L29] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L33] Click YouTube Live Schedule app:

[L43] 

[L44] * Name: Give your YouTube Live Schedule a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only. The app will try to get the video's title and use as name, but you can change it to something else.

[L45] * URL: Paste the link to your YouTube channel here.

[L46] * Caption: If the YouTube video has closed caption, the app will show it. Note: closed caption must be created by the YouTube video author, auto generated caption may not work.

[L47] * Live Time: Set a date and time for the YouTube Live video.

[L48] * Timezone: Select your time zone.

[L49] * Repeat: Set the frequency of the YouTube Live video.

[L50] 

[L51] Click Save.

[L52] 

[L53] After Saving, you can Preview live videos to verify.

[L61] 

[L62] * Name: Give your YouTube Live Schedule a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only. The app will try to get the video's title and use as name, but you can change it to something else.

[L63] * URL: Auto generated the YouTube channel.

[L64] * Caption: If the YouTube video has closed caption, the app will show it. Note: closed caption must be created by the YouTube video author, auto generated caption may not work.

[L83] | **HOW THE APP WORKS** |

[L84] | The YouTube Live Schedule app has a very specific way it functions, and understanding this may help work out any issues you may be having with it.

[L85] Assume:* *x **=*** the time you set in the YouTube Live Schedule app

[L86] * *y =* the time the actual livestream becomes available from YouTube

[L87] 

[L88] The app checks for live data every 30 seconds at **x**, **x+30,** and **x+60**, *then stops**.***So **x needs to be slightly later than y** (usually 1ŌĆō2 minutes), otherwise it may miss the live stream. |

[L89] 

[L90] For more information about using Schedule, please refer to [Schedule Guide](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016981853).

[L91] 

[L92] NOTE: If your schedules ends earlier than 11AM, YouTube Live Schedule will play the next video on your Channel.

[L98] ## **Tips & Notes:**

[L99] 

[L100] You should set your event in YouTube to live a few minutes before the actual schedule time in OptiSigns, as YouTube take a few mins to set the event live.

[L101] 

[L102] If you set your event to be live publicly (so people can view it without logging on to YouTube), it may take up to 10min from the time you set the event to Live till the time it's actually live on YouTube (likely due to YouTube review process).┬Ā┬Ā
