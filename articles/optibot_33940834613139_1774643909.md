[L97] ### What Content Might Be Affected When Moving from a Paid Plan or Trial to a Free Plan?

[L98] 

[L99] If youŌĆÖve created assets or playlists in OptiSigns while on your free trial or while using a paid account and you choose to downgrade to a Free Plan, some of these may be affected.

[L100] 

[L101] Certain apps will be unavailable for use on a Free Plan. These apps are marked with a red lock.

[L102] 

[L103] ![example app with lock icon in upper right corner](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/33940834606867)

[L104] 

[L105] You will be unable to create assets using these apps while on a Free Plan. Any assets created using one of these apps during your trial or as a paid subscriber will still exist on the OptiSigns backend, but will be unable to display on your screens.

[L106] 

[L107] If your used storage is more than 1GB, after your account is downgraded to a Free Plan, you will have limited ability to create new assets. You will need to delete some files to free up available storage for your free plan.

[L108] 

[L109] You will lose access to certain features that are not available on a Free plan, such as using Schedules, SplitScreens, or widgets in designer. Warning messages will appear when you attempt to use those features.
