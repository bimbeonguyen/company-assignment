[L1] # How to move screen to other team

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you through how to move the screen to another team.

[L4] 

[L5] First, you need to have another team in your account.

[L6] 

[L7] If you don't have another team yet, you can add a team to your account. Here is a how-to add team [article.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360034883113) 

[L8] 

[L9] To move your screen, go to Screens on your web portal.

[L10] 

[L11] Click the three-period button on the screen. Then click "**Move To Team**".

[L12] 

[L13] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003190142)

[L14] 

[L15] Then you can select which team you want to move to.

[L16] 

[L17] That's it. Your screen will move to the specific team.

[L18] 

[L19] Note: 

[L20] 

[L21] Sometimes it will pop out an error message "**You have reached the maximum number of screens for your team, to continue adding more screens, please update a number of screens limit of your team.**"

[L22] 

[L23] It means you need to update the **Number of screens limit** for your team.

[L24] 

[L25] 1. You can go to the Account Members.

[L26] 

[L27] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360104227633)

[L28] 

[L29] 2. Click the three-period button to Update the team. 

[L30] 

[L31] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003230301)

[L32] 

[L33] 3. Update the Number of screens limit in the team

[L34] 

[L35] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360104227553)

[L36] 

[L37] Then you are good to move the screen to the team.
