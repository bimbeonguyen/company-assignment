[L1] # Your Facebook Page Doesn't Show in The Dropdown List

[L2] 

[L3] In this article, we will guide you how to solve the issue if your Facebook Page doesn't show in the dropdown list of OptiSigns Facebook page.  

[L4]   

[L5] **First, you can go to the Facebook Business Integrations page:**

[L6] 

[L7] <https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools&ref=business_login_reentry>

[L8] 

[L9] Remove the OptiSigns app.

[L10] 

[L11] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/20749959088659)

[L12] 

[L13] Then you can recreate the Facebook app in the OptiSigns portal: <https://app.optisigns.com/app/assetManagement>

[L14] 

[L15] * **It will pop out login with your Facebook account.**

[L16] 

[L17] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/51302925708435)* **Select your Facebook Page and allow the OptiSigns to your Facebook page**

[L18] 

[L19] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/51302925708819) 

[L20] 

[L21] * **Select Continue as "Your Profile Name"**

[L22] 

[L23] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/51302925709587)You should be able to see your Facebook page in the dropdown list.
