[L1] # Configuring Mass Deployment with Jamf Pro MDM on Apple Devices

[L2] 

[L3] Efficiently managing digital signage across multiple devices is crucial for businesses to ensure smooth operations and consistent updates. OptiSigns, in conjunction with a Mobile Device Management (MDM) system, offers a streamlined process for mass enrolling your devices. This guide will walk you through the steps to distribute and manage OptiSigns digital signage software using a Jamf MDM system on Apple devices.

[L17] ## Requirements

[L18] 

[L19] To proceed with this guide, please ensure that you have:

[L20] 

[L21] 1. Jamf Pro MDM account.

[L22] 

[L23] 2. OptiSigns.com credentials.

[L24] 

[L25] 3. Apple devices running iOS or tvOS.

[L26] 

[L27] 4. Access to Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager.

[L28] 

[L29] ## Step 1: Load OptiSigns App Inside Jamf MDM

[L30] 

[L31] Inside ABM (Apple Business Manager) volume purchase licenses of OptiSigns Digital Signage (It's free).

[L32] 

[L33] We assume that the ABM VPP account is linked to your Jamf Pro instance, otherwise, use this Jamf [Video Guide](https://trainingcatalog.jamf.com/volume-purchasing/637880)┬Āto do so.

[L34] 

[L35] After populating ABM apps into Jamf MDM, you should see OptiSigns Digital Signage app inside MDM Mobile Device Apps section, as shown below:

[L36] 

[L37] **![chrome_QXt1erz8BI.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/31703018962963)**

[L39] ## Step 2: OptiSigns App Enrollment with Jamf Pro MDM

[L40] 

[L41] Before deploying the app to devices, you can preconfigure it to have your device automatically enrolled into your OptiSigns account.

[L42] 

[L43] * This is not required, but if you are managing a large number of devices, this will make the deployment much easier.

[L44] 

[L45] To do this, navigate to the **mobile device apps section** in Jamf MDM ŌåÆ Click on the **OptiSigns Digital Signage app ŌåÆ** Select the **App Configuration** section ŌåÆ Complete the configuration as shown below:

[L46] 

[L47] ![chrome_xqP0Dfxy2g.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/36280396747283)

[L48] 

[L49] Let's go through each section of the configuration:

[L50] 

[L51] ![chrome_QVJc5ejA6j.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/36280396752915)

[L52] 

[L53] 1. **serialNo:**┬ĀSerial number of the device, you can map this to a variable from your MDM.

[L54] 2. **accountId:** This is your OptiSigns Account ID, you need to enter it manually.

[L55] 

[L56] Account ID can be found inside the OptiSigns portal, by visiting the **[Screens tab](https://app.optisigns.com/app/screenManagement)** ŌåÆ Finding the screen you'd likeŌåÆ Clicking **Edit Screens** ŌåÆ Click **Advanced** ŌåÆ Click **More** ŌåÆ Click on the "**i**" button┬Ā

[L57] 

[L58] ![chrome_yBWo4GT2Dw.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/31704324281107)

[L59] 

[L60] This will open your **Device Info**:

[L61] 

[L62] ![chrome_81PqujFdUR.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/31704337896467)

[L63] 

[L64] 3. **screenName** - This is the screen name that will appear on the OptiSigns portal, as shown in the screenshot below. Normally this is mapped to a variable from your MDM.
