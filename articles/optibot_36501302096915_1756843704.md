[L1] # How to access the Troubleshooting page of the OptiSigns Player

[L2] 

[L3] Sometimes you may experience issues with the OptiSigns app. If that happens, you can navigate to the **Troubleshooting page** from the side menu in the OptiSigns app to check the connection status and other details.

[L4] 

[L5] ┬Ā

[L6] 

[L7] ### **How to Access the Troubleshooting Page**

[L8] 

[L9] * [Android OS device](#0)

[L10] * [OptiSigns Pro Player](#1)

[L11] * [Windows OS device](#2)

[L12] 

[L13] For **Android OS devices** such as the OptiSigns Android Stick, Amazon Fire Stick, Google Chromecast, and similar devices:

[L14] 

[L15] 1. Press the **three-bar button** on your remote to open the side menu of the OptiSigns app.

[L16] 2. Navigate to **Troubleshooting** under the **Advanced Options** section.

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/36501689295763)

[L19] 

[L20] From there, you can view detailed information about the appŌĆÖs status and connectivity to assist with troubleshooting.

[L21] 

[L22] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/36501689296275)

[L23] 

[L24] * **Check Internet Connection**: Verifies whether the device has an active internet connection.

[L25] * **Check Connection to API Services**: Tests the device's connection to OptiSigns services.

[L26] 	+ **Note**: If this check fails, it may be due to a firewall blocking the connection. Refer to our [**Whitelist Article**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360047275934) for the required URLs and ports.

[L27] * **Check File Downloading**: Confirms the status of downloadable files (e.g., images, videos) being downloaded to the device.

[L28] * **Network Information**: Displays the current network the device is connected to.

[L29] 	+ **WiFi/Ethernet Details**: Includes IP Address, SSID, Signal Strength, Channel, Connection Type, and MAC Address.

[L30] * **Device Information:**

[L31] 	+ Screen Name, Pairing Code, Screen Resolution, OptiSigns App Version, OptiSigns MDM App Version, OS Version, Manufacturer, Model, Serial Number

[L32] 	+ Heartbeat/Polling Interval: Indicates how frequently the device communicates with OptiSigns servers and the last received signal.

[L33] * **Running Time**: Shows how long the OptiSigns app has been running on the device.

[L34] * **Storage**: Displays used and total storage capacity.

[L35] * **Memory**: Displays used and total memory capacity.

[L36] * **System Time**: Shows the current system time on the device.

[L37] * **System Time Zone**: Displays the time zone configured on the device.

[L38] * **Assigned Content Type**: Indicates the type of content the device is playing (e.g., Asset, Playlist, Schedule).

[L39] * **Assigned Content Name**: Provides the name of the content being displayed.

[L40] * **Device Created Date**: Displays the date the device was activated.

[L41] * **Operational Schedule Assigned**: Shows whether an operational schedule is assigned (**Y/N**).

[L42] * **Mute Status**: Displays the current audio status of the device.

[L43] * **Heavy Content Status**: Indicates whether the device is handling heavy content (e.g., 4+ zones or SplitScreen with 4K video) (**Y/N**)
