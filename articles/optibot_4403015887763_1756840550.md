[L1] # Moderate your social media feeds

[L2] 

[L3] If you are using OptiSigns to display your social feed like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter, sometimes you want the option to review/approve certain posts before they show up on your screens. This is particularly useful when you use Hashtags as your social feeds, as this gives you a chance to review the content.

[L4] 

[L5] OptiSigns has a built-in content moderation feature that allows you to do just that.

[L6] 

[L7] #### **Here's how to use it:**

[L8] 

[L9] If you haven't created your [Instagram](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016388733), [Facebook](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360023848334), or [Twitter](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016375473) wall, you can go ahead and create one.

[L10] 

[L11] In Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter app settings, click Advance, and there's an option for Moderation.

[L12] 

[L13] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403008439571)

[L14] 

[L15] By default "Always show new posts" is checked, this means that all new posts will automatically show up on your wall/feed.

[L16] 

[L17] You can uncheck, and click on the wheel ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403015754515) button to moderate the content.

[L18] 

[L19] You then can filter, and search for posts.

[L20] 

[L21] And click ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403015850387) to hide the post or ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403008606995) to delete the post (delete means the post will be deleted from the list in OptiSigns only, not from your account)

[L22] 

[L23] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403015839379)

[L24] 

[L25]  

[L26] 

[L27] That's it, you have learned how to moderate your social media feed on your digital signage screens.

[L28]
