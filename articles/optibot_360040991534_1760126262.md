[L1] # How to use the TechCrunch News App

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain how to use the TechCrunch News app on OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Creating a TechCrunch News Asset](#Creating)

[L7] 

[L8] The OptiSigns TechCrunch app allows you to display TechCrunch News on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Startups, AI, Venture, Security, Crypto, Apps, Fintech, Hardware news and more.

[L9] 

[L10] QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. TechCrunch News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)

[L28] ## Creating a TechCrunch News Asset

[L29] 

[L30] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L31] 

[L32] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L33] 

[L34] Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add¬†**TechCrunch**¬†on your Account.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516237886867)

[L37] 

[L38] ¬†

[L39] 

[L40] Click **TechCrunch**:

[L41] 

[L42] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516237891347)

[L43] 

[L44] Enter your TechCrunch Wall App information:

[L45] 

[L46] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516237896851)

[L47] 

[L48] * Name: Name of your TechCrunch Wall app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L49] * Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.

[L50] * Theme: Select Light or Dark Theme.

[L51] * Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[L52] 

[L53] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516216045331)

[L54] 

[L55] * Font Size: Set the font size for the TechCrunch Wall app.

[L56] 

[L57] Clicking¬†**Advanced**opens up additional options:

[L58] 

[L59] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516237900179)

[L60] 

[L61] * Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:  

[L62] ¬†

[L63] 

[L64] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45516216052627)

[L65] 

[L66] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the TechCrunch News Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

[L67] 

[L68]   

[L69] After you've configured the TechCrunch News app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your TechCrunch app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.  

[L70]
