[L1] # How to Change Time Zone on Your Raspberry Pi device

[L2] 

[L3] Your schedule doesn't start or end at the same time as your schedule. The issue may be your device's time zone setting. You can check your device's setting.

[L4] 

[L5] In this guide, we will walk you through end to end process to change the time zone on your Raspberry Pi device.

[L6] 

[L7] Let's begin,

[L8] 

[L9] ### How to Change Time Zone on your Raspberry Pi device

[L10] 

[L11] To change the time zone on your Raspberry Pi,  go to Preferences > Raspberry Pi Configuration > Localisation > Timezone. Then select your Set Timezone.

[L12] 

[L13] 1. Press the **Start Menu** 

[L14] 

[L15] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-1.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094966293)

[L16] 

[L17] 2. Click "**Preferences** " in the menu

[L18] 

[L19] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-2.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094966313)

[L20] 

[L21] 3. Select "**Raspberry Pi Configuration**"

[L22] 

[L23] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-3.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094966333)

[L24] 

[L25] 4. Go to "**Localisation**" tap in the Raspberry Pi Configuration

[L26] 

[L27] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-4.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360094966353)

[L28] 

[L29] 5. Click "**Set Timezone**" in Localisation

[L30] 

[L31] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-5.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092742514)

[L32] 

[L33] 6. Select your location area. Then click "**OK**"

[L34] 

[L35] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-6.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092742674)

[L36] 

[L37] 7. Click "**OK**" again

[L38] 

[L39] ![RPi_Change_Time_Zone-7.1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360092743014)

[L40] 

[L41] Changing the time zone on your Raspberry Pi can let your screen start and end with the correct schedule. 

[L42] 

[L43] You're all set.
