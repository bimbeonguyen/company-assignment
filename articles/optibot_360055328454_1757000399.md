[L1] # How to Enable/Disable Auto Update for OptiSigns app on Fire TV

[L2] 

[L3] By default Fire TV will automatically update your apps when there's new release.

[L4] 

[L5] You can double check or enable/disable this feature by going to **Settings** > **Applications** > **Appstore** > **Automatic Updates**. Then change it to “**On**.” All of your apps will now update themselves.

[L6] 

[L7]  

[L8] 

[L9] 1. From your Fire Stick homepage go to **"Settings"**

[L10] 

[L11] ![Fire_Stick-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500012115741)

[L12] 

[L13] 2. Scroll through and click **"Applications"**

[L14] 

[L15] ![Fire_Stick-17_Application.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011814382)

[L16] 

[L17] 3. Then click **"Appstore "**

[L18] 

[L19] ![Fire_Stick-51_app_store.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011814402)

[L20] 

[L21] 4. Click "**Automatic Updates"**, and change it to **“On”**

[L22] 

[L23] ![Fire_Stick-51_auto_update.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500011814422)

[L24] 

[L25] All of your apps will now update themselves. The OptiSigns app will update to the latest version on your device.
