[L1] # Data Residency and OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] Customers on the Engage or Enterprise Plans can request their data to be stored in a certain country, or region for data residency requirements.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] By default, all customer's data are stored simultaneously in the:

[L8] 

[L9] * N. Virginia - hosted by Amazon AWS

[L10] * New York City - hosted by Digital Ocean

[L11] 

[L12] And distributed to your devices via CDN for the fastest speed.

[L13] 

[L14]  

[L15] 

[L16] Engage or Enterprise customers can request your data to be stored in:

[L17] 

[L18] * US:

[L19] 	+ N. Virginia (Amazon AWS)

[L20] 	+ New York City (Digital Ocean)

[L21] * EU:

[L22] 	+ Frankfurt (Amazon AWS)

[L23] 	+ Amsterdam (Digital Ocean)

[L24] * UK:

[L25] 	+ London (Amazon AWS)

[L26] * Canada:

[L27] 	+ Montreal (Amazon AWS)

[L28] * Australia:

[L29] 	+ Sydney (Amazon AWS)

[L30] 

[L31] Please contact [support@optisigns.com](mailto:support@optisigns.com) or your sales rep if you are interested in pricing or would like to enable this feature on your account
