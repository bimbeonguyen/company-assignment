[L56] ## Generating a Recurring Schedule

[L57] 

[L58] Follow the steps in the "Create a Schedule" section.

[L59] 

[L60] * **Set Recurrence:**

[L61] 	+ Choose from options like:

[L62] 		- Repeat Daily

[L63] 		- Repeat Weekly (you can select specific days like Sunday, Monday, etc.)

[L64] 		- Repeat Monthly (on a specific date each month)

[L65] 	+ Create a Custom Recurrence:

[L66] 		- Scroll down to and click on the **Custom** option.

[L67] 		- Set up a more tailored schedule, such as repeating every other day, or specific days of the week on a bi-weekly basis.

[L68] 		- Define the recurrence pattern according to your needs.

[L69] * Save the Recurring Schedule: After setting the recurrence pattern, click **Save** to finalize the recurring schedule.

[L92] ## Handling Overlapping Schedules

[L93] 

[L94] When creating or editing schedules, OptiSigns will notify you of any overlaps.

[L95] 

[L96] **Types of Overlaps:**

[L97] 

[L98] * **Unintentional Overlaps:**

[L99] 	+ Adjust the start and end times of the schedules to ensure there are no conflicts.

[L100] * **Intentional Overlaps:**

[L101] 	+ The last updated schedule will take priority during the conflicted time.

[L110] ## Scheduling Around Time Zones

[L111] 

[L112] The screens/devices will play according to their local time zones.

[L113] 

[L114] **Example:**

[L115] 

[L116] * You (and your computer) are in the CST time zone (GMT - 6).

[L117] * Device 1 is in the EST time zone (GMT - 5).

[L118] * Device 2 is in the PST time zone (GMT - 8).

[L119] 

[L120] You set a schedule item to play from 10 AM to 11 AM on 1/1/20XX.

[L121] 

[L122] * Device 1 will play the schedule item from 10 AM to 11 AM on 1/1/20XX in its local time (EST).

[L123] * Device 2 will play the schedule item from 10 AM to 11 AM on 1/1/20XX in its local time (PST).

[L124] 

[L125] Daylight saving time adjustments will be handled automatically.

[L189] ## Frequently Asked Questions About Schedules

[L190] 

[L191] **What if there is a one-time schedule overlapping a recurring schedule?**

[L192] 

[L193] The one-time schedule will take priority.

[L194] 

[L195] **Example:**

[L196] 

[L197] * Recurring schedule A (9 AM - 5 PM daily) overlaps with a one-time schedule B (10 AM - 11 AM) today.

[L198] 

[L199] **Playback Order:**

[L200] 

[L201] * 9 AM - 10 AM: Recurring schedule A

[L202] * 10 AM - 11 AM: Single schedule B

[L203] * 11 AM - 5 PM: Recurring schedule A resumes

[L204] 

[L205] **What if there are two recurring schedules overlapping?**

[L206] 

[L207] The most recently created/ updated schedule will take priority.

[L208] 

[L209] **What happens when there's nothing scheduled?**

[L210] 

[L211] If there is no scheduled content, the screen will display a black screen by default. To avoid this, you can set default content. There are two ways to do this:

[L212] 

[L213] * **At the Schedule Level:** Edit the specific schedule, click on **Settings**, and select your preferred default content (None, Account Default, Asset, or Playlist).

[L214] 

[L215] |  |  |

[L216] | --- | --- |

[L217] | **OptiSigns 1.0** | **OptiSigns 2.0** |

[L218] |  |  |

[L219] 

[L220] * **At the Account Level:** Apply a default content setting that will apply to all newly created schedules. The schedule-level setting will take priority if both are set.

[L221] 

[L222] |  |  |

[L223] | --- | --- |

[L224] | **OptiSigns 1.0** | **OptiSigns 2.0** |

[L227] For detailed information, visit: [Assign Default Content for Schedules](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040943374)

[L230] Yes.┬Ā

[L231] 

[L232] On **OptiSigns 1.0** you can click on **Move Schedule** on the top of the Schedule menu and then select the folder you wish to move the schedule to. On **OptiSigns 2.0**, you'll need to select the schedule you wish to move, then hit the **Move to** button which appears. You may then select the folder to move the schedule to.
