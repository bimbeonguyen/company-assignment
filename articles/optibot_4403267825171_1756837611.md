[L1] # How to use the Social Mix App

[L2] 

[L3] The Social Mix app allows you to mix your posts from Instagram, Facebook, Twitter in a single social wall.

[L4] 

[L5] This article will guide you through how to create and use the Social Mix app.

[L16] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L17] 

[L18] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click┬á[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L19] 

[L20] Then log on to our portal:┬á<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L21] 

[L22] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add the Social Mix app to your Account.

[L23] 

[L24] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403348129811)

[L25] 

[L26] Enter your Social Mix information:

[L27] 

[L28] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4403274794003)

[L29] 

[L30] * Name: Name of your Social Mix Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will **not**┬ábe displayed on your screens.

[L31] * Add Source: Add the source for your social mix, you can bring in Facebook, Instagram Personal, Instagram Business.

[L32] * Display Mode: Select┬á[**Single Post View**](https://social-player.optisigns.com/instagram/?asset_id=y89t3dg76jewc7nec),┬á**[Social Wall View](https://social-player.optisigns.com/instagram/?asset_id=ry2oyv3hqui83njer)**, or **[Social Kiosk View](https://social-player.optisigns.com/instagram/?asset_id=0csdimtv6c6p2blg8)**, or **Bricks View**. Click on the links to see live example.

[L33] * Theme:┬áSelect Light or Dark Theme. You can also customize your background color if you select Custom.

[L34] 

[L35] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26482818362003)

[L36] 

[L37] * Speed: Select how fast you want the wall to scroll or switch between posts.  

[L38] You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[L39] 

[L40] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26482818369043)

[L41] 

[L42] * Font Size: Set the font size for the Social Mix app.

[L43] 

[L44] You can click Advanced and set:

[L45] 

[L46] * Filter Posts:┬áBy Time (i.e last 30 days) or by last XX number of posts.

[L47] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the Social Mix Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

[L48] * Continue Playing Content in Playlist: In the playlist, the Twitter app plays 10 items in the 1st round, and it will start with #11 item in the 2nd round.

[L49] * Show QR Code: Select whether you want the QR code to link to the Twitter Page URL or you can select a Custom URL which you can determine what type of code type you want to generate and link to.

[L50] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4404999008019)

[L51] 

[L52] ┬á

[L53] 

[L54] ┬á

[L55] 

[L56] Click Save.  

[L57] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app to fetch posts and prepare your wall, so please be patient.  

[L58]   

[L59] 

[L60] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L61] 

[L62] You have created your Social Mix Wall.  

[L63] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.┬á

[L64] 

[L65] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L66] 

[L67] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
