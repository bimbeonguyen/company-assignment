[L1] # How to Preview a Screen

[L2] 

[L3] You can always preview a screen before committing an assignment.

[L4] 

[L5] Visit the [Screen Management](https://app.optisigns.com/app/screenManagement) page, and find the screen you'd like to preview.

[L6] 

[L7] Click **Edit**, and the Edit Screen window will pop-up.

[L8] 

[L9] Simply click **Preview.**

[L10] 

[L11] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360046667794)

[L12] 

[L13] You can also preview any screens at any time by clicking on the **Preview** button in the list of screens:
