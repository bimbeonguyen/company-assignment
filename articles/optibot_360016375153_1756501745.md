[L1] # Assign content to your screens

[L2] 

[L3] Once you get screens added, you can start assigning, updating the content, or scheduling the content it plays.  

[L4] The steps below are for you to assign a playlist, but you can follow the same process to assign any assets or schedules. You can also schedule your content to go live.

[L5] 

[L6] ### 1) Assign content to your screen

[L7] 

[L8] To assign the playlist to your screen: Go to the **Screen** tab.

[L9] 

[L10] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315187359123)

[L11] 

[L12] Click "**Edit**" button on the screen you want to change.

[L13] 

[L14] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315221562387)

[L15] 

[L16] Click the Type drop-down list and select Playlist.

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315265234067)

[L19]  

[L20] 

[L21] Click the Selected Playlist drop-down and select the playlist you've created. In this case, we select "Lobby TV Playlist"

[L22] 

[L23] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315320625555)

[L24] 

[L25] Click **Save**

[L26] 

[L27] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315343621395)

[L28]  

[L29] 

[L30] That's all, the Screen will be updated.

[L31] 

[L32] ### 2) Schedule your content to go-live

[L33] 

[L34] After you select your content, you can click the **Schedule** button.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315402767763)

[L37] 

[L38] Then you can set up the schedule to go-live  

[L39] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/19315466202003)

[L40] 

[L41] * **Schedule go-live time**:

[L42] 	+ Set a time when you want the screens to start playing this content. The screen(s) will play this content from this time onward.

[L43] 	+ An Expiration feature will also be provided to stop the content that is temporarily assigned.

[L44] 

[L45] That's all, the Screen will be scheduled.
