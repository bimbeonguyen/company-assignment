[L1] # How to Use the Stock App

[L2] 

[L3] ### The Stock app allows you to display your choice of stock tickers on screen, or as part of a Split Screen configuration as a ticker.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Setting Up a Stock App](#SettingUp)

[L7] * [Deploying the Stock App](#Deploying)

[L8] 	+ [As a Single Asset](#Asset)

[L9] 	+ [As a Split Screen Ticker](#ScrollingStrip)

[L10] 

[L11] The Stock App allows you to display live ticker prices from the marketplace. Stock prices are updated every 15 minutes.

[L12] 

[L13] ---

[L14] 

[L15] ## What You'll Need

[L16] 

[L17] * An OptiSigns account - [**Standard Plan** or higher](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)

[L18] * An [OptiSigns-enabled device](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360021855653-What-hardware-and-devices-are-supported)

[L19] * A screen, [set up and paired with OptiSigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/18823504383891-OptiSigns-Getting-Started-Guide)

[L20]

[L22] 

[L23] ## Setting Up a Stock App

[L24] 

[L25] On the OptiSigns portal, go to **Assets ŌåÆ Add Asset ŌåÆ Apps**.

[L26] 

[L27] ![how to create asset optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213501459)

[L28] 

[L29] Select **Stock**:

[L30] 

[L31] ![stock app](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548189610515)

[L32] 

[L33] Set up your Stock app:

[L34] 

[L35] ![stock app interface optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213509395)

[L36] 

[L37] **Name -** The name of your Stock asset. This is for organizational purposes within OptiSigns only and will not display on your screens.

[L38] 

[L39] **Title -** Displayed Title that will show on screen. It will show above your list of ticker symbols.

[L40] 

[L41] **Stock Symbol -** Enter your ticker symbols. These appear as tags in the UI, and will determine which stocks are tracked onscreen:

[L42] 

[L43] ![stock symbols](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213515027)

[L44] 

[L45] **Theme -** Choose between Light and Dark themes, or upload your own Custom theme.

[L46] 

[L47] **Speed -** Choose the speed at which the ticker refreshes. Choose from **Slow**, **Medium**, **Fast**, or **Custom**. When Custom is chosen, you can adjust it via the below slider:

[L48] 

[L49] ![speed stock app optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213521043)

[L50]

[L52] 

[L53] ## Deploying a Stock App

[L54] 

[L55] You can deploy your new Stock app as an individual asset, or as part of a Split Screen as a ticker.

[L56] 

[L57] ### As an Asset

[L58] 

[L59] To get your new Stock asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

[L60] 

[L61] ![choosing screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213524499)

[L62] 

[L63] This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

[L64] 

[L65] ![optisigns edit screen](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548189626643)

[L66] 

[L67] Here, select **Asset** under Content type, then hit **Change** next to Selected Asset.

[L68] 

[L69] Then, select your created Stock Asset:

[L70] 

[L71] ![select stock asset optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213532307)

[L72] 

[L73] Now hit **Save**. Your Stock asset will now display on screen.

[L74] 

[L75] ### As a Scrolling Strip

[L76] 

[L77] Stock apps are most often wanted as a Scrolling Strip as part of a [**Split Screen app**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

[L78] 

[L79] To create this, you'll need to set up the Stock app as a Scrolling Strip. Let's get started.

[L80] 

[L81] On the OptiSigns portal, go to **Assets ŌåÆ Add Asset ŌåÆ Apps**.

[L82] 

[L83] ![add asset optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213501459)

[L84] 

[L85] Select **Scrolling Strip**:

[L86] 

[L87] ![select scrolling strip app optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213534483)

[L88] 

[L89] This will open up a **Scrolling Strip** app creation window. Select **Stock** under Type:

[L90] 

[L91] ![scrolling strip app stock](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213542163)

[L92] 

[L93] Then, select your created Stock Asset:

[L94] 

[L95] ![select stock asset optisigns](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/48548213532307)

[L96] 

[L97] Configure the rest of the app - see our article on [**How to Create a Scrolling Strip**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613-How-to-create-a-scrolling-strip-bars) for more information on these options.

[L98] 

[L99] That's it! It is now ready to be part of a Split Screen. See our article on [**Creating and Using Split Screens**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App) for more information on how to set that up.
