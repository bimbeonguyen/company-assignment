[L1] # How Can I Remove/Delete Uploaded Files or Assets?

[L2] 

[L3] 1. Hover the mouse on the asset in the **Files/Assets** tab.

[L4] 2. Click the vertical **... icon** on the asset/file you'd like to remove.

[L5] 3. Click **Delete.**

[L11] **Can I recover my deleted files? Does the app have a trash bin or recycle bin?**

[L12] 

[L13] No. Once the file is deleted, it's completely deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered. Please carefully consider your files and assets before deleting them from your account.
