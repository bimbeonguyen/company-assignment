[L1] # Show YouTube video's subtitle/closed caption

[L2] 

[L3] You can show YouTube video's subtitles on your OptiSigns Digital Signage player.

[L4] 

[L5] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360076614454)

[L6] 

[L7] Simply check the Caption box of your YouTube asset in OptiSigns.  

[L8] NOTE: The YouTube video needs to have closed caption generated by the video author. Auto-generated captions may not work (this is a YouTube limitation).┬Ā
