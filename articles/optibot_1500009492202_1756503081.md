[L1] # How to use On This Day app

[L2] 

[L3] On this day is a small trivia app getting highlights from Wikipedia's database of what happens on this date. Usually it's birthday or some holiday events.

[L4] 

[L5] ¬†

[L6] 

[L7] Here's how it look.  

[L8] \* Not all event has pictures  

[L9] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500014509322)

[L10] 

[L11] ¬†

[L12] 

[L13] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L14] 

[L15] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L16] 

[L17] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L18] 

[L19] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add On This Day on your Account.

[L20] 

[L21] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483772551955)

[L22] ¬†

[L23] 

[L24] Click On This Day:

[L25] 

[L26] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500014812621)

[L27] ¬†

[L28] Enter your On This Day app information:

[L29] 

[L30] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500014813721)

[L31] 

[L32] * Name: Name of your On This Day wall app, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will **not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L33] * Display Mode: Select **Kiosk, Social Wall, or Timeline** You can click Preview button to see how they look.

[L34] * Theme:¬†Select Light or Dark Theme.

[L35] * Font Size: Set the font size for the On This Day app.

[L36] 

[L37] Click Save.  

[L38] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app to fetch posts and prepare your wall, so please be patient.  

[L39]   

[L40] 

[L41] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L42] 

[L43] You have created your On This Day.  

[L44] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L45] 

[L46] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L47] 

[L48] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L49]
