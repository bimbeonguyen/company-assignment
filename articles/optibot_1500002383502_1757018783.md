[L1] # How to install OptiSigns as Screensaver on Roku

[L2] 

[L3] Updated Sep 2023. After Roku OS 12.0.0 updates, Roku is enforcing stricter to power management & screensaver.

[L4] 

[L5] Roku platform has a lot of restrictions. **In short, if you just need to display static menus or simple slideshows, and don't mind spending sometime setting it up, then it's OK.**

[L6] 

[L7] For better compatibility and reliability, we recommend a dedicated player like our [Android Stick Player](https://links.optisigns.com/szzk) or Google ChromeCast, or any [Windows, Mac, Linux](https://www.optisigns.com/download) devices.

[L8] 

[L9] В 

[L10] 

[L11] **How to install OptiSigns Player on Roku:**

[L12] 

[L13] There are 2 ways to install OptiSigns Player on Roku:

[L14] 

[L15] * Normal channel/app can play images, videos but cannot autostart

[L16] * [Screensaver](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500002383502) can only play images (Continue to read this article)

[L17] 

[L18] First, please update these two settings on your Roku device.

[L19] 

[L20] * Settings -> System -> Power -> Auto Power Saving -> uncheck "Reduce power after 15 minutes" and "Turn off after 4 hours"

[L21] * Settings -> Network -> Bandwidth saver -> Off

[L22] 

[L23] ### How to set the OptiSigns as Screensaver on Roku
