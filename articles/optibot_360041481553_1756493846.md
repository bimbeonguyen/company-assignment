[L1] # How to use Simple Calendar App

[L2] 

[L3] Simple Calendar App is a simple way to display a calendar for your office.  

[L4] You can also add a simple message on the calendar wall as well.  

[L5] Below is an example:

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055275573)

[L8] 

[L9] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L10] 

[L11] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L12] 

[L13] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L14] 

[L15] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App".

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360055275613)

[L18] 

[L19] Click Simple Calendar app:

[L20] 

[L21] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054404234)

[L22]  

[L23] Enter your Simple Calendar wall information:

[L24]  

[L25]  ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360054570894)

[L26] 

[L27] * Name: Name of your Simple Calendar Wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L28] * Text: Optional, if you want to put a message under the date.

[L29] * Start week on: Choose to start the week on Sunday or Monday

[L30] * Theme: Choose color theme from range of pre-built theme or customize your own with your own brand color

[L31] 

[L32] Click Save.  

[L33] After Saving, you can Preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app prepare your wall.  

[L34]   

[L35] 

[L36] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L37] 

[L38] You have created your Simple Calendar Wall.  

[L39] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab. 

[L40] 

[L41] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L42] 

[L43] You can put the created walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L44] 

[L45]
