[L1] # How to install OptiSigns as channel/app on Roku

[L2] 

[L3] |  |

[L4] | --- |

[L5] | Updated Sep 2023: After Roku OS 12.0.0 updates, Roku is more strict with power management and screensaver feature. |

[L6] 

[L7] Roku platform has a lot of restrictions. **In short, if you just need to display static menus or simple slideshows, and don't mind spending some time setting it up, then it's OK.**

[L8] 

[L9] For better compatibility and reliability, we recommend a dedicated player like our [Android Stick Player,](https://links.optisigns.com/szzk)┬ĀGoogle ChromeCast, or any [Windows, Mac, Linux](https://www.optisigns.com/download) devices.

[L10] 

[L11] ┬Ā

[L12] 

[L13] ### **How to Install OptiSigns Player on Roku:**

[L14] 

[L15] There are 2 ways to install OptiSigns Player on Roku:

[L16] 

[L17] * Normal channel/app can play images, videos, but it **cannot autostart** (Continue to read this article)

[L18] * [Screensaver](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500002383502) can **only play images**

[L19] 

[L20] #### **OptiSigns App on Roku Supports:**

[L21] 

[L22] * Images, Videos, Documents

[L23] * Canvas, Templates, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive apps

[L24] * Playlist, Schedule

[L25] * Portrait, Landscape mode, screen rotation

[L26] 

[L27] #### 

[L28] **Not supported:** (these are the limitations specific to Roku Platform)

[L29] 

[L30] * All other apps such as Social Media, Youtube, News, Streaming videos, etc.

[L31] * Split Screen display

[L32] * Roku has a small amount of storage, so be mindful of how much content you would like to cache on it

[L33] 

[L34] **Let's get started:**

[L35] 

[L36] First, please update these two settings on your Roku device.

[L37] 

[L38] * Settings > System > Power > Auto Power Saving > uncheck "Reduce power after 15 minutes" and "Turn off after 4 hours"

[L39] * Settings > Network > Bandwidth saver > Off

[L40] 

[L41] ### **To install OptiSigns on Roku, there are 2 high level steps:**

[L42] 

[L43] 1. Install OptiSigns channel/app on your Roku

[L44] 2. Pair your screen with┬Ā[OptiSign portal](https://app.optisigns.com/) and start assigning content to it

[L45] 

[L46] #### **1) Install OptiSigns channel/app on your Roku**

[L47] 

[L48] There are 2 ways to install OptiSigns.

[L49] 

[L50] 1. Add the Optisigns channel to your account:┬Ā<https://my.roku.com/add/optisigns>  

[L51] And add it to your devices.

[L52] 2. Search for Optisigns on your Roku device  

[L53] ┬ĀOn your Roku TV, Search for OptiSigns Channel.

[L54] 

[L55] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360058620454)

[L56] 

[L57] Add the Channel

[L58] 

[L59] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360058620534)

[L60] 

[L61] First time when launched, you will see this screen:  

[L62] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360058622134)

[L63] 

[L64] #### **2) Pair your screen with OptiSigns Portal and start assigning content to it**

[L65] 

[L66] Now you are ready to go to our portal at: <https://app.optisigns.com/>

[L67] 

[L68] Pair the screen using your screen's code and start assigning content to it.

[L69] For detailed steps click┬Ā[here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016374813).

[L70] 

[L71] ┬Ā

[L72] 

[L73] #### Optional:

[L74] 

[L75] If you hear a periodic clicking sound once the app is running, this is the simulation of a button click to prevent your display from re-engaging while content is being displayed.

[L76] 

[L77] You can remove the click sound in the Roku settings.

[L78] 

[L79] Go to Settings > Audio > Menu Volume. Set this to **Off**.
