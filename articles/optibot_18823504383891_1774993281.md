[L27] #### Pick one of the 3 ways below to get OptiSigns running on your TV:

[L28] 

[L29] 1. The easiest way to get started is to get your favorite brand of TV and get our[**OptiSigns OptiStick**](https://shop.optisigns.com/products/optisigns-android-stick-player-2) to run OptiSigns on it. Follow [**this guide**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/27267311796243) to learn how to set up your OptiSigns Player

[L30] 2. Already have a TV or Signage Player? Go to our[**Downloads**](https://www.optisigns.com/download) page to see if our app can be installed on it.

[L31] 3. Not ready for hardware purchase yet? Just download the [**Windows Player app**](https://links.optisigns.com/win) or [**MacOS Player app**](https://links.optisigns.com/mac) and use your laptop as test screen.

[L32] 

[L33] Once OptiSigns App is installed, **launch it**.┬Ā

[L34] 

[L35] The app will ask if you want to automatically start when the device starts up.

[L36] 

[L37] Then, you will see this **pairing screen:**

[L38]

[L40] 

[L41] Go to the OptiSigns portal: <https://app.optisigns.com/>

[L42] 

[L43] Once logged in, click **Add Screen (1.0)** or **New Screen (2.0).**

[L46] |  |  |

[L47] 

[L48] A pop-up screen will appear, type in the **Pair Code** showing up on your screen. Click **Pair**.

[L51] |  |  |

[L52]

[L80] ## **Push Content to Your Screen**

[L81] 

[L82] Go back to the **Screens**page, and click **Edit**on your preferred screen. You can also click the **screen's name** on the new UI.

[L83] 

[L84] | **OptiSigns 1.0** | **Updated** |

[L85] |  |  |

[L86] 

[L87] For┬Ā**Type**, select your desired content: asset, playlist or schedule. For this example, we chose an **Asset.**

[L88] 

[L89] Then, select your desired content from the drop down menu.

[L90] 

[L91] | **OptiSigns 1.0** | **Updated** |

[L92] |  | ┬Ā |

[L93]
