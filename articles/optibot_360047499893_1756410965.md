[L1] # How to use Google Traffic App

[L2] 

[L3] You can use OptiSigns to show Traffic around your locations, this could be helpful for your employees, customers, visitors as they are coming in and out of your location.

[L4] 

[L5] Here's an example how it would look.¬†

[L6] 

[L7] ![mceclip6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360082037654)

[L8] 

[L9] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L10] 

[L11] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L12] 

[L13] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L14] 

[L15] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add Google Traffic app to your Account.

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360068828173)

[L18] ¬†

[L19] 

[L20] Click Google Traffic:

[L21] 

[L22] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360067714994)

[L23] 

[L24] ¬†After that, you can enter information for your Google Traffic app.

[L25] 

[L26] * Name: Name of your Google Traffic asset, this is the name in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L27] * Location: Center of the map where you want to display traffic around it. Note: Traffic information will be updated every 15 min.

[L28] * Zoom: select zoom level for the map view. We recommend keeping it as 10 - 16. This should give a good coverage for a city, metro area.

[L29] * Show Markers (Optional): if you want to show the market for the Location you selected above.

[L30] 

[L31] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360082036614)

[L32] 

[L33] After Saving, you can Preview the Google Traffic app.  

[L34]   

[L35] 

[L36] ## **That's all! Congratulations!**

[L37] 

[L38] You have created your Google Traffic app.  

[L39] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L40] 

[L41] You can assign the newly created instance to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L42] 

[L43] You can put the created social walls in a Playlist and/or Schedule too.
