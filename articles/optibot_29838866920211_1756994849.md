[L1] # How to add Google Sheets as a DataSource for OptiSync

[L2] 

[L3] #### Using our new OptiSync feature in Designer, you can add your Google Sheets to your DataSources and apply to your designs or our prebuilt Repeater Templates or Components. For a breakdown on OptiSync, please visit our guide **[here](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/29217646663187)**.

[L9] ## Adding Your Google Sheets as a DataSource

[L10] 

[L11] There are two different methods to adding Google Sheets to your DataSources:

[L12] 

[L13] #### **Method 1: Through Designer**

[L14] 

[L15] * On the Files/Assets page, select **Designer** from the Apps

[L16] * Select **DataSource** on the side menu, then **Add DataSource**

[L17] * Click **Google Sheets**

[L18] * Copy and Paste your Google Sheets URL into the provided text box, then **Import Data**

[L19] 	+ Note: You will be prompted to sign into your Google

[L20] * Once your Google Sheets is imported into a table in our portal, click **Save**

[L21] * **Name** your DataSource something easily recognizable

[L22] * Select your Synchronization and Update Interval

[L23] * Click **Done**

[L24] 

[L25] **Method 2: Through Account Settings**

[L26] 

[L27] * In our portal, select your account name, then **More**, then **DataSources**

[L28] * Select **Google Sheets**from the list of options

[L29] * Copy and Paste your Google Sheets URL into the provided text box, then **Import Data**

[L30] 	+ Note: You will be prompted to sign into your Google

[L31] * Once your Google Sheets is imported into a table in our portal, click **Update**

[L32] * **Name** your DataSource something easily recognizable

[L33] * Select your Synchronization and Update Interval

[L34] * Click **Done**

[L35] * Your **DataSource** will now be listed within your Advanced Account Settings

[L36] 

[L37] **Synchronization**

[L38] 

[L39] * **Only import once:** This option imports the data only once when you add the DataSource. After the initial import, the data will not be updated automatically.

[L40] * **Periodic direct access:** This option regularly fetches the latest data directly from the device through your gateway, providing "live access" to the data and ensuring you have the most up-to-date information available directly from the device.

[L41] 	+ *This is better for more consistent update intervals, but could cause performance issues.*

[L42] * **Periodic background sync:** This option periodically syncs data to your server in the background at regular intervals, reducing the need for direct queries to the device.
