[L1] # Changing screen's orientation (portrait & landscape)

[L2] 

[L3] OptiSigns support rotation of your screens:

[L4] 

[L5] * Landscape (0 Degree)

[L6] * Rotate 90 Degrees

[L7] * Rotate 180 Degrees

[L8] * Rotate 270 Degrees

[L9] 

[L10] Of course, for best display experience, please ensure you have content that would fit the portrait modes. Our apps are designed to fit portrait mode as well.
