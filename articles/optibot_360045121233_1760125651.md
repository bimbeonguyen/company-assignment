[L1] # How to use the New York Times app

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this article, we'll explain how to use the Guardian News app on OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] * [What You'll Need](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040991534#WhatYouNeed)

[L6] * [Creating a The New York Times News Asset](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040991534#Creating)

[L7] 

[L8] The OptiSigns New York Times app allows you to display news from The New York Times on your digital signs. It covers the most up-to-date Top Stories, World news, Business, Sports, Economy, Culture, Lifestyle news and more.

[L9] 

[L10] QR Codes are displayed, which a viewer can scan with their phone to read the relevant article. The New York Times News can also be displayed as [Scrolling Strip.](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360026559613)

[L21] * An OptiSigns [**Free plan or higher**](https://www.optisigns.com/pricing)**\***

[L28] ## Creating a The New York Times News Asset

[L29] 

[L30] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L31] 

[L32] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L33] 

[L34] Go to **Files/Assets**, Click on "App" and add┬Ā**The New York Times**┬Āon your Account.

[L35] 

[L36] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517288290451)

[L37] 

[L38] ┬Ā

[L39] 

[L40] Click **The New York Times**:

[L41] 

[L42] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517312106771)

[L43] 

[L44] Enter your The New York Times Wall App information:

[L45] 

[L46] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517288293779)

[L47] 

[L48] * Name: Name of your The New York Times Wall app, this is the name of the app in your asset list. It will **not** be displayed on your screens.

[L49] * Category: Select the region and category that you want to focus on. You can check as many as you want.

[L50] 

[L51] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517312109843)

[L52] 

[L53] * Theme: Select Light or Dark Theme.

[L54] * Speed: Select how fast you want the app to scroll or switch between posts. You can also customize your speed if you select Custom.

[L55] 

[L56] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517288297363)

[L57] 

[L58] * Font Size: Set the font size for the The New York Times Wall app.

[L59] 

[L60] Clicking┬Ā**Advanced**opens up additional options:

[L61] 

[L62] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517288298515)

[L63] 

[L64] * Date Format: Choose from a set of pre-formatted dates, or select a custom date format:  

[L65] ┬Ā

[L66] 

[L67] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/45517288300051)

[L68] 

[L69] * Scrolling Strip Font Size: If you put the The New York Times News Wall app to a SplitScreen zone that is a scrolling strip, you can set how big you want the scrolling text to be.

[L70] 

[L71]   

[L72] After you've configured the New York Times News app to your liking, hit **Save**. Your New York Times app will appear as an Asset in OptiSigns, and it can now be directly assigned to a screen or used in a Playlist. This asset can then be edited at any time from the Files/Assets tab.
