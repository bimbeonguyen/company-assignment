[L1] # How to put ZenDesk Dashboard on your signs

[L2] 

[L3] This article will guide you through how to create and use a Zendesk App.

[L4] 

[L5] ## Let's jump in and get started:

[L6] 

[L7]  Go to App screen and select Zendesk:

[L8] 

[L9] ![zendesk-1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360099562894)

[L10] 

[L11] Fill out:

[L12] 

[L13] * Name: name of your Zendesk App, this is only for use in File/Asset list, it will not be displayed on your screen.

[L14] * URL: the URL link to the Zendesk dashboard you want to display.

[L15] * Update interval: default is 10 minutes. This means the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes in your presentation. If you have a lot of slides, you may want to change this interval to something longer.

[L16] 

[L17] ![zendesk-2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360099562874)

[L18] 

[L19] Click Save to save your asset.

[L20] 

[L21] ## That's all!

[L22] 

[L23] Congratulation! You have created your Zendesk App.

[L24] 

[L25] On your device, you will need to enter in username and password to log in.  

[L26] If you are using FireStick, you can use the [Fire TV Remote app on your phone](https://www.lifewire.com/use-phone-as-remote-control-for-amazon-fire-tv-stick-4571277) to make it easier to enter password, or connect up a [Bluetooth keyboard, mouse](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360037391854-How-to-display-content-inside-websites-that-require-login-).

[L27] 

[L28] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L29] 

[L30] You can assign the newly created app to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L31] 

[L32] You can put the walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L33]
