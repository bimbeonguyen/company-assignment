[L1] # How to use Airtable with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] *Airtable* is a platform for building collaborative applications. You can display it on your Digital Signage screen with the OptiSigns.

[L4] 

[L5] You can view an example here:  

[L6] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/9392530973331)

[L7] 

[L8] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L9] 

[L10] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click┬Ā[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L11] 

[L12] Then log on to our portal:┬Ā<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L13] 

[L14] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App" and add Airtable on your Account.

[L15] 

[L16] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/9392427830163)

[L17] 

[L18] Enter details for your Airtable:

[L19] 

[L20] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/9392405153555)

[L21] 

[L22] * Name: for you to remember, you can use the same Airtable name.

[L23] * Embed: Paste in your Embed Code.

[L24] * Update Interval: Default is 600 seconds (10 minutes). This mean the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes in your Airtable. If you have a lot of slides, you may want to change this interval to something longer.

[L25] 

[L26] Note: Here is how to get the Embed code from your Airtable.┬Ā  

[L27] Click "**Share view**" button and click "**<>Embed this viewon your site**"┬Ā  

[L28] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/9392485841299)  

[L29]   

[L30] 

[L31] Then you can edit the width or height in the Embed code. Then Copy and Paste it the OptiSigns' Airtable app.  

[L32] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/9392446643731)

[L33] 

[L34] Click Save.

[L35] 

[L36] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L37] 

[L38] You have created your Airtalbe app.  

[L39] You can change it any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.┬Ā

[L40] 

[L41] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L42] 

[L43] You can put the Airtalbe app in a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L44]
