[L1] # MacOS

[L2] 

[L3] ### In this guide, we will walk you through the end-to-end process to install OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your macOS. You can easily use your Mac as a test screen. Follow the steps below:

[L4] 

[L5] ---

[L6] 

[L7] ## **Here are the high-level steps:**

[L8] 

[L9] 1) **Download** & **Set up** OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your Mac.  

[L10]   

[L11] 2) Use OptiSigns' web portal to assign content and manage your screens

[L12] 

[L13] #### So, let's dive in!

[L14] 

[L15] ---

[L16] 

[L17] ## Download & Set up OptiSigns Digital Signage App on your Mac.

[L18] 

[L19] To install the macOS-compatible download for the app, click **[here](https://links.optisigns.com/mac).**

[L20] 

[L21] Once downloaded, **Right-click** and select "**Open**"

[L22] 

[L23] ![1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085379074)

[L24] 

[L25] Drag ŌĆ£**OptiSigns Digital Signage**ŌĆØ to Applications.

[L26] 

[L27] ![2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086568693)

[L28] 

[L29] In your Spotlight, enter ŌĆ£**OptiSigns Digital Signage**ŌĆØ and click it.

[L30] 

[L31] ![3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085379094)

[L32] 

[L33] Select ŌĆ£**Open**ŌĆØ

[L34] 

[L35] ![4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085379154)

[L36] 

[L37] The app will run in full-screen mode, and generate a pairing code for you to pair your screen with the [app.optisigns.com](http://app.optisigns.com/) portal. If you move the mouse around, you will see 3 buttons in the top right-hand corner to **resize**, **open** **side** **bar** **menu** or **close** the app.

[L38] 

[L39] ![2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085363834)

[L40] 

[L41] On the left side menu, you can set Orientation, etc. The app has AutoStart and Fullscreen on Startup checked as default. So now, next time the Mac started up, it will run the OptiSigns app automatically.

[L42] 

[L43] ![3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085364994)

[L44] 

[L45] You now can go to our portal at:┬Ā<https://app.optisigns.com/>┬Āto pair the screen.

[L46] 

[L47] ![4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086554593)

[L48] 

[L49] Once you log in, click the "**Add screen**" button.

[L50] 

[L51] ![5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086554613)

[L52] 

[L53] In this pop-up, type in the Pair Code showing up on the OptiSigns App > click **Pair**.

[L54] 

[L55] ![6.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360085365134)

[L56] 

[L57] The Optisigns App will change to:

[L58] 

[L59] ![7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360086554793)

[L61] Now you are ready to upload and assign content.
