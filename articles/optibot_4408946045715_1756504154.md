[L1] # How to Integrate Bonusly Digital Signage with Optisigns

[L2] 

[L3] Some companies use [Bonusly](bonus.ly)¬†for internal communication channel and like to show the Messages sent between agents on a signage across their work space.¬†

[L4] 

[L5] ¬†

[L6] 

[L7] In this article we will show you how to use Bonusly with OptiSigns Digital Signage.

[L8] 

[L9] First step is to¬†open a website app on your Optisigns account, Here is [how to use Website app](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016382473-How-to-use-a-Website-link-with-OptiSigns).

[L10] 

[L11] Here are the steps:

[L12] 

[L13] Navigate to Files / Assets page, then click on Apps.

[L14] 

[L15] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.02.20_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408944989075)

[L16] 

[L17] ¬†

[L18] 

[L19] Navigate to Website App and open the app:

[L20] 

[L21] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.04.43_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408952589459)

[L22] 

[L23] Copy this URL and paste in the URL section of your website app:  

[L24] "https://bonus.ly/company/digital\_signage?access\_token=95ab0f0554b796b875216b25588936c"

[L25] 

[L26] ¬†

[L27] 

[L28] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.11.03_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408945206035)

[L29] 

[L30] ¬†

[L31] 

[L32] Next go to your [Bonusly](Bonus.ly) account page and navigate to INTEGRATIONS page

[L33] 

[L34] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_3.42.34_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408951958419)

[L35] 

[L36] ¬†

[L37] 

[L38] Navigate to ATOM bonus feed

[L39] 

[L40] ¬†

[L41] 

[L42] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.14.08_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408952720147)

[L43] 

[L44] ¬†

[L45] 

[L46] In this section Grab The API Access token Specific to your business page¬†

[L47] 

[L48] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_6.41.12_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408961499539)

[L49] 

[L50] ¬†

[L51] 

[L52] Next go to the Optisigns app and replace the "?access\_token=xxxxx" code after the URL in the website app.

[L53] 

[L54] The whole URL SHOuld look something like this:  

[L55] "https://bonus.ly/company/digital\_signage?access\_token=95ab0f0554b796b875216b25588936c"

[L56] 

[L57] Then click Save.

[L58] 

[L59] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.16.46_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408952864787)

[L60] 

[L61] ¬†

[L62] 

[L63] That's it!

[L64] 

[L65] ¬†

[L66] 

[L67] Now you can use the website app and assign it to your screens to show the [Bonusly](Bonus.ly) signage updates.

[L68] 

[L69] ![Screen_Shot_2021-10-20_at_4.45.56_PM.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/4408945771411)

[L70] 

[L71] ¬†
