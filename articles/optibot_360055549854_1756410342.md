[L1] # Pre-configure Wifi with OptiSigns devices

[L2] 

[L3] When ordering devices from OptiSigns you can put in WiFi information & credentials and the device will be configured with that WiFi detail before shipped out.

[L4] 

[L5] This is very useful and will save you time in deployment.

[L6] 

[L7] To do this, just enter WiFi when you order the device from:

[L8] 

[L9] <https://app.optisigns.com/app/s/order-device>
