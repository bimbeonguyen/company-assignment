[L1] # How to Fix The Scaling Issue on OptiSigns Android Device

[L2] 

[L3] If you encounter any issues with displaying the correct text size or graph size using the OptiSigns Android Stick, following these steps should help resolve your problem.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] Here is an example of Different Scaling Views:

[L8] 

[L9] * **160dpi Display View:**

[L10] 

[L11] ![Screenshot 2024-03-26 170334.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27822258702483)

[L12] 

[L13] * **480dpi Display View**:

[L14] 

[L15] ![Screenshot 2024-03-26 170635.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27822196127891)

[L16] 

[L17] This article guides you on how to change the OptiSigns Android Device Scaling Settings.

[L18] 

[L19]  

[L20] 

[L21] 1. Go to the **Settings**

[L22] 

[L23] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608849125779)

[L24] 

[L25] 2. Click **Android Settings**

[L26] 

[L27] ![annotely_image (8).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608838868243)

[L28] 

[L29] 3. Click **Extra Settings**

[L30] 

[L31] ![annotely_image (9).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608838883603)

[L32] 

[L33] 4. Click **Display**

[L34] 

[L35] ![annotely_image (10).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608838907027)

[L36] 

[L37] 5. Select **Display Size**

[L38] 

[L39] ![annotely_image (12).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608838920083)

[L40] 

[L41] 6. Click the right arrow on your remote until the **-** sign is highlighted and you can change the Default dpi Settings.

[L42] 

[L43] ![annotely_image (13).jpeg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/27608838930963)

[L44] 

[L45] After completing these steps, you can relaunch the OptiSigns app. Adjusting the settings should allow you to achieve the desired scaling on your screen.

[L46]
