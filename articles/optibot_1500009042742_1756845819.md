[L1] # FireOS TVs (Amazon, Insigna, Toshiba, TCL)

[L2] 

[L3] |  |

[L4] | --- |

[L5] | As of Feb 2024, FireStick and Fire TV are **no longer recommended** as digital signage player. This is due to **[Amazon eliminating the ability to auto start app](https://www.aftvnews.com/amazon-blocks-long-running-fire-tv-capability-breaking-popular-apps-with-no-warning-and-giving-developers-the-runaround/)** with FireStick & Fire TVs starting FireOS 8. |

[L6] 

[L7] There are workarounds with [ADB commands](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/23274673797139) or manually to start the app.

[L8] However, those workarounds are inconvenient, and we believe without an easy way to AutoStart apps, it is not a good solution for a Digital Signage player.

[L9] 

[L10] ┬Ā

[L11] If you have older model of TVs with Amazon FireOS 7 or lower, you can continue with this article to get it set up.

[L12] ## **Here are the high level steps:**

[L13] 

[L14] 1) Download OptiSigns Digital Signage from Amazon App Store

[L15] 

[L16] 2) Using OptiSigns' web portal to assign content and remotely manage your screens

[L17] 

[L18] So let's dive in!

[L19] 

[L20] ### **1) Download & Set up OptiSigns Digital Signage from Amazon App Store**

[L21] 

[L22] Press the┬Ā**Search**┬Āicon on the┬Ā**Find**┬Āsection of the Fire TV,

[L23] 

[L24] ![Fire_Stick-12.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399157505811)

[L25] 

[L26] Type in "OptiSigns".  

[L27] Fire TV Search can be tricky to use, and search as you type sometimes does not work properly.  

[L28] After you typed "optisigns", be sure to press the menu down button until the "Optisigns" text is highlighted and then click on it.

[L29] 

[L30] ![Fire_Stick-13.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399157512211)

[L31] Click **Download** on the OptiSigns app icon

[L32] 

[L33] ![Fire_Stick-14.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399157514387)

[L34] 

[L35] Once the app is installed, **launch** it.

[L36] 

[L37] The app will ask if you want to automatically start when device start up. It recommended to select┬Ā**yes**, unless you want to launch the app yourself every time manually.

[L38] 

[L39] You then will see this pairing screen:

[L40] 

[L41] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399157527059)

[L42] 

[L43] Go to our website:┬Ā[https://app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/app/screenManagement)

[L44] 

[L45] If you don't have an account already, create one, or you can also log in with Google or Facebook account.

[L46] 

[L47] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399172852627)Once you logged in, click "**Add screen"** button

[L48] ┬Ā

[L49] 

[L50] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399172854931)

[L51] In this pop up, type in the Pair Code showing up on your Fire TV screen. Then, click **Pair**:

[L52] ┬Ā

[L53] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399157541779)

[L54] 

[L55] The Fire TV screen will change to:

[L56] 

[L57] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26399172862355)

[L58] 

[L59] Now you are ready to upload and assign content.

[L60]
