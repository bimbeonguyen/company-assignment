[L1] # How to keep OptiSigns running on Fire Stick

[L2] 

[L3] If you have experience with the OptiSigns app will go to sleep mode after 4 hours on the Fire Stick, you can follow this article to turn off the **Still Watching** feature.

[L4] 

[L5]  

[L6] 

[L7] 1. Press the **Home button**, and navigate to **Settings**menu

[L8] 

[L9] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18470989801875)

[L10] 

[L11] 2. Select the **Preferences** menu

[L12] 

[L13] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18471006265875)

[L14] 

[L15] 3. Click on the arrow down and go to **Data Usage Monitoring**

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18470994943507)

[L18] 

[L19] 4. Then go to **Still Watching**

[L20] 

[L21] **![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18470957779603)**

[L22] 

[L23] 5. Click it to turn it **OFF**

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip4.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/18471006271123)

[L26] 

[L27] Then you can relaunch the OptiSigns app. It will not pop out the warning message.

[L28]
