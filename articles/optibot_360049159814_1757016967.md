[L1] # How to use Walls.io App

[L2] 

[L3] You can display walls.io social media walls on your digital signs using OptiSigns Wall.io app.  

[L4] Simply by copying the walls.io URL and put it on OptiSigns Walls.io app.

[L5] 

[L6] If you prefer not to have the walls.io brand bar on top, you can use walls.io's embed code with our [Embed App](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360035815114).

[L7] 

[L8] You can read more below for details steps by steps guide.

[L9] 

[L10] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073358514)

[L11] 

[L12] ¬†

[L13] 

[L14] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L15] 

[L16] Go go Walls.io, view your social walls and copy the URL like below.

[L17] 

[L18] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360074557813)

[L19] 

[L20] You will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click¬†[here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L21] 

[L22] Then log on to our portal:¬†<http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L23] 

[L24] Go to Files/Assets, Click on¬†**Apps**.

[L25] 

[L26] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073378854)

[L27] 

[L28] Select the Walls.io App.

[L29] 

[L30] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073383534)

[L31] 

[L32] Enter the details needed.

[L33] 

[L34] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360073383554)

[L35] 

[L36] * Name: Identification of your wall, this is the name of the wall in your asset list. It will¬†**not**¬†be displayed on your screens.

[L37] * URL Paste the URL of your walls.io here.

[L38] * Update interval:¬†How often your wall should be refreshed. Walls.io automatically update your walls, so it's not critical to set this parameter low. 600 seconds is recommended.

[L39] 

[L40] Click¬†**Save**.

[L41] 

[L42]   

[L43] After Saving, you can preview the wall. It may take several seconds for the app prepare your wall.  

[L44]   

[L45] 

[L46] ## **That's all! Congratulations!**

[L47] 

[L48] You have created your Walls.io.  

[L49] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.¬†

[L50] 

[L51] You can assign the newly created wall to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L52] 

[L53] You can put the created walls of Playlists and Schedules too.
