[L1] # Stretch your images, document - Stretch vs Fit vs Zoom your content

[L2] 

[L3] In general, we recommend you to upload images, document that will fit the size of your screens.  

[L4] Most likely your big screens TV will have resolution of: 1920 x 1080 pixels (Full HD) or 3840 x 2160 pixels (4K).

[L5] 

[L6] However, if you upload an image or document that is smaller than your screen's resolution. You can select to Fit or Stretch or Zoom it so that it can fill the screen.
