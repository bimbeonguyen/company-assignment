[L35] ## Option 1: Setting Up a Private Sheet

[L36] 

[L37] ***This option is useful if you have private information on your Sheet or want more access and customization of your Sheet in OptiSigns.***

[L38] 

[L39] In Google Sheets, **open the Google Sheet** you'd like to import **ŌåÆ Copy the URL**

[L40] 

[L41] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/33979909649171)

[L42] 

[L43] Return to your Google Sheets app in the OptiSigns portal.┬Ā

[L44] 

[L45] **Name** your asset ŌåÆ **Paste the Sheet's URL** into the URL box ŌåÆ Click **Next**

[L46] 

[L47] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/33979909650835)

[L48] 

[L49] A **"Sign**┬Ā**In With Google"** box will appear beneath the URL box, click on it.

[L50] 

[L51] ![chrome_QH2y6SgYtm.jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/33979969425299)

[L52] 

[L53] A Google authorization pop-up window will appear ŌåÆ Follow the steps to authenticate your account connected to the Google Sheets ŌåÆ Click **Continue**

[L54] 

[L55] ![chrome_eX5jtfgERa.jpg](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/33979909659027)

[L56] 

[L57] Once permission is granted, you can **customize** and **set up** your Google Sheet asset:

[L58] 

[L59] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/34038249680403)

[L60] 

[L61] * **Select Sheet:** Allows you to select a single sheet from within the Google Sheet to display

[L62] 	+ ***Reload Sheets:*** This will refresh the data to get an updated list Sheets available within that entire document for you to choose from.

[L63] * **Customize Display Region:** Allows you to input the Start Cell and End Cell to select the region shown on your display.

[L64] 	+ *This **cannot** be unselected or selected once the asset is first saved. If selected upon creating the app, you can continuously adjust the display region whenever you'd like.*

[L65] * **Scale:** Adjust how you'd like the Sheet to scale and display on the screen.

[L66] * **Orientation:** Choose between Landscape or Portrait

[L67] * **Speed:** Adjusts the speed at which data transitions.

[L68] 	+ ***Slow or 5:*** 19 seconds

[L69] 	+ ***Medium or 10:*** 14 seconds

[L70] 	+ ***Fast or 15:*** 9 seconds

[L71] * **Force Sync Interval:** System will force sync at the hour interval you select. (*Minimum: 1 hour*)

[L72] 

[L73] Once done, Click **Save.**

[L126] ## Limitations

[L127] 

[L128] * **Update Intervals:**

[L129] 	+ **Private Sheet:**Google will notify our system when you update your Sheet, and we will reload the information in as little as 5 minutes, up to one hour (or whenever you set your Force Sync Interval).

[L130] 	+ **Published Sheet:** Google updates the webpage automatically every 5 minutes. Your screens should then update in around 5-10 minutes, up to the Force Sync interval (minimum 1 hour).

[L131] * **Hidden Sheet:** If you try to import a hidden Sheet, we will not be able to access the content. This will cause the Preview window or Screens to display as black.

[L132] * **Custom Display Region:**

[L133] 	+ Currently, it is only supported for Private Sheet.

[L134] 	+ This must be set up during the creation of the asset. Once this is selected for an asset, it cannot be unselected. Or, if you do not select it upon creating the asset, you cannot select it later.

[L135] 	+ If you set up Custom Display Region, you can always adjust the inputted cells and scaling.
