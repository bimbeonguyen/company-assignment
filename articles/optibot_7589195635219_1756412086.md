[L1] # Using eyedrop feature to select color in Designer app

[L2] 

[L3] Sometimes you need to select a color from your design or other sources to be used in your design

[L4] 

[L5] You can do so in Designer.

[L6] 

[L7] When you are using a brush, object drawing, background color selection, etc.

[L8] 

[L9] Click the Color Palate button, then click the Eyedrop button.

[L10] 

[L11] Your cursor will turn into an eyedrop crosshair. You can select anywhere on your desktop, even in a different window, applications on your desktop.

[L12] 

[L13] ┬Ā

[L14] 

[L15] After that, you can use this color anywhere on your Design.

[L16]
