[L86] ## Seamless and Secure Integration Between OptiDev and OptiSigns

[L87] 

[L88] OptiSigns and OptiDev share security frameworks, meaning it is possible to publish OptiDev projects to OptiSigns without ever making the project public. Because of this shared authentication framework, you can securely connect both platforms using the **same login credentials**.

[L89] 

[L90] This is a big deal, as it means you can publish OptiDev apps and assets directly to OptiSigns *without exposing them publicly*. It will only be accessible within your OptiSigns environment, which is capable of being restricted by [User](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046356113-Advanced-Security-Managing-User-Roles), [Folder](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360044600474-Folder-Level-Permissions), or [Team](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360034883113-Working-with-Teams-and-Security-Levels). No public URL will ever need to be generated, meaning your internal dashboards, tools, and business applications remain secure and ***only accessible by authorized users within your organization.***

[L91] 

[L92] To use this feature, you will need a Standard subscription on the OptiDev platform.
