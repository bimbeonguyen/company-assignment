[L1] # How to use Waitwhile App

[L2] 

[L3] Waitwhile is a great platform to display and manage your in location waitlist and more.  

[L4] Customers, visitors can sign up for a waitlist by scanning QR code (or with links you text them even before they arrived).  

[L5] Then you can use OptiSigns to display this waitlist on your digital signs.

[L6] 

[L7] For more info about how Waitwhile work watch their [2min video](https://youtu.be/XkUt0b9LeBs) or visit [their website here.](https://waitwhile.com/)

[L8] 

[L9] ![hero image](https://waitwhile.com/assets/images/hero-image/Hero_Image.png)

[L10] 

[L11] To put Waitwhile List on your Digital Signs.

[L12] 

[L13] Go to┬Ā[app.optisigns.com](https://app.optisigns.com/).

[L14] 

[L15] Click Files/Assets, then click Apps

[L16] 

[L17] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/26483773496595)

[L18] 

[L19] Then click the Waitwhile app

[L20] 

[L21] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/11783716359571)

[L22] 

[L23] Set up your Waitwhile App:

[L24] 

[L25] ![mceclip1.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/11783728605203)

[L26] 

[L27] * Name: name of your Waitwhile App, this is only for use in File/Asset list, it will not be displayed on your screen.

[L28] * Waitlist URL: the URL of your Waitlist.  

[L29] 

[L30] 	+ To get your Waitlist URL, login to your <https://waitwhile.com/> account, click Settings > Waitlist and copy your Waitlist URL.

[L31] 

[L32] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/11784045008147)

[L33] 

[L34] * Update interval: default is 10 minutes. This means the app will refresh the link every 10 mins for any changes.

[L35] * Auto Scroll: Toggle the auto scroll so that all your Waitlist is viewable.

[L36] 	+ Scroll Type: You can select

[L37] 		- * + Down & Up: the app will scroll the web page down, and then scroll up.

[L38] 				+ Scroll to: the app will jump to a certain location on the web page

[L39] 	+ Speed: how fast you want to scroll, you can also customize the speed.

[L40] 	+ Page Height: enter the number in Pixel. This is needed because the app does not know how long your Waitlist is.

[L41] * Zoom: zoom may help the content to be displayed better on your screens.

[L42] * Show QR Code: Display the QR code for your Join Waitlist URL.

[L43] 	+ To get your Join Waitlist URL, login to your <https://waitwhile.com/>account, click Settings > Waitlist and copy your Join Waitlist URL.

[L44] 

[L45] ![mceclip3.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/11784139517843)

[L46] 

[L47] ┬Ā

[L48] 

[L49] * + QR Code Position: position of QR Code.

[L50] 	+ QR Code Size: size of QR Code.

[L51] 

[L52] Click Save to save your asset.

[L53] 

[L54] You can preview how it look by clicking the "Preview button".

[L55] 

[L56] ┬Ā

[L57] 

[L58] ## That's all!

[L59] 

[L60] Congratulation! You have created your Waitwhile App.

[L61] 

[L62] You can change the wall any time by click on it in the Files/Assets tab.

[L63] 

[L64] ┬Ā

[L65] You can assign the newly created app to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L66] 

[L67] You can put the walls in a Playlist, Schedule too.
