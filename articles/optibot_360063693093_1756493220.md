[L1] # How to use Approval Workflow feature

[L2] 

[L3] With OptiSigns Pro or Enterprise Plan, you can set up Approval Workflow so that you can have:

[L4] 

[L5] 1) Contributors in your organization that can create, upload contents, make changes to Playlists

[L6] 

[L7] 2) Then submit to Admin or Approver to review & approve (email notification available)

[L8] 

[L9] 3) Once approved, the changes will be applied to your screens immediately

[L10] 

[L11] ┬Ā

[L12] 

[L13] **To demonstrate an example,** we will create 2 users:

[L14] 

[L15] * John the Proposer - who will add some content to a playlist and submit for approval

[L16] * Jane the Approver - who will review the changes made by John and approve it

[L17] 

[L18] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L19] 

[L20] First go to [Account Member page](https://beta.optisigns.com/app/s/account-members) and add John with Content Proposal role and Jane with Content Approval role.

[L21] 

[L22] By default, there 5 roles on your Pro or Enterprise account.

[L23] 

[L24] * Admin: have full access to the account, include billing.

[L25] * User: can create, edit, delete all content, screens for the folders they have access to

[L26] * Content Proposal: can create new files, assets and propose change to playlist

[L27] * Content Approval: like User, but also have authority to review and approve change proposal

[L28] * Read Only: can only view what they have access to

[L29] 

[L30] Pick the Content Proposal role for John.

[L31] 

[L32] ![mceclip2.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003459722) And Content Approval role for Jane.

[L34] Once added you will have 2 users like below.

[L36] ![mceclip0.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360102331574)

[L40] ### **Log in as John the Proposer and make some changes to a playlist**

[L41] 

[L42] You will see there's even an A|B view to compare Original vs Proposed.

[L43] 

[L44] Once change is made, click on Submit for Approval.

[L45] 

[L46] ![mceclip5.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003504041)

[L47] 

[L48] John can pick to send a notification to All Approvers, or selected approvers, in this case let's select Jane.

[L49] 

[L50] **Note:** this is only select who are to be notified, all approvers, admins still can review and approve all change proposal.

[L51] 

[L52] After proposal submitted, John can still continue to change the playlist till Jane approves it. Once Jane approves, he will need to submit a new proposal.

[L58] ### **Proposal Notification and Approval Process:**

[L59] 

[L60] If Jane has an email associate with her account, she will get an email notification like below.

[L61] 

[L62] She can click on the Review button to be taken straight to the Playlist to review.

[L63] 

[L64] ![mceclip7.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360102332874)

[L65] 

[L66] Jane will also get notification when she log on to OptiSigns.

[L67] 

[L68] Click on the notification item will take her to the playlist in that proposal.

[L69] 

[L70] ![mceclip8.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003460722)

[L71] 

[L72] Jane can review and Reject or Approve the change.

[L73] 

[L74] If **Approve** is clicked, the proposed change will be applied to the playlist right away, and if there are screens playing this playlist, the screens will be updated immediately.

[L75] 

[L76] Jane can also make more changes, modify to the Proposed Playlist before approving it.

[L77] 

[L78] If **Reject** is clicked, proposed change is discarded. Note that you will not be able to recover discarded change once rejected, Jane may want to contact John and suggest changes in that case.

[L79] 

[L80] ![mceclip9.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003504701)

[L81] 

[L82] If Jane went on vacation, came back and there are many approvals waiting for her.

[L83] 

[L84] She can go to the Approval Requests page to view them all.

[L85] 

[L86] ![mceclip10.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360102333594)

[L87] 

[L88] In the Approval Request page, Jane or any Admin, Approvers can see all the pending approval in the system (regardless of who submitted it or who it submitted to).

[L89] 

[L90] Jane can review in detail by click View Detail to go to the specific playlist.

[L91] 

[L92] Or click approve reject right there.

[L93] 

[L94] This page also useful if other Admin, Approvers need to cover for Jane to review change proposal when Janes is not available.

[L95] 

[L96] ![mceclip11.png](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/1500003505001)

[L98] ## **Summary:**

[L99] 

[L100] The idea is for the Content Proposer, contributor to be able to upload files, create new assets, and propose change to playlists.

[L101] 

[L102] Content Proposer cannot Edit Screens, Change Assets, Schedule.

[L103] 

[L104] We would like to keep the process simple for both the Proposers and Approvers. Imagine if an approver needs to look at a change in an asset that belong to a Playlist that's in a Schedule 2 weeks from now. It would not be easy for the approver to figure out what's the actual impact of the change.┬Ā
