[L24] ## Screen Tagging

[L25] 

[L26] Your screens can be tagged in much the same way as an asset. This has a number of different uses:

[L27] 

[L28] * Organizing numerous screens, by location, type of screen, etc.

[L29] * Grouping sets of screens together for quick pushes of assets or playlists

[L30] * Allowing Emergency Messages/CAP Alerts to appear on certain screens with specific tags

[L31] * Enabling Remote Commands to be able to alter the content displayed on multiple screens at once

[L32] 

[L33] You can add as many different tags as you want to a screen. Please note that these screen tags are separate from those tags you may have applied to your assets. For more information on tagging assets, see the [**Asset Tagging**](#AssetTagging) section later in this article.

[L34] 

[L35] To tag your screens, select the screens, then hit **Edit**.

[L47] ### Pushing to Multiple Screens

[L48] 

[L49] The most commonly used feature of screen tagging is the ability to push content - be that an asset, playlist, or schedule - to multiple screens at once.

[L50] 

[L51] To do this, find the piece of content you wish to push, then click the **Three Dots**:

[L52]

[L54]

[L60] 

[L61] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38062664604691)

[L64] 

[L65] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38062653695891)

[L71] With Tag Rules, it's possible to set a network of Tags for an Asset to display on.

[L112] ## Asset Tagging

[L113] 

[L114] Tagging assets can be extremely helpful when you have many screens across many locations and when certain content does not apply to all screens.

[L115] 

[L116] For example, letŌĆÖs say you want to advertise certain special deals in one location, but not another. This asset can be tagged with a simple ŌĆ£locationŌĆØ marker, which will signal where it should be displayed. You can apply as many tags as you like to a single asset.

[L117] 

[L118] Tags may only be applied to existing assets in OptiSigns. To learn how to create an asset, see our article on [**How to Upload and Manage Files and Assets**](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/en-us/articles/360016247974-How-to-Upload-Manage-Your-Files-Assets)**.**

[L119] 

[L120] Once there is an asset to tag, from within the **Files/Assets** tab on the OptiSigns portal, there are two methods to add/edit tags:

[L121] 

[L122] 1. Single asset

[L123] 2. Multiple (bulk) assets

[L124] 

[L125] ### Single Asset Tagging

[L137] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/38062664624019)
