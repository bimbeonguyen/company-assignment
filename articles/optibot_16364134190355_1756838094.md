[L1] # How to use Streaming App with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] Putting a Streaming video on your digital signs screens is easy with OptiSigns.  

[L4]   

[L5] 

[L6] ## **Let's jump in and get started:**

[L7] 

[L8] First, you will need to have your screens set up and paired. For more information on how to do that, click [here](https://www.optisigns.com/blog/how-to-set-up-digital-signs-with-optisigns-and-amazon-fire-tv).

[L9] 

[L10] Then log on to our portal: <http://app.optisigns.com/>

[L11] 

[L12] Go to Files/Assets, Click on "App". And click the Streaming App.

[L13] 

[L14] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/16364071877779)

[L15] 

[L16] Enter your Streaming App information:

[L17] 

[L18] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/16364077889299)

[L19] 

[L20] * Name: Give your Streaming App a name. This is for displaying in your asset list only.

[L21] * URL: Put your streaming URL here.

[L22] * Render Engine: Select the Normal, CrossWalk, or Advanced Website on your type of device.  

[L23] 

[L24] 	+ Require gesture: Set webview for website with video to require touch to play

[L25] 	+ Simulate touch after load: Set webview for website video require touch to play (If you experiencing video auto play issue with your website, try combination of this setting and Required user gesture)

[L26] 

[L27] Click Save.  

[L28] After Saving, you can Preview the video to verify.

[L29]  

[L30] ## **That's all! Congratulation!**

[L31] 

[L32] You have added a Streaming App.

[L33] 

[L34] You can assign the newly created Streaming App asset to your screen by going to Screens, click Edit screens and assign the wall to screens that you want.

[L35] 

[L36] You can put the Streaming App to a Playlist, Schedule too.

[L37] 

[L38]  

[L39]
