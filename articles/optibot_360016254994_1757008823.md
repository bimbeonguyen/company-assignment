[L1] # How to add & use Vimeo video with OptiSigns

[L2] 

[L3] We support Vimeo out of the box.

[L4] 

[L5] You just need to log on, add Vimeo App:

[L6] 

[L7] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360023836854)

[L8] 

[L9] Then go to Vimeo, find the video you like to copy the link

[L10] 

[L11] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360024795633)

[L12] 

[L13] Then Paste the link to OptiSigns' Vimeo app.  

[L14] You don't have to enter the video name, the app will automatically get the official name of the video from Vimeo.

[L15] 

[L16] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360023836954)

[L17] 

[L18] You then can use this Vimeo video on your playlist or assign directly to screen if you'd like.

[L19] 

[L20] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/360024795773)

[L21] 

[L22]  

[L23] 

[L24] **Note**: If you want to set your Vimeo as not public, you can set your Viemo as "**Hide from Vimeo".** Then you can add "[social-player.optisigns.com](http://social-player.optisigns.com/)" in the Specific domains of Embed.

[L25] 

[L26] ![](https://support.optisigns.com/hc/article_attachments/15320186326547)

[L27]
